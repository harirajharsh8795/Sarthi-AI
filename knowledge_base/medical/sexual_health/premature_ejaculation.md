# Premature Ejaculation (Premature Ejaculation/Sheeghrapatan) ki Pehchan, Kaaran aur Upachar (Behavioral Techniques and SSRI Therapy Guide)

## Overview

### English
Premature Ejaculation (PE), clinically termed rapid ejaculation (popularly known as **Sheeghrapatan** in India), is defined by three core criteria:
1. Ejaculation that always or nearly always occurs prior to or within **1 minute** of vaginal penetration (**Intravaginal Ejaculatory Latency Time - IELT**).
2. The inability to delay ejaculation on all or nearly all vaginal penetrations.
3. Negative personal consequences, such as distress, frustration, or the avoidance of sexual intimacy.
Pathophysiologically, PE is linked to hypersensitivity of penile sensory receptors or low serotonin levels in the brain's ejaculatory reflex pathway. Treatment combines behavioral therapy (**Stop-Start** and **Squeeze** techniques), topical anesthetics (Lidocaine), and fast-acting SSRIs (Dapoxetine).

## Definition
* **IELT (Intravaginal Ejaculatory Latency Time):** The time from the start of vaginal penetration to the onset of intravaginal ejaculation.
* **Stop-Start Technique:** A behavioral exercise where the man stops stimulation just before reaching the "point of ejaculatory inevitability" (climax) until the urge subsides, then resumes.
* **Dapoxetine:** A short-acting selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI) taken on-demand 1-3 hours before intercourse, specifically approved to treat PE.

## Ejaculatory Dysfunction: Clinical Comparison

It is vital to distinguish premature ejaculation from normal variation and erectile dysfunction:

| Feature | Premature Ejaculation (PE) | Normal Ejaculatory Variant | Erectile Dysfunction (ED) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Penetration Time (IELT)**| **$< 1\text{ Minute}$** | $3 - 8\text{ Minutes}$ | Variable, erection lost before ejaculation |
| **Ejaculation Control** | Completely absent | Controlled in most situations | Erection fails, no penetration possible |
| **Neurological Cause** | Penile hypersensitivity / Low Serotonin | Normal variation | Blood vessel blockage / Adrenaline rush |
| **Surgical/Local Cream**| Lidocaine sprays reduce sensitivity | Not required | PDE5 Inhibitors (Viagra) raise blood flow |
| **First-Line Drug** | **Dapoxetine** (On-Demand SSRI) | Counseling / Reassurance | **Sildenafil / Tadalafil** |

## Symptoms of Premature Ejaculation

### English
* **Primary:** Ejaculation occurring consistently within 30-60 seconds of penetration or even during foreplay; complete lack of voluntary control over ejaculation.
* **Secondary Psychological:** Frustration and loss of self-confidence; avoidance of sexual situations due to fear of failure; relationship strain and distress in both partners.

* **मुख्य लक्षण:** प्रवेश के ३०-६० सेकंड के भीतर या उससे पहले ही वीर्य का स्खलन हो जाना; स्खलन पर कोई नियंत्रण न रख पाना।
* **मानसिक लक्षण:** निराशा, हीन भावना, असफलता के डर से संभोग से बचना।

### Hinglish
* **Main Symptoms:** Penetration ke 30-60 seconds ke andar ya touch karte hi virya ka nikal jana; ejaculation ko chah kar bhi delay (rok) na paana.
* **Mental Symptoms:** Frustration aur self-confidence ka achanak kam ho jana; partner ke samne embarrassment aur depression feel hona.

## Behavioral First Aid & Safety Protocols

If experiencing PE, execute these behavioral rescue protocols:

### Step 1: The Stop-Start Technique (Behavioral Method)

#### English
* This trains the brain to recognize the "point of no return" (ejaculatory inevitability).
  1. Stimulate the penis until you feel you are about to ejaculate.
  2. Stop all stimulation completely. Let the erection partially subside.
  3. Wait 30-60 seconds, then restart stimulation.
  4. *Repeat this cycle 3-4 times before allowing ejaculation. Doing this daily for 6-8 weeks increases IELT significantly.*

#### Hindi
* **स्टॉप-स्टार्ट तकनीक:** उत्तेजना को स्खलन से ठीक पहले पूरी तरह रोक दें। ३०-६० सेकंड रुकें जब तक कि इच्छा कम न हो जाए, फिर शुरू करें। इसे संभोग के दौरान ३-४ बार दोहराएं।

#### Hinglish
* **Stop-Start Technique:** Sex ya masturbation ke dauran jab aapko lage ki semen nikalne hi wala hai (point of no return), tabhi touch karna aur movement rokk dein. Erection ko thoda kam hone dein. **30-60 seconds wait karein**, jab urge kam ho jaye to fir se start karein. Ise 3-4 bar repeat karein. Isse brain ka control center strong hota hai.

### Step 2: The Squeeze Technique

#### English
* Just before ejaculation is about to happen, have the partner gently squeeze the ring of the penis (at the corona/head interface) for 15-20 seconds. This physically reduces erection pressure and delays ejaculation.

#### Hindi
* **स्क्वीज़ तकनीक:** स्खलन से ठीक पहले, लिंग के सिरे (corona) को हल्के से १५-२० सेकंड के लिए दबाएं ताकि दबाव कम हो सके।

#### Hinglish
* **Squeeze Technique:** Ejaculation aane se just pehle, partner se boliye ki wo penis ki glans (tip) ke base ko gently 15-20 seconds ke liye squeeze (dabayein). Isse erection tension down ho jayegi aur discharge delayed ho jayega.

### Safety Warnings (Don'ts)

#### English
* **Do NOT use local anesthetic creams (Lidocaine) incorrectly.** Apply a very thin layer only to the glans penis 10-15 minutes before sex. **You must wash the cream off completely before intercourse.** *If you do not wash it off, the cream will transfer to the vagina, numbing your partner's vagina and ruining their sexual sensation.*
* **Do NOT use daily SSRIs without urological checkup.**
* **Do NOT buy local "virya rokne wali" capsules.**

#### Hindi
* **सुन्न करने वाली क्रीम (Lidocaine) का सही उपयोग करें:** संभोग से पहले इसे धो लें, अन्यथा महिला साथी का अंग भी सुन्न हो जाएगा।
* **देसी नीम-हकीमों की दवाओं से बचें।**

#### Hinglish
* **Lidocaine cream lagakar direct sex na karein:** Penile sensitivity kam karne ke liye Lidocaine cream glans (head) par 10-15 mins pehle lagayein. Lekin **intercourse se pehle ise complete pani se dho lein**. Agar aap nahi dhoyenge to ye partner ki vagina ko bhi numbing (sunn) kar degi, jisse unhe sex me kuch feel nahi hoga.
* **Fake herbal tablets se bachein.**

## Diagnostic Evaluation
* **Clinical Sexual History & IELT Measurement:** The doctor uses a stopwatch check (historically) or patient estimate to confirm penetration time $< 60\text{ seconds}$.
* **Thyroid Profile (TFT):** Mandatory because **hyperthyroidism** (overactive thyroid) is a major hormonal cause of premature ejaculation.
* **Urine Routine & Prostatic Fluid Culture:** Checked to rule out prostatitis (prostate infection), which causes sudden PE.

---

## Medical & Pharmacological Management
* **Dapoxetine Therapy:** A rapid-onset, short-acting SSRI. Taken **on-demand** (30mg or 60mg) 1 to 3 hours before sexual activity. It rises serotonin rapidly to delay ejaculation and is cleared from the body in 24 hours, avoiding systemic side effects.
* **Topical Local Anesthetics:** Lidocaine/Prilocaine spray or creams applied locally.
* **Daily SSRIs (Off-label):** e.g., Paroxetine or Fluoxetine, which delay ejaculation as a side effect.

## Prevention of Premature Ejaculation
* **Use Double Condoms:** Thick latex condoms physically reduce friction and sensitivity, extending intercourse time naturally.
* **Treat Hyperthyroidism:** Restoring TSH levels can cure hormonal PE.
* **Stress Management:** Focus on intimacy rather than penetration time.

## Possible Complications of Untreated PE
* **Severe Relationship Discord & Frustration.**
* **Secondary Erectile Dysfunction:** Caused by performance anxiety and the constant fear of rapid release.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **What is premature ejaculation (PE)?**
   PE is when a man ejaculates sooner during sexual intercourse than he or his partner would like, typically within 1 minute of vaginal penetration.

2. **What is IELT?**
   IELT stands for Intravaginal Ejaculatory Latency Time. It is the time between vaginal penetration and ejaculation. An IELT of less than 60 seconds is diagnosed as PE.

3. **How does Dapoxetine treat PE?**
   Dapoxetine is a fast-acting SSRI. It increases serotonin levels in the brain's ejaculatory pathway, which delays the ejaculation reflex on-demand.

4. **What is the "Stop-Start" technique?**
   It is a behavioral exercise: stimulate the penis until you feel close to ejaculation, stop all touch for 30-60 seconds until the urge passes, and repeat this cycle 3-4 times.

5. **Why should I wash off Lidocaine cream before sex?**
   Lidocaine is a local anesthetic that numbs the skin. If not washed off completely, it will transfer to your partner's vagina and numb it, destroying their sensation.

6. **Can hyperthyroidism cause premature ejaculation?**
   Yes. An overactive thyroid gland (hyperthyroidism) speeds up nervous system signaling, which can trigger rapid, uncontrolled ejaculation.

7. **Where can I get cheap generic PE medicines in India?**
   Generic Dapoxetine (30mg) and Lidocaine sprays are available at PM Jan Aushadhi Kendras at a very low cost.

8. **How do thick condoms help with sheeghrapatan?**
   Thicker latex condoms act as a physical barrier that reduces the sensitivity of the penile skin, helping delay ejaculation naturally.

9. **What is the "Squeeze" technique?**
   When close to ejaculation, the partner gently squeezes the base of the penile glans (head) for 15-20 seconds. This lowers erection pressure and delays release.

10. **Is premature ejaculation linked to prostate infections?**
    Yes. Chronic prostatitis (prostate inflammation) increases nerve excitability in the pelvis, often causing sudden-onset premature ejaculation.

11. **What is the difference between PE and ED?**
    PE is the inability to control the timing of ejaculation. ED is the inability to achieve or maintain a firm erection.

12. **Is Dapoxetine an everyday antidepressant?**
    No. Unlike other SSRIs, Dapoxetine is short-acting and taken only "on-demand" (1-3 hours before sex). It does not need to be taken daily.

13. **What is the price of generic Dapoxetine (30mg) at Jan Aushadhi Kendras?**
    Generic Dapoxetine (30mg) is available at PM Jan Aushadhi Kendras for only ₹20 to ₹35 per strip of 10 tablets.

14. **Can performance anxiety cause sheeghrapatan?**
    Yes. Performance anxiety releases adrenaline, which triggers the fight-or-flight response, forcing the body to ejaculate quickly.

15. **Does the Ayushman Bharat card cover psychosexual therapy?**
    Yes, the PM-JAY scheme covers cashless outpatient psychiatric consultations, diagnostic thyroid panels, and counseling packages.

16. **Why should I avoid using local baba remedies for PE?**
    Unregulated street remedies often contain dangerous heavy metals (like lead) or strong sedatives that damage the kidneys and cause permanent impotence.

17. **Can Kegel exercises help PE?**
    Yes. Strengthening the pelvic floor muscles (pubococcygeus) helps men gain better voluntary control over the ejaculatory reflex.

18. **What are the side effects of Dapoxetine?**
    Common side effects include mild nausea, headache, dizziness, and a dry mouth. It can rarely cause a sudden drop in blood pressure upon standing (orthostatic syncope).

19. **How is PE diagnosed clinically?**
    Diagnosis is based on a detailed sexual history, estimated or measured IELT under 60 seconds, and ruling out thyroid issues or prostate infections.

## Common Myths vs Facts

* **Myth:** Masturbation during childhood is the primary cause of premature ejaculation in adults.
  * **Fact:** Masturbation is a normal behavior and does not cause PE. However, masturbating very quickly in secret can train the brain to ejaculate rapidly.
* **Myth:** Sucking on a key delays ejaculation instantly during intercourse.
  * **Fact:** Sucking keys is a useless superstition. Ejaculation is controlled by serotonin and sensory nerves, managed by stop-start exercises or Dapoxetine.
* **Myth:** Drinking warm buffalo milk before sex cures sheeghrapatan permanently.
  * **Fact:** Milk provides nutrition but has no chemical effect on the brain's ejaculatory reflex or serotonin levels.
* **Myth:** A real man should be able to last for 1 hour during vaginal intercourse.
  * **Fact:** The average medical IELT (time from penetration to release) for healthy men is only **3 to 7 minutes**. The 1-hour claim is a pornography myth.
* **Myth:** Premature ejaculation means the man has weak sperm and cannot have children.
  * **Fact:** PE has zero link to sperm count or quality. Unless ejaculation happens before vaginal penetration, fertility is completely normal.
* **Myth:** Daily use of Viagra cures sheeghrapatan.
  * **Fact:** Viagra only helps with erections; it does not delay ejaculation. Only Dapoxetine or behavioral techniques delay ejaculation.
* **Myth:** Applying mustard oil (sarso tel) on the glans makes the skin thick and cures PE.
  * **Fact:** Mustard oil can cause skin burns and severe chemical dermatitis. Use safe Lidocaine sprays under advice.
* **Myth:** Generic Dapoxetine from Jan Aushadhi Kendras is weaker than branded versions.
  * **Fact:** Generic Dapoxetine contains the exact same active drug and works identically to delay ejaculation, at a fraction of the cost.
* **Myth:** Taking 3 tablets of Dapoxetine together will make you last all night.
  * **Fact:** Taking an overdose of Dapoxetine causes severe vomiting, dangerous drops in blood pressure, fainting, and neurological issues. Take only the prescribed dose.
* **Myth:** PE is always a permanent condition that can never be cured.
  * **Fact:** With a combination of behavioral training (stop-start), pelvic floor exercises, and on-demand medication, PE can be successfully treated.
