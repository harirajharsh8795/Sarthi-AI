# Infertility (Infertility/Banjhpan) ki Pehchan, Kaaran aur Upachar (Semen Analysis, Ovulation and IVF Guide)

## Overview

### English
Infertility is a disease of the male or female reproductive system defined by the failure to achieve a pregnancy after **12 months or more of regular, unprotected sexual intercourse** (or **6 months** for women aged $>35$). Pathophysiologically, infertility is a shared clinical condition:
* **Male Factors (30-40%):** Primarily low sperm count (oligozoospermia), poor sperm motility (asthenozoospermia), or abnormal sperm shape (teratozoospermia).
* **Female Factors (30-40%):** Primarily ovulatory disorders (PCOS, thyroid issues), fallopian tube blockage (post-TB pelvic inflammatory disease in India), or endometriosis.
* **Unexplained (10-20%):** No clear cause found. Diagnosis requires semen analysis, HSG tube check, and hormone profiles. Treatment ranges from ovulation induction to IVF.

### Hindi
बांझपन (Infertility / बांझपन या निःसंतानता), पुरुष या महिला की प्रजनन प्रणाली की एक बीमारी है, जिसे **12 महीने या उससे अधिक समय तक नियमित, असुरक्षित यौन संबंध** के बाद भी गर्भधारण (pregnancy) न हो पाने के रूप में परिभाषित किया जाता है (35 वर्ष से अधिक उम्र की महिलाओं के लिए 6 महीने)। यह दोनों साथियों में से किसी के कारण हो सकता है: **पुरुष बांझपन** (शुक्राणुओं की संख्या या गतिशीलता में कमी), **महिला बांझपन** (अंडोत्सर्ग [ovulation] न होना, या डिंबवाही नलियों [fallopian tubes] में रुकावट)। इसका इलाज दवाओं, आईयूआई (IUI) या आईवीएफ (IVF / टेस्ट ट्यूब बेबी) तकनीक से किया जाता है।

## Quick Summary
Infertility is the failure to conceive after 12 months. Causes feature sperm abnormalities, anovulation, and tubal blockages. Diagnostics check semen parameters, HSG dye scans, and FSH/LH/TSH levels. Management uses ovulation induction, IUI, and IVF.

## Definition
* **Oligozoospermia:** A low concentration of sperm in semen, defined by the WHO as less than **15 million sperm per mL**.
* **HSG (Hysterosalpingography):** A specialized X-ray scan using a radiographic dye injected into the uterus to check if the fallopian tubes are open or blocked.
* **IVF (In Vitro Fertilization):** An assisted reproductive technology (ART) where eggs are surgically removed, fertilized by sperm in a lab dish, and the embryo is implanted back into the uterus.

## Diagnostic Checkup Protocol: Male & Female Evaluations

To find the cause of infertility, both partners must be tested together:

| Partner | Diagnostic Test | Normal Clinical Value | Purpose of Test |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Male** | **Semen Analysis** | Volume $> 1.5\text{ mL}$, Count $> 15\text{ Million/mL}$, Motility $> 40\%$ | Checks sperm count, active movement, and normal shape |
| **Female** | **AMH (Anti-Müllerian Hormone)**| $1.5 - 4.0\text{ ng/mL}$ | Measures remaining ovarian egg reserve (egg count) |
| **Female** | **HSG Dye Test** | Free spill of dye into the pelvis | Checks if fallopian tubes are open for sperm-egg meeting |
| **Female** | **Day 2 FSH / LH** | Ratio approx $1:1$, both $< 10\text{ mIU/mL}$ | Evaluates ovarian reserve and checks for PCOS ($LH:FSH > 2:1$) |
| **Female** | **TSH & Prolactin** | TSH $< 2.5\text{ uIU/mL}$, Prolactin $< 25\text{ ng/mL}$ | Rules out thyroid or prolactin excess causing ovulation block |

## Symptoms & Warning Signs

### English
* **Primary:** The inability to conceive after 1 year of unprotected intercourse.
* **Secondary Female Signs:** Highly irregular, absent, or extremely painful periods; abnormal facial hair growth and acne (PCOS signs); severe pelvic pain (endometriosis warning).
* **Secondary Male Signs:** Difficulty with erection or ejaculation (ED); pain or swelling in the testicles (varicocele); history of childhood mumps.

### Hindi
* **महिला लक्षण:** मासिक धर्म का अत्यधिक अनियमित या बंद होना; चेहरे पर अनचाहे बाल आना (PCOS); पेडू (pelvis) में तेज दर्द।
* **पुरुष लक्षण:** वीर्य में शुक्राणुओं की कमी; अंडकोश में सूजन या दर्द (varicocele)।

### Hinglish
* **Female Warning Signs:** Periods ka severely irregular hona ya ruk jana; face par extra hair growth (PCOS); lower abdomen me tez dard (endometriosis).
* **Male Warning Signs:** Erection ya ejaculation me problem (ED); testicles (andkosh) me sujan ya naso ka phoolna (varicocele); bachpan me mumps (galsua) fever hona.

## Safety & Support Guidelines for Couples

### Do's (What to do)

#### English
* **Seek clinical evaluation for both partners simultaneously.** In 40% of cases, the male factor is the sole cause. Testing only the female partner delays diagnosis.
* **Track the Fertile Window.** The highest chance of pregnancy is during ovulation (typically **days 11 to 16** of a 28-day cycle). Have intercourse every alternate day during this window.
* **Manage BMI and weight.** A BMI of $18.5 - 22.9\text{ kg/m}^2$ optimizes ovulation and sperm quality.
* **Quit smoking and limit alcohol.** Both destroy sperm DNA and egg quality.

#### Hindi
* **दोनों साथियों की जांच एक साथ कराएं:** ४०% मामलों में कारण पुरुष बांझपन होता है।
* **फर्टाइल विंडो (Fertile Window) ट्रैक करें:** मासिक धर्म के ११वें से १६वें दिन के बीच संबंध बनाएं।
* **वजन संतुलित रखें।**

#### Hinglish
* **Husband-Wife dono ki jaanch ek sath karwayein:** 40% cases me baanjhpan ka kaaran male factor (sperm issues) hota hai. Sirf female ki checkup karwana time waste karta hai.
* **Fertile Window track karein:** Ovulation time (periods shuru hone ke **11th day se 16th day** ke beech) ko track karein. Is window me alternate days (1 din chodd kar) physical relations banana pregnancy ke chances kafi badhata hai.
* **Weight control rakhein:** High weight ovulation ko block karta hai. Rojana exercise karein.
* **Nasha completely band karein:** Cigarette aur alcohol sperm DNA ko damage karte hain.

### Don'ts (What NOT to do)

#### English
* **Do NOT use oil-based lubricants or saliva** during intercourse when trying to conceive. Most lubricants and saliva are highly toxic to sperm, blocking their movement. Only use sperm-friendly lubricants.
* **Do NOT take hot tubs or saunas** (for men). High heat destroys sperm production in the testicles. Keep scrotal temperature cool.
* **Do NOT ignore pelvic tuberculosis.** In India, genital TB is a leading cause of permanent fallopian tube damage, requiring early ATT antibiotics.
* **Do NOT undergo unprescribed hormonal therapies.**

#### Hindi
* **संभोग के दौरान तेल या थूक का उपयोग न करें:** ये शुक्राणुओं (sperm) को मार देते हैं।
* **पुरुष गर्म पानी के टब (hot tubs) में न बैठें:** अत्यधिक गर्मी से शुक्राणु मर जाते हैं।

#### Hinglish
* **Sex ke dauran oil ya thuk (saliva) na lagayein:** Coconut oil, vaseline ya saliva sperms ke liye highly toxic hote hain aur unhe kill kar dete hain. Erection smoother karne ke liye sirf sperm-friendly lube hi use karein.
* **Men hot water bath/sauna avoid karein:** Testicles me extra heat padne se sperms dead ho jate hain. Tight underwear pehnna avoid karein.
* **Genital TB ko ignore na karein:** India me aanto ya uterus ki TB fallopian tubes ko block kar deti hai. Iska timely ATT course karwayein.

## Medical & Assisted Reproductive Technologies (ART)

### Ovulation Induction & IUI

#### English
* **Ovulation Induction:** Giving oral tablets (Clomiphene Citrate or Letrozole) to help ovaries produce and release healthy eggs.
* **IUI (Intrauterine Insemination):** A procedure where the husband's semen is washed in a lab to concentrate active sperms, which are then injected directly into the female's uterus during ovulation using a catheter.

#### Hindi
* **ओव्यूलेशन इंडक्शन:** अंडे बनने की दवाएं (Letrozole) देना।
* **आईयूआई (IUI):** प्रयोगशाला में धुले हुए सक्रिय शुक्राणुओं को सीधे गर्भाशय में इंजेक्ट करना।

#### Hinglish
* Egg banana start karne ke liye gynecologist **Letrozole** ya Clomiphene tablets dete hain.
* **IUI Procedure:** Husband ke semen ko lab me wash karke active sperms ko catheter ke jariye direct female ke uterus (bachhadani) me inject kiya jata hai, taaki sperm-egg meeting easy ho sake.

### IVF (In Vitro Fertilization / Test Tube Baby)

#### English
* If tubes are blocked or sperm count is extremely low, IVF is used. Eggs are retrieved from the ovaries, fertilized with sperm in a petri dish (or via ICSI - injecting a single sperm directly into an egg), and the resulting embryo is placed back in the uterus.

#### Hindi
* आईवीएफ (IVF / टेस्ट ट्यूब बेबी), जिसका उपयोग बंद ट्यूबों या गंभीर रूप से कम शुक्राणुओं में किया जाता है।

#### Hinglish
* **IVF (Test Tube Baby):** Jab tubes block hon ya sperm count kafi low ho, to IVF kiya jata hai. Female ke eggs aur male ke sperms ko body se bahar nikal kar lab petri-dish me fertilize kiya jata hai, aur embryo (bhrun) ko bachhadani me fit kiya jata hai.

## Medicines
*Saarthi AI does not prescribe medicines. The following describes clinical drug categories related to infertility:*
1. **Selective Estrogen Receptor Modulators (SERMs):** Induce ovulation (e.g., Clomiphene Citrate).
2. **Aromatase Inhibitors:** First-line for PCOS ovulation (e.g., Letrozole).
3. **Gonadotropins:** Hormone injections to mature eggs (e.g., FSH, LH, hCG injections).
4. **Insulin Sensitizers:** Improve fertility in PCOS (e.g., Metformin).

## Prevention of Infertility
* **Screen for STIs:** Untreated Chlamydia or Gonorrhea causes Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID), scarring the fallopian tubes.
* **Protect Testicular Temperature:** Avoid keeping laptops directly on the lap.
* **Maintain Active Lifestyle:** Avoid sedentary work to prevent obesity-related anovulation.

---

## Possible Complications of Infertility Treatments
* **OHSS (Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome):** A life-threatening complication of IVF hormone injections, causing fluid leakage into the lungs/abdomen.
* **Multiple Gestations:** Increased risk of twins, triplets, or high-risk pregnancies.

---

## Emergency Warning Signs
Go to the nearest emergency department immediately or call 108 if you observe:
* **A female undergoing IVF hormone injections develops sudden severe abdominal swelling, breathing difficulty, and rapid weight gain (OHSS emergency).**
* **Sudden severe, sharp, one-sided lower abdominal pain in early pregnancy.**

---

## Government Health Programs & Laws (India)
* **Assisted Reproductive Technology (Regulation) Act, 2021:** Regulates IVF clinics in India, setting age limits (up to 50 for females, 55 for males), banning commercial surrogacy, and enforcing safety standards.
* **National Programme for Prevention of Tuberculosis (NTEP):** Provides free diagnostics and free ATT medicines for genital TB to prevent tubal infertility in women.
* **Ayushman Bharat (AB-PMJAY):** Covers cashless packages for diagnostic hysteroscopy, tubal blockage surgeries, and gynecological ICU admissions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **What is infertility?**
   Infertility is defined as the inability to conceive after 12 months or more of regular, unprotected sexual intercourse.

2. **Why should both partners be tested for infertility?**
   Infertility is a shared condition. In about $40\%$ of cases, the cause is male factor (sperm issues), and in $40\%$ it is female factor. Testing both saves time.

3. **What is a normal sperm count?**
   According to WHO standards, a normal semen analysis shows a sperm concentration of at least **15 million sperm per mL** of semen, with over 40% active motility.

4. **What is Hysterosalpingography (HSG)?**
   An HSG is an X-ray test that uses a special dye injected into the uterus to check if the fallopian tubes are open and normal.

5. **How does Clomiphene or Letrozole help in infertility?**
   These are oral medications that stimulate the brain to release FSH, helping the ovaries grow and release a mature egg (ovulation).

6. **What is the difference between IUI and IVF?**
   IUI involves injecting washed sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation. IVF involves retrieving eggs and sperm, fertilizing them in a lab dish, and transferring the embryo.

7. **Where can I get free treatment for genital TB in India?**
   Free diagnosis, chest X-rays, and complete ATT drug courses are provided free of cost at all government DOTS centers under the NTEP program.

8. **Can PCOS cause infertility?**
   Yes. Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) causes hormonal imbalances that prevent ovaries from releasing eggs regularly, leading to anovulatory infertility.

9. **Why is using saliva as a lubricant bad when trying to conceive?**
   Saliva contains digestive enzymes and bacteria that alter vaginal pH and are toxic to sperm, killing them or blocking their movement.

10. **What is the "Fertile Window"?**
    It is the 5-6 day period in a woman's menstrual cycle (usually days 11 to 16) when having sex has the highest chance of resulting in pregnancy.

11. **What is AMH and what does it test?**
    Anti-Müllerian Hormone (AMH) is a blood test that measures a woman's ovarian reserve, showing the quantity of remaining eggs.

12. **Why does heat damage male fertility?**
    Testicles are located outside the body because sperm production requires a temperature $2^\circ\text{C}$ lower than body temperature. High heat (hot baths, laptops on lap) kills sperm.

13. **What is Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome (OHSS)?**
    OHSS is a dangerous complication of IVF hormone injections where ovaries swell, leaking fluid into the abdomen and lungs, causing breathing distress.

14. **What is the price of generic Letrozole at Jan Aushadhi Kendras?**
    Generic Letrozole (2.5mg) tablets are available at PM Jan Aushadhi Kendras for only ₹30 to ₹40 per strip, compared to ₹150-₹220 in private pharmacies.

15. **Does the Ayushman Bharat card cover IVF treatments?**
    The PM-JAY scheme covers cashless packages for diagnostic hysteroscopy, laparoscopy, and tubal block surgeries, but does not cover IVF/IUI cycles.

16. **How does pelvic TB cause infertility?**
    The tuberculosis bacteria infect the fallopian tubes, causing severe inflammation and scarring, which leads to permanent tube blockage.

17. **What is ICSI?**
    Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) is an advanced IVF procedure where a single selected sperm is injected directly into a retrieved egg using a micro-needle.

18. **Can thyroid disorders cause infertility?**
    Yes. Both hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism disrupt menstrual cycles and block normal egg release, preventing conception.

19. **What is the age limit for IVF in India?**
    Under the ART Act 2021, the legal age limit for undergoing assisted reproduction is up to 50 years for women and up to 55 years for men.

20. **Is generic Metformin useful for fertility?**
    Yes. In women with PCOS and insulin resistance, generic Metformin lowers insulin levels, which helps restore regular ovulation and periods.

## Common Myths vs Facts

* **Myth:** Infertility is always the woman's fault.
  * **Fact:** Infertility is equally shared. Male factors (low count, poor motility) account for about 40% of all infertility cases. Both must be tested.
* **Myth:** If you already have one child, you cannot suffer from infertility.
  * **Fact:** This is called **Secondary Infertility**. It is very common for couples to struggle to conceive a second child due to age or new health issues.
* **Myth:** Sucking on a key increases sperm count or cures blocked tubes.
  * **Fact:** Sucking keys is a useless superstition. Blocked tubes require surgery or IVF, and sperm count requires lifestyle changes and medicine.
* **Myth:** Cough syrups cure infertility by thinning vaginal mucus.
  * **Fact:** There is no scientific evidence that cough syrups cure infertility. Unregulated drug intake can harm fetal development.
* **Myth:** Men produce the same quality of sperm throughout their entire lives.
  * **Fact:** While men produce sperm lifelong, sperm quality, DNA integrity, and motility decline significantly after the age of 40-45.
* **Myth:** IVF babies (test-tube babies) are born with birth defects or are not genetically yours.
  * **Fact:** IVF babies are completely normal and healthy. They are created using the couple's own eggs and sperm, making them 100% genetically theirs.
* **Myth:** Drinking beer increases sperm count because it has yeast.
  * **Fact:** Alcohol is a direct toxin to the testicles. Heavy drinking damages sperm shape, reduces motility, and causes erectile dysfunction.
* **Myth:** Generic Letrozole or Clomiphene from Jan Aushadhi clinics is less effective in growing eggs.
  * **Fact:** Generic fertility drugs contain the exact same active molecules and are equally effective, but are 80% cheaper.
* **Myth:** You must have sex daily to conceive during the fertile window.
  * **Fact:** Having sex every alternate day (every 48 hours) is sufficient. It allows sperm count and volume to rebuild, maintaining semen quality.
* **Myth:** Fallopian tube blockage can be cleared by massage or herbal oils.
  * **Fact:** Blocked tubes are physically closed by scar tissue. They cannot be opened by external massage. They require laparoscopy or bypass via IVF.
