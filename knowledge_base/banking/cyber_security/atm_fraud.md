# ATM Fraud (एटीएम धोखाधड़ी)

## Overview

### English
ATM fraud is a criminal act where fraudsters steal cash or card details from unsuspecting users at Automated Teller Machines (ATMs). This is achieved through physical tampering (skimmers, card trapping), PIN theft (shoulder surfing, hidden cameras), or social engineering scams near the ATM cabin.

### Hindi

## Quick Summary
ATMs are highly targeted by cybercriminals for cash and card data theft. The most common physical attack is "ATM Skimming," where criminals attach a duplicate card reader card slot over the actual machine slot to copy the magnetic strip details. They pair this with a tiny pinhole camera hidden near the screen or keyboard to capture the 4-digit PIN as the customer types it.

Another method is "Card Trapping" or "Cash Trapping," where physical obstacles are placed inside the card slot or cash dispenser. The card or cash gets stuck, and a fraudster posing as a helpful bystander tricks the customer into leaving to get bank help, following which the fraudster retrieves the trapped card/cash. RBI mandates strict guidelines for ATM cabin safety, including security guards, single-card insert locks, and CCTV cameras.

---

## Definition
ATM Fraud is any unauthorized physical or digital compromise of an Automated Teller Machine or its users, resulting in the theft of payment card credentials (chip/magnetic data), PINs, or physical currency.

---

## Why It Matters
Being alert to ATM fraud is critical because:
- **Direct Cash Drain:** Victims suffer immediate, physical cash loss from their savings accounts.
- **Vulnerability of Rural Citizens:** Many senior citizens and rural residents who are not tech-savvy are targeted by fraudsters posing as helpful bank representatives near ATMs.
- **Security Compliance:** Knowing how to check for tampered ATM slots keeps your card details completely secure.

---

## How It Works
The execution cycle of a helper/card-switching ATM fraud:

Fraudster stands inside or near the ATM cabin, waiting for a target (often elderly or uneducated)
↓
Target customer enters cabin and struggles to withdraw cash or check balance
↓
Fraudster politely offers help: "Aaiye, main help kar deta hu."
↓
Fraudster takes the card, inserts it, and asks the customer to type the PIN (memorizing the PIN)
↓
Fraudster executes a distractor technique (e.g., drops money on the floor or points to a screen error)
↓
In a split second, the fraudster swaps the original card with a dummy card of the same bank
↓
Fraudster hands the dummy card back to the customer, claiming the machine is not working
↓
Customer leaves; fraudster uses the original card and memorized PIN at a nearby ATM to withdraw cash

## Features
- **ATM Skimming:** Installing external card readers to steal magnetic data.
- **PIN Capture Cameras:** Pinhole cameras hidden on the ATM canopy to record keypad entries.
- **Card/Cash Trapping:** Blocking the slot with glue or physical slides to capture the cash/card.
- **Card Swapping:** Fraudsters swapping your real card with a fake card during fake assistance.

## Risks
- **Financial Loss:** Loss of major savings if the daily ATM withdrawal limit is set high.
- **Physical Safety Risk:** Coercion or shoulder surfing by gangs operating inside ATM booths.
- **Account Blockage:** Incorrect attempts by fraudsters can block your card, disrupting your plans.

## RBI / Government Rules
- **One Customer at a Time:** Security guards must ensure only one customer enters the ATM cabin at a time.
- **Transaction Time-out Refunds:** If cash is not dispensed but debited, banks must refund the money within 5 working days of reporting. Beyond 5 days, the bank must pay the customer ₹100 per day of delay.
- **EMV Chip Mandate:** RBI mandated that all ATMs must support EMV Chip and PIN transactions (magnetic strip only transactions are disabled) to prevent skimming.

## Step-by-Step Process

### How to Check ATM Safety Before Inserting Card:
1. **Check the Card Slot:** Lightly wiggle the card insertion slot. If it feels loose, has sticky residue, or blocks the flashing green light, do not insert your card.
2. **Scan the Keyboard:** Look for a keypad overlay (a duplicate keyboard placed over the real one). Press a few keys; they should feel firm, not mushy.
3. **Inspect the Overhead Area:** Look at the ceiling panel directly above the keypad or screen. Watch out for tiny holes that could hide micro-cameras.
4. **Enforce Privacy:** Ensure no one else is inside the cabin. If someone is standing close, ask them to wait outside.
5. **Secure PIN Entry:** Cover the keypad completely with your other hand while typing the PIN.
6. **Confirm Transaction Close:** Wait until the welcome screen or "Transaction Cancelled" screen appears before leaving the ATM.

---

## Safety Tips
- **Never Accept Help from Strangers:** If the machine fails, contact bank staff or call the helpline number written inside the cabin. Do not let anyone touch your card.
- **Keep ATM PIN Private:** Do not write the PIN on the back of the card or reveal it to family members or guards.
- **Register for SMS Alerts:** Ensure your active mobile number is registered so you receive instant debit alerts for every withdrawal.
- **Set Low ATM Limits:** Use mobile banking to set low daily ATM withdrawal limits (e.g., ₹5,000) to minimize risk.

---

## Common Mistakes
- **Leaving the ATM Early:** Leaving the cabin immediately when cash is delayed, allowing fraudsters to retrieve the cash once it dispenses.
- **Allowing Multiple People in the Cabin:** Allowing other strangers to stand inside the cabin while you are executing your transaction.
- **Not Checking for Skimmers:** Inserting cards into ATMs without a quick physical check of the slot.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What is ATM fraud?** Theft of card data or physical cash from users at ATM machines.
2. **What is an ATM skimmer?** A device attached over the real card reader to copy the magnetic strip data of cards inserted.
3. **What is shoulder surfing?** Peeking over a customer's shoulder to steal their ATM PIN while they enter it.
4. **What should I do if my card gets stuck in the ATM?** Do not leave the cabin. Call the bank's toll-free customer helpline immediately to block the card.
5. **What happens if the ATM deducts money but does not dispense cash?** File a complaint with your bank. RBI rules require the bank to refund the money within 5 working days.
6. **Will I get compensation if the bank delays the cash refund?** Yes, banks must pay ₹100 per day of delay after 5 working days.
7. **Is it safe to accept help from ATM security guards?** No, guards should only manage the cabin entry. Do not share your PIN or card with the guard.
8. **How do I block my card if it is stolen at an ATM?** Use your mobile app or call the bank's toll-free helpline instantly.
9. **Can a cloned card work at an ATM without a PIN?** No, ATM transactions always require the 4-digit PIN.
10. **What is card swapping?** A scam where fraudsters switch your real card with a fake one during a fake help attempt.
11. **Why does the ATM slot flash a green light?** It indicates the slot is clean and active. If the light is blocked or invisible, it might have a skimmer overlay.
12. **Can fraudsters see my PIN using the ATM camera?** No, the official bank CCTV camera is angled to only capture your face/cabin, not the keypad. Pinhole cameras installed by thieves, however, target the keypad.
13. **Is there any charge to complain about ATM fraud?** No, it is a free service.
14. **What is the daily ATM withdrawal limit?** Depends on the card variant, usually ₹20,000 to ₹40,000 for basic cards.
15. **Can I use a zero balance card at any ATM?** Yes, BSBDA and Jan Dhan cards work at all domestic ATMs.
16. **How do I report a physical theft at an ATM?** Call the police helpline (112) or the bank security helpline immediately.
17. **What is the cybercrime toll-free number?** 1930.
18. **Can I file an ATM complaint online?** Yes, on your bank's website or mobile app.
19. **What is a cash recycler machine (CRM)?** A machine that accepts cash deposits and dispenses cash; safety rules are the same as ATMs.
20. **Should I change my PIN after using a suspicious ATM?** Yes, reset your PIN immediately using the mobile app or a secure ATM.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** Bank CCTV cameras record your PIN. **Fact:** Bank cameras are legally positioned to protect privacy and do not record the keypad keys.
2. **Myth:** Cloned cards only work for online shopping. **Fact:** Cloned cards can withdraw physical cash from ATMs if the fraudsters also captured your PIN.
3. **Myth:** If you enter your PIN backwards, the ATM dispenses cash but alerts the police. **Fact:** This is a false rumor. Entering the PIN backwards will simply result in a "Wrong PIN" error.
4. **Myth:** ATM cash trapping happens only in private ATMs. **Fact:** It can happen at any ATM if criminals physically modify the dispenser shutter.
5. **Myth:** If my cash is not dispensed, I will lose the money forever. **Fact:** Under RBI rules, banks must refund auto-debited transaction failures within 5 working days.
6. **Myth:** Skimming devices are huge and easily visible. **Fact:** Modern skimmers are extremely thin, color-matched, and fit perfectly over the card slot.
7. **Myth:** You must pay a fee to complain about ATM failures. **Fact:** Filing transaction disputes is completely free.
8. **Myth:** If the security guard is present, ATM fraud cannot happen. **Fact:** Fraudsters often distract guards or operate when guards are inactive. Stay vigilant.
9. **Myth:** Only credit cards are targetted by skimmers. **Fact:** Debit cards are targetted more often because they link directly to cash savings.
10. **Myth:** The bank will call you to ask for your ATM PIN if your card is stuck. **Fact:** No bank representative will ever ask for your PIN under any circumstances.

## Conversation Examples
1. **User:** Mera card ATM machine me phans gaya hai aur nikal nahi raha. Main kya karu?
   **Assistant:** Machine ke paas se bilkul na hatein. Kisi anjan vyakti se madad na lein. Turant bank ke customer care number par call karke card ko permanently block (hotlist) karwa dein taaki koi dusra card nikal kar use na kar sake.
2. **User:** ATM se cash nahi nikla par mere account se ₹10,000 debit ho gaye hain. Refund kab milega?
   **Assistant:** Ghabraiye nahi. Aap bank me iski written complaint darj karein. RBI rules ke tehat bank ko 5 working days ke andar paise refund karne honge. Agar 5 din se zyada delay hota hai, toh bank aapko ₹100 per day ka fine dega.
3. **User:** Kisine ATM cabin me mere piche khade hokar mera PIN dekh liya hai, mujhe kya karna chahiye?
   **Assistant:** Aap turant kisi dusre ATM me jaakar ya mobile banking app se apna PIN badal (reset) dein taaki wo aapke card ka galat istemal na kar sake.
4. **User:** Kya main guard ko apna card de sakta hu check karne ke liye ki machine chal rahi hai ya nahi?
   **Assistant:** Bilkul nahi. Bank rules ke mutabik card non-transferable hota hai. Guard ya kisi bhi teesre vyakti ko card na dein aur na hi unhe apna PIN batayein.
5. **User:** ATM machine par skimmer laga hai, mujhe kaise pata chalega?
   **Assistant:** Card dalne wale slot ko halka hila kar dekhin. Agar wo dheela hai, ya alag color ka plastic dikh raha hai, toh wahan skimmer ho sakta hai. Aise machine me card na dalein.
6. **User:** Mere savings account se ATM cash withdraw ka SMS aaya hai, par card mere paas hai.
   **Assistant:** Ye card cloning fraud ho sakta hai. Turant card block karein aur cyber crime helpline number 1930 par call karke fraud report karein.
7. **User:** Kya minor ATM card par transaction limits set ho sakti hain?
   **Assistant:** Haan, parents apne net banking ya branch se minor card ki daily withdrawal limit set kar sakte hain taaki bacha limited cash hi nikal sake.
8. **User:** ATM room me kitne logo ko ek sath jana allowed hai?
   **Assistant:** RBI safety rules ke mutabik ek waqt me sirf ek hi customer ko ATM cabin ke andar hona chahiye. Agar koi aur andar hai, toh unhe bahar rukne ko kahein.
9. **User:** Kya swipe machine par PIN reset ho sakta hai?
   **Assistant:** Nahi, PIN change ya reset sirf aapke bank ke ATM par ya mobile app/internet banking se hi kiya ja sakta hai.
10. **User:** Cash deposit machine (CRM) me paise fans jayein toh kya karein?
    **Assistant:** CRM transaction slip sambhal kar rakhein jisme transaction ID aur 'failed' status likha ho, aur branch me jaakar immediate refund request submit karein.
11. **User:** Kya bank manager phone par ATM PIN badalne me help karta hai?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, bank manager ya koi bhi bank employee phone par PIN nahi maangta aur na hi badalta hai. Ye poori tarah automated process hota hai jo aapko khud karna hota hai.
12. **User:** ATM transaction delay compensation ke liye kya koi extra form bharna padta hai?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, automatic system refund delay hone par compensation calculation karke account me credit kar deta hai. Agar nahi milta, toh principal complaint file karein.
13. **User:** Maine ATM card change karwaya hai, purana PIN chalega?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, har naye card ke liye naye sire se Green PIN generator process ke zariye naya PIN set karna padta hai.
14. **User:** Green PIN generation ke liye kya detail chahiye?
    **Assistant:** Aapko card, account number aur bank me registered mobile number chahiye, jahan security OTP aayega.
15. **User:** Kya regular ATM transactions block ho sakti hain agar CIBIL kharab ho?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, savings account aur debit card transactions ka CIBIL score se koi lena-dena nahi hota.
