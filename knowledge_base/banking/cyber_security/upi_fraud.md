# UPI Fraud (यूपीआई धोखाधड़ी / ऑनलाइन भुगतान घोटाला)

## Overview

### English
UPI fraud refers to fraudulent activities executed on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) network. Common tactics include "Collect Request" scams, fake QR code payments, and social engineering tricks where victims are misled into entering their UPI PIN under the false belief that they are receiving money.

### Hindi

## Quick Summary
UPI has revolutionized instant digital payments in India, but it has also become a major target for cybercriminals. The most common UPI scam is the "Receive Money Scam." Scammers post fake advertisements on online marketplaces (like OLX) pretending to buy furniture or electronics. They contact the seller, send a UPI "Collect Request" or a QR code, and tell the seller: "Enter your UPI PIN to receive the advance payment."

Many users do not know the basic rule of UPI: **You never need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.** The PIN is strictly used to authenticate and deduct funds from your account. Other scams include fake customer care numbers on Twitter/Google and third-party screen-sharing apps that capture UPI PINs.

---

## Definition
UPI Fraud is the unauthorized exploitation of the Unified Payments Interface ecosystem through spoofed collection requests, malicious Quick Response (QR) codes, or social engineering to coerce users into executing cryptographically signed fund debits.

---

## Why It Matters
Understanding UPI fraud is critical because:
- **Instant Money Transfer:** UPI transfers are real-time and irreversible. Once the money is sent, it cannot be recalled by the bank immediately.
- **Widespread Usage:** UPI is used daily by millions of street vendors, small businesses, and individuals, making it the most vulnerable digital payment channel.
- **Psychological Scams:** Criminals exploit user ignorance about how digital money routing works, making awareness the only defense.

---

## How It Works
The standard execution flow of a UPI Collect Request scam:

Victim posts an item for sale on an online classifieds site (e.g., OLX)
↓
Scammer calls victim, pretending to be an army officer: "I will buy it. I am sending an advance via UPI."
↓
Scammer sends a "Collect Request" of ₹10,000 on PhonePe/GPay to the victim's mobile number
↓
Victim's phone displays notification: "[Scammer Name] is requesting ₹10,000. Pay/Decline."
↓
Scammer calls: "I have sent the money. Just click on 'Pay' and enter your UPI PIN to accept it."
↓
Victim clicks 'Pay' and enters their secret 6-digit UPI PIN
↓
The UPI network processes the transaction as an authorized debit
↓
₹10,000 is immediately transferred from the victim's bank account to the scammer's wallet
↓
Victim receives debit alert and realizes they were scammed

## Eligibility
- **Targets:** All users of UPI-enabled mobile applications (BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, Mobikwik, bank-specific UPI apps).

## Required Documents
- **Not Applicable:** For reporting UPI fraud, you must provide:
  - Disputed UPI Transaction ID (starts with 3, 4, or UTR number).
  - Screenshot of the payment confirmation page showing the receiver's UPI ID (VPA).
  - Screenshot of the chat/SMS from the scammer.
  - Cyber Crime Portal complaint copy.

---

## Features
- **Collect Requests (Request Money):** Triggering push notifications demanding funds.
- **Dynamic QR Codes:** Generating QR codes that contain embedded payment instructions to deduct money.
- **VPA Spoofing:** Creating virtual payment addresses that look like official handles (e.g., hdfchelpline@upi instead of official support).

---

## Benefits of UPI Security
- **Financial Safety:** Prevents accidental fund debits during daily transactions.
- **Dispute Resolution:** Knowing how to flag transactions inside the UPI app for quick NPCI audits.

---

## Risks
- **Instant Fund Drain:** Complete loss of account balances linked to the UPI ID.
- **Unsecured Transfers:** UPI does not have credit-card like chargeback protections, making recovery dependent on freezing the beneficiary account fast.
- **Device Insecurity:** Storing UPI PINs in mobile notes apps, allowing spyware to access them.

---

## Charges & Fees
- **UPI Transactions:** Free.
- **UPI Dispute Filing:** Free.

---

## RBI / Government Rules
- **No PIN for Receiving Money:** RBI and NPCI run national campaigns stating that entering a UPI PIN is only required for sending money, never for receiving.
- **NPCI Dispute Redressal Mechanism:** NPCI mandates that UPI apps must have a built-in 'Raise Dispute' option for every transaction, allowing users to report frauds directly to the NPCI switch.
- **UPI Daily Transaction Limits:** Capped at ₹1 Lakh per day (₹5 Lakh for specific categories like hospitals/educational institutions) as per NPCI guidelines to limit risk exposure.

## Step-by-Step Process

### How to Raise a UPI Dispute on BHIM/Google Pay:
1. **Open App:** Open your UPI application and go to the 'Transaction History' or 'Activity' section.
2. **Select Transaction:** Select the fraudulent transaction you want to dispute.
3. **Click Raise Dispute:** Tap on 'Raise Dispute' or 'Report a Problem'.
4. **Choose Reason:** Select the appropriate dispute reason (e.g., "Unauthorized transaction" or "Did not initiate").
5. **Submit Details:** Submit the request. The app forwards the dispute to your issuing bank and NPCI for investigation.
6. **Report to Cyber Cell:** Call **1930** instantly to freeze the receiver's bank account.

---

## Safety Tips
- **The Golden Rule:** Never enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code if someone claims they are sending you money.
- **Decline Unknown Requests:** If you receive a pop-up collect request from an unknown UPI ID, click "Decline" immediately.
- **Verify Merchant Name:** When scanning QR codes at shops, double-check the merchant name displayed on your screen before typing your PIN.
- **Disable UPI auto-debit:** Do not authorize recurring UPI mandates unless you fully trust the service provider.

---

## Common Mistakes
- **Confusing "Pay" with "Receive":** Believing the scammer's explanation that clicking the "Pay" button is the only way to accept a digital transfer.
- **Searching Customer Care on Social Media:** Tweeting your transaction issues and calling numbers provided by fake bank support handles on Twitter/X.
- **Sharing UPI PIN on Phone Calls:** Disclosing your 4-digit or 6-digit UPI PIN to callers claiming to be from Google Pay or PhonePe support.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **Do I need my UPI PIN to receive money?** No, you never need to enter your PIN to receive money.
2. **What is a collect request in UPI?** A feature where a user requests money from your UPI ID. It shows a pop-up prompting you to pay.
3. **Can money be stolen by scanning a QR code?** Scanning a QR code takes you to a payment page. Money is only debited if you enter your UPI PIN.
4. **How do scammers use OLX for UPI fraud?** They pose as buyers, send a fake payment QR code or collect request, and tell you to enter your PIN to receive the advance.
5. **What should I do if I sent money to a wrong UPI ID?** Contact your bank immediately, use the 'Raise Dispute' option in the UPI app, and report it on the NPCI portal (npci.org.in).
6. **What is a VPA?** Virtual Payment Address; your unique UPI ID (e.g., name@okaxis).
7. **Is there a limit on UPI transactions?** Yes, NPCI caps standard UPI transactions at ₹1 Lakh per day.
8. **Can I use UPI without a debit card?** You can set up UPI PIN using your Aadhaar card if your bank supports Aadhaar-based OTP verification.
9. **How do I report a UPI fraud?** Call **1930** immediately and register a complaint at cybercrime.gov.in.
10. **What is the golden hour in UPI fraud?** The first 2 hours after the fraud, during which freezing the money in the scammer's account is highly successful.
11. **Do UPI apps charge for filing disputes?** No, raising disputes is a free service.
12. **Can scammers hack my UPI if they know my UPI ID?** No, they cannot access your money using only your UPI ID. They need your UPI PIN or access to your device.
13. **Why did my UPI transaction fail but money was debited?** It is a technical failure. The money is usually refunded automatically within 24-48 hours.
14. **What is a dynamic QR code?** A QR code that contains a specific transaction amount and merchant detail pre-loaded.
15. **Can I link my credit card to UPI?** Yes, currently only RuPay credit cards can be linked to UPI.
16. **What is the cyber crime cell helpline?** 1930.
17. **How does NPCI help in UPI disputes?** NPCI acts as the central clearing house, helping banks verify transaction routes and process refunds.
18. **Can UPI PIN be reset online?** Yes, using your debit card details or Aadhaar via your UPI app.
19. **What is a spam UPI ID?** A UPI handle flagged by banks/NPCI for fraudulent activities. UPI apps warn you before paying such IDs.
20. **Should I block my bank account if UPI is compromised?** Yes, if you suspect your UPI PIN is leaked, change it immediately or block your bank account.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** Scanning a QR code will automatically credit money to your account. **Fact:** QR codes are only used to initiate payments; scanning a QR code can only deduct money, never credit it.
2. **Myth:** UPI is unsafe because it has no passwords. **Fact:** UPI is highly secure, requiring device binding, app lock, and a secret 4-digit or 6-digit transaction PIN.
3. **Myth:** The UPI PIN is the same as the ATM PIN. **Fact:** You set your UPI PIN separately in the UPI app; it is different from your physical ATM card PIN.
4. **Myth:** If you delete the UPI app, your bank account is closed. **Fact:** Deleting the app only removes the UPI interface; your bank account remains active.
5. **Myth:** Scammers can guess your UPI PIN easily. **Fact:** Only if you set simple codes like 123456 or use your birth date. Always use a random, secure PIN.
6. **Myth:** Banks can reverse UPI transactions instantly. **Fact:** Because UPI transfers are real-time, banks cannot reverse them without the consent of the receiver's bank or cyber cell orders.
7. **Myth:** The merchant can charge a fee for UPI payments. **Fact:** Government rules mandate zero merchant discount rate (MDR) for UPI, making it free for customers and merchants.
8. **Myth:** Only smartphones can use UPI. **Fact:** You can use UPI on feature phones without internet by dialing **\*99#** (USSD-based UPI).
9. **Myth:** A UPI collect request can access your contacts list. **Fact:** No, collect requests only use mobile numbers or VPAs to route payment demands.
10. **Myth:** All UPI apps are owned by private foreign companies. **Fact:** BHIM is owned by the government (NPCI). Other apps (GPay, PhonePe) operate as third-party providers on NPCI's network.
