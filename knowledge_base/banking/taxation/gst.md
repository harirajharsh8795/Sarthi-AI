## Eligibility
- Any Indian citizen above 18 years.
- Businesses registered in India.
- Entities holding a valid PAN card.
- Not Applicable for non-residents unless explicitly specified.

## Required Documents
- Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport).
- Address Proof (Utility Bill, Voter ID).
- Financial Statements (Bank statements, ITR).
- Business Registration (if applicable).
