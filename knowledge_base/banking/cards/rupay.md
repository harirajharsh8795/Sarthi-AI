# RuPay Card (रुपे कार्ड)

## Overview

### English
RuPay is India's domestic card payment network launched by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) in 2012. It offers debit, credit, and prepaid cards across all commercial, rural, and cooperative banks, featuring lower transaction fees and exclusive integration with UPI.

### Hindi
रुपे (RuPay) भारत का घरेलू कार्ड भुगतान नेटवर्क है, जिसे 2012 में नेशनल पेमेंट्स कॉरपोरेशन ऑफ इंडिया (NPCI) द्वारा लॉन्च किया गया था। यह सभी वाणिज्यिक, ग्रामीण और सहकारी बैंकों में डेबिट, क्रेडिट और प्रीपेड कार्ड प्रदान करता है, जिसमें कम लेनदेन शुल्क और यूपीआई (UPI) के साथ विशेष एकीकरण की सुविधा मिलती है।

## Quick Summary
RuPay was created under the guidance of the RBI to establish a secure, low-cost domestic card payment system in India. Since its launch, RuPay has become the dominant card network, playing a crucial role in government financial schemes like the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY). Every Jan Dhan account holder receives a free RuPay debit card with zero annual fees.

A key advantage of RuPay is its cost-efficiency. Because transactions are processed within India, merchant discount rates (MDR) are lower, saving costs for local shopkeepers. Additionally, RuPay credit cards offer a unique feature that allows users to link their cards to UPI apps (like Google Pay, BHIM, PhonePe) to make merchants scan payments directly from their credit limit, which is not supported by global networks.

---

## Definition
RuPay is a highly secure, domestic card payment network designed and operated by the NPCI, facilitating electronic retail payments across commercial banks, regional rural banks, and cooperative credit societies in India.

---

## Why It Matters
Every Indian citizen should understand RuPay cards because:
- **UPI Linking Benefit:** RuPay is currently the only card network whose credit cards can be linked directly to UPI, enabling PIN-based credit payments at local tea stalls and grocery shops.
- **Accident Insurance:** Offers complimentary accidental death and permanent disability insurance of up to ₹2 Lakhs to retail cardholders.
- **Support for Local Businesses:** Merchant transaction fees are lower, meaning local shopkeepers prefer RuPay over Visa or Mastercard.
- **National Pride and Data Safety:** Transaction data is stored locally in secure servers in India, ensuring national financial sovereignty.

---

## How It Works
The domestic routing and payment authorization cycle of a RuPay card:

Cardholder taps or enters PIN at merchant POS
↓
POS terminal sends transaction request to the merchant's bank
↓
Request is routed through NPCI's domestic payment switch (RuPay network)
↓
NPCI routes the request directly to the cardholder's issuing Bank in India
↓
Bank verifies balance and authorization, then deducts funds
↓
Response is routed back via NPCI to the merchant terminal
↓
Transaction completes in milliseconds (faster routing due to domestic servers)

## Eligibility
- **Zero Balance / Jan Dhan Accounts:** All PMJDY and BSBDA account holders are eligible for free RuPay Classic cards.
- **Regular Savings/Current Accounts:** Anyone holding a bank account can request a RuPay Classic, Platinum, or Select card from their bank.
- **Credit Cards:** Offered to individuals meeting the bank's income and CIBIL score requirements.

---

## Required Documents
- **Not Applicable:** If you have an active bank account, you can request a RuPay card by submitting a card upgrade request form. No additional documents are needed.

---

## Features
- **UPI Credit Integration:** Link credit cards to UPI for merchant QR code payments.
- **Contactless Payments:** Tap-and-pay enabled for transactions under ₹5,000.
- **NCMC (National Common Mobility Card) Wallet:** Many RuPay cards come with NCMC, which allows offline wallet payments for metro rides, buses, and toll booths across India.
- **Accidental Insurance Cover:** Complimenatry cover built into classic and platinum variants.

---

## Benefits
- **Zero Annual Fees:** PMJDY and basic RuPay cards have no annual maintenance charges.
- **Insurance Claim:** RuPay Classic offers ₹1 Lakh and RuPay Platinum offers ₹2 Lakhs of accidental insurance cover.
- **Exclusive Cashback:** Platinum and Select variants offer merchant discounts, cashbacks on utility bills, and free airport lounge access.

---

## Risks
- **Overseas Acceptance Limits:** While RuPay works globally through partnerships with Discover, Diners Club, and JCB networks, some foreign websites and smaller international shops may not accept it as widely as Visa or Mastercard.
- **Phishing Fraud:** Vulnerability to generic online card frauds if card numbers and OTPs are shared.

---

## Charges & Fees
Typical charges (subject to bank-specific tariffs and RBI guidelines):
- **Annual Maintenance Fees:** Nil for Jan Dhan/BSBDA RuPay cards. For standard RuPay Platinum, fees range from ₹150 to ₹300 per year (generally lower than foreign counterparts).
- **Merchant Discount Rate (MDR):** As per government mandate, zero MDR applies to UPI and RuPay debit card transactions, meaning merchants pay zero fees to accept them.
- **International Transaction Markup:** Nominal foreign currency markup (usually 1.5% to 2.5%) when used abroad.

---

## RBI / Government Rules
- **Promotion of Domestic Schemes:** The Government of India and RBI promote RuPay by mandating zero merchant fees (MDR) on RuPay debit transactions to encourage digital adoption.
- **Local Data Storage Compliance:** All transaction data must be stored locally in India within 24 hours, complying with RBI's data localization mandate (2018).
- **UPI Linking Permission:** RBI officially permitted linking of RuPay credit cards to UPI in 2022.

## Step-by-Step Process

### How to Link RuPay Credit Card to UPI:
1. **Open UPI App:** Open your preferred UPI application (BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm).
2. **Go to Profile:** Click on your profile picture and select 'Add Bank Account' or 'Link Credit Card'.
3. **Select Credit Card option:** Choose 'Link RuPay Credit Card on UPI'.
4. **Select Bank:** Select the bank that issued your RuPay credit card.
5. **Verify Details:** The app will automatically fetch your card details linked to your mobile number.
6. **Set UPI PIN:** Enter the last 6 digits and expiry date of the card. Enter the OTP received, and set a new 4 or 6 digit UPI PIN.
7. **Make Payment:** Scan any merchant QR code, select your RuPay credit card as the payment source, enter the UPI PIN, and complete the payment.

## Safety Tips
- **Active Card Rule for Insurance:** Remember that the accidental insurance benefit is only valid if you have performed at least one transaction (financial or non-financial) using your RuPay card within the 90 days prior to the accident.
- **Do not share PIN/CVV:** Keep your 3-digit CVV and 4-digit PIN confidential.
- **Turn off International Usage:** If you do not travel abroad, keep international settings switched off in your bank's app.

---

## Common Mistakes
- **Assuming RuPay Credit Cards work for Peer-to-Peer Transfer:** Trying to send money to a friend's personal UPI ID using a RuPay credit card on UPI. It only works for merchant payments (QR codes at shops).
- **Ignoring Insurance Active Period:** Not using the card for over 90 days, which invalidates the complimentary accidental insurance cover.
- **Paying extra to merchants:** Allowing merchants to charge extra fees (e.g., 2%) for swiping RuPay debit cards, which is illegal as RuPay debit has zero MDR.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What is a RuPay card?** It is India's domestic card payment network developed by the NPCI.
2. **Who owns RuPay?** The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).
3. **How does RuPay differ from Visa and Mastercard?** RuPay is a domestic Indian network with lower transaction fees; Visa and Mastercard are American multinational networks.
4. **Can I link my RuPay credit card to UPI?** Yes, this is an exclusive benefit of RuPay credit cards in India.
5. **Can I link my RuPay debit card to UPI?** Yes, you can use your debit card details to set up UPI PIN and make payments from your savings account.
6. **Are there any annual charges for RuPay cards?** PMJDY (Jan Dhan) RuPay cards have zero annual fees. Other variants have low annual fees set by the respective bank.
7. **Is my data safe with RuPay?** Yes, as all transaction data is processed and stored within India under RBI regulations.
8. **Does RuPay work outside India?** Yes, RuPay Global cards work internationally on ATMs, POS, and online sites through partnerships with Discover, Diners Club, and JCB networks.
9. **What is the accidental insurance cover on RuPay cards?** Up to ₹1 Lakh for Classic cards and ₹2 Lakhs for Platinum cards.
10. **What is the 90-day active card rule?** The insurance claim is valid only if the card was used at least once in the 90 days before the accident.
11. **What is NCMC on a RuPay card?** National Common Mobility Card; a feature allowing offline tap payments for metro, bus fares, and toll plazas.
12. **Are there different types of RuPay cards?** Yes, Classic, Platinum, and Select.
13. **Can I get a RuPay card from any bank?** Yes, almost all public sector, private sector, regional rural, and cooperative banks issue RuPay cards.
14. **Why do merchants prefer RuPay?** Because RuPay debit cards have zero Merchant Discount Rate (MDR), meaning shopkeepers pay no fees to accept them.
15. **Can I withdraw cash from any ATM using a RuPay card?** Yes, RuPay cards work at all ATMs across India.
16. **How do I file a RuPay insurance claim?** The nominee must submit the claim form, death certificate, FIR, and proof of card transaction within 90 days to the issuing bank.
17. **Can I use a RuPay card for international online shopping?** Yes, if it is a RuPay Global card and international transactions are enabled in your bank's app.
18. **Can I convert a Visa/Mastercard card to RuPay?** Yes, you can request your bank to replace your existing card with a RuPay card.
19. **What is the limit for contactless payments on RuPay?** Up to ₹5,000 per transaction without entering a PIN.
20. **Does RuPay offer airport lounge access?** Yes, RuPay Platinum and Select cards offer free access to domestic and international airport lounges.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** RuPay cards cannot be used for online shopping. **Fact:** RuPay cards are fully accepted on all Indian online websites and apps.
2. **Myth:** RuPay is only for poor people with Jan Dhan accounts. **Fact:** RuPay offers premium variants like Platinum and Select with high limits, lounge access, and exclusive merchant offers.
3. **Myth:** You can send money to friends using a RuPay credit card on UPI. **Fact:** RuPay credit cards on UPI can only be used to pay merchants (business accounts) by scanning QR codes.
4. **Myth:** RuPay cards do not work at ATMs outside India. **Fact:** RuPay Global cards work at international ATMs displaying Discover, Diners Club, or JCB logos.
5. **Myth:** All RuPay cards have free insurance forever. **Fact:** The insurance cover requires the cardholder to perform at least one transaction in the 90 days prior to the accident.
6. **Myth:** Visa is safer than RuPay. **Fact:** RuPay uses the same international chip and PIN security standards (EMV) and is highly secure.
7. **Myth:** RuPay card transactions take longer to process. **Fact:** Since servers are based locally in India, transaction processing is often faster than foreign networks.
8. **Myth:** You cannot convert purchases to EMIs on RuPay credit cards. **Fact:** Yes, you can convert transactions to EMIs just like any other credit card.
9. **Myth:** The merchant can charge a 2% fee to swipe a RuPay debit card. **Fact:** Charging customers extra fees for card swipes is illegal; RuPay debit cards have zero MDR.
10. **Myth:** RuPay is owned by a private foreign company. **Fact:** RuPay is owned by the NPCI, which is a non-profit organization owned by a consortium of Indian banks.

## Conversation Examples
1. **User:** Kya main apne credit card ko UPI se link kar sakta hu?
   **Assistant:** Haan, agar aapke paas RuPay credit card hai, toh aap use Google Pay, PhonePe ya BHIM app se link karke merchant QR code scan karke direct credit card se payment kar sakte hain. Visa ya Mastercard credit cards me ye suvidha abhi available nahi hai.
2. **User:** RuPay card ke accidental insurance ka claim kaise milta hai?
   **Assistant:** Claim lene ke liye cardholder ka accident se pehle 90 din ke andar kam se kam ek transaction (ATM, POS ya online) karna zaroori hai. Accident hone par nominee ko bank me death certificate, FIR aur claim form submit karna hoga.
3. **User:** Merchant bole raha hai ki RuPay card swipe karne par 2% extra charge lagega. Kya ye sahi hai?
   **Assistant:** Nahi, ye bilkul galat aur illegal hai. Government rules ke mutabik RuPay debit cards par zero MDR (Merchant Discount Rate) lagta hai, isliye dukandar aapse koi extra charge nahi le sakta.
4. **User:** RuPay aur Visa me kya farq hai?
   **Assistant:** RuPay ek Indian domestic network hai jiski transaction fees low hoti hain aur ye UPI se link hota hai. Visa ek US-based global network hai jo internationally zyada accepted hai par iski fees thodi zyada hoti hain.
5. **User:** Kya Jan Dhan account wale RuPay card par bhi lounge access milta hai?
   **Assistant:** Jan Dhan accounts ke sath basic RuPay Classic card milta hai jisme lounge access nahi hota. Lounge access ke liye aapko RuPay Platinum ya Select card ki zaroorat hoti hai.
6. **User:** NCMC kya hai jo mere RuPay card par likha hai?
   **Assistant:** NCMC (National Common Mobility Card) ek unique feature hai jisse aapka card ek travel pass ki tarah kaam karta hai. Aap isse metro, bus fares aur toll gates par directly tap karke payment kar sakte hain.
7. **User:** Kya main RuPay card se Amazon par shopping kar sakta hu?
   **Assistant:** Haan, bilkul. RuPay cards sabhi major shopping websites (Amazon, Flipkart) par accept hote hain.
8. **User:** Mera RuPay card foreign me kaam nahi kar raha.
   **Assistant:** Pehle check karein ki aapke card par 'Global' ya international logo (Discover/JCB) hai ya nahi. Agar hai, toh bank app me jaakar 'International Usage' ko active karein.
9. **User:** RuPay card ka annual charge kitna hota hai?
   **Assistant:** Jan Dhan accounts ke card bilkul free hote hain. Regular accounts ke cards par bank ₹150 se ₹250 tak ka nominal annual maintenance charge lete hain.
10. **User:** RuPay card kisne banaya hai?
    **Assistant:** RuPay card ko NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) ne banaya hai, jo India ke sabhi retail payments ko manage karti hai.
11. **User:** Kya main RuPay credit card se kisi ke bank account me paise transfer kar sakta hu?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, credit card to bank account transfer UPI ke zariye allowed nahi hai. Aap sirf registered merchants (shopkeepers) ko hi QR code scan karke pay kar sakte hain.
12. **User:** RuPay card par kitna insurance milta hai?
    **Assistant:** RuPay Classic card par ₹1 Lakh aur RuPay Platinum card par ₹2 Lakh ka accidental death and permanent disability insurance milta hai.
13. **User:** Kya cooperative banks bhi RuPay card dete hain?
    **Assistant:** Haan, sabhi cooperative aur regional rural banks (RRBs) apne customers ko RuPay debit cards issue karte hain.
14. **User:** RuPay credit card ko UPI se link karne par koi charge lagta hai?
    **Assistant:** Chote transactions (₹2,000 se kam) par koi merchant charge nahi lagta, aur customer ke liye ye service bilkul free hai.
15. **User:** Kya online booking ke liye RuPay card safe hai?
    **Assistant:** Haan, RuPay card transactions me RBI ke mandatory two-factor authentication (OTP) ke zariye complete security milti hai, jo ise poori tarah safe banati hai.
