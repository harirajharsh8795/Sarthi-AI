# PIN Reset (पिन रीसेट करना)

## Overview

### English
PIN reset is a security feature that allows debit or credit cardholders to change their existing Personal Identification Number (PIN) or generate a new one if forgotten. Modern banking supports paperless PIN generation (Green PIN) via ATMs, mobile apps, net banking, or IVR.

### Hindi

## Quick Summary
A Personal Identification Number (PIN) is a 4-digit numeric code required to authorize ATM cash withdrawals and physical POS retail transactions. If a customer forgets their PIN or suspects it has been compromised, they must reset it immediately. Historically, banks used to courier printed paper PIN mailers to the customer's registered address, which was slow and carried security risks.

Today, banks have adopted the "Green PIN" standard, which allows customers to generate and reset their PINs instantly. To generate a Green PIN, a one-time password (OTP) is sent to the registered mobile number. The customer inputs this OTP at an ATM or on the bank's portal to create a secure 4-digit code.

---

## Definition
A PIN Reset is the cryptographic process of updating the Personal Identification Number linked to a payment card in the bank's card management database, using multi-factor authentication (like OTP and card details) to verify the cardholder's identity.

---

## Why It Matters
Knowing how to reset a card PIN is essential to:
- **Prevent Unauthorized Debits:** If someone sees you typing your PIN at a shop, resetting it immediately blocks them from using your card.
- **Restore Account Access:** Resolves card blockages due to entering the wrong PIN three times.
- **Maintain High Security:** Periodic PIN changes are a standard recommendation by the RBI to keep accounts safe.
- **Environmental Friendly:** The paperless Green PIN process saves trees by eliminating paper envelopes.

---

## How It Works
The standard Green PIN reset process at an ATM:

Cardholder forgets PIN or wants to change it
↓
Cardholder inserts card at bank's ATM and selects "PIN Generation/Green PIN"
↓
Bank verification system sends 6-digit OTP to the registered mobile number
↓
Cardholder enters the OTP and their bank account number
↓
System validates the details and prompts for "New PIN"
↓
Cardholder enters a secret 4-digit PIN twice
↓
ATM screen displays "PIN Changed Successfully" and database updates in real-time

## Eligibility
- **Applicable Cards:** All active debit, credit, and forex cards issued by banks in India.
- **Registered Mobile:** The customer's mobile number must be registered with the bank to receive the mandatory OTP.

---

## Required Documents
- **Not Applicable for Digital Reset:** No physical documents are required. You only need:
  - Active Debit/Credit card.
  - Linked registered mobile phone.
  - Linked bank account number.

---

## Features
- **Green PIN:** Instantly generated via secure digital channels (ATM, Web, App).
- **Block Protection:** Automatically resets the temporary block imposed due to 3 incorrect PIN entries.
- **IVR Reset:** Support for voice-guided PIN resets by calling the bank's automated phone system.

---

## Benefits
- **Instant Activation:** Card can be used immediately after the PIN is set.
- **Zero Travel:** Can be done from home using net banking or mobile apps (without visiting the branch).
- **Reduced Fraud Risk:** Eliminates the risk of paper PINs being intercepted in the post.

---

## Risks
- **SIM Swap Fraud:** If fraudsters clone your SIM card, they can intercept the Green PIN OTP and reset your card PIN.
- **Shoulder Surfing:** Someone watching you input your new PIN at the ATM or on your phone.
- **Phishing Portals:** Fake bank websites prompting you to enter card details and PINs to "verify" or "upgrade" the card.

---

## Charges & Fees
- **PIN Reset Charges:** Nil (Resetting PIN online or via bank ATMs is free. A minor charge may apply if a customer requests a physical paper PIN mailer via post).

---

## RBI / Government Rules
- **Three Strikes Rule:** RBI guidelines require banks to temporarily block card transactions if an incorrect PIN is entered three consecutive times, to prevent brute-force attacks by thieves.
- **Mandatory OTP Verification:** Banks must enforce multi-factor authentication (specifically OTP on registered mobile) for all paperless PIN resets.

## Step-by-Step Process

### How to Reset Debit Card PIN via Net Banking:
1. **Log in:** Open the bank's official net banking portal and log in.
2. **Go to Cards:** Go to the 'Cards' or 'Requests' section.
3. **Select PIN Generation:** Click on 'Instant PIN Generation' or 'Debit Card PIN Reset'.
4. **Choose Card:** Select the card number from the list and enter the CVV.
5. **Verify OTP:** Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
6. **Set New PIN:** Enter a new 4-digit PIN, re-enter it to confirm, and click submit.
7. **Instant Update:** The PIN is updated immediately; you can use it for your next purchase.

---

## Safety Tips
- **Avoid Predictable PINs:** Do not set simple PINs like 1234, 0000, 1111, or your birth year (e.g., 1995).
- **Change PIN regularly:** Change your card PIN once every six months to maintain security.
- **Delete OTP Messages:** Immediately delete the OTP messages from your phone after the PIN is set.
- **Do not share PIN with family:** Keep your PIN private. Do not disclose it to relatives, children, or bank employees.

---

## Common Mistakes
- **Entering Wrong PIN repeatedly:** Continuing to try different forgotten PINs until the card is blocked. If you forget, perform a PIN reset immediately instead.
- **Using Public Computers for Net Banking Reset:** Resetting PINs on public cyber cafe computers, which may have keyloggers installed.
- **Disclosing CVV during Call:** Sharing the card's 3-digit CVV with customer care executives during phone-based PIN resets. The IVR system handles inputs securely; humans do not need CVVs.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What is a Green PIN?** A paperless, OTP-based digital method to generate or reset a card PIN.
2. **How can I reset my ATM PIN if I forgot it?** You can reset it using your bank's app, net banking, or by visiting their ATM and using the 'Green PIN' option.
3. **What happens if I enter the wrong PIN 3 times?** The card is temporarily blocked for 24 hours to prevent unauthorized access. You can reset it immediately online to unblock it.
4. **Is it safe to reset my PIN online?** Yes, it is secure as long as you are using the official bank app or portal on a secure private connection.
5. **Can bank staff reset my PIN?** Bank employees cannot see or set your PIN. They can only help you trigger the Green PIN OTP process.
6. **What is an IVR PIN reset?** Resetting your PIN by calling the bank's customer service number and entering details via the phone keypad on the automated voice system.
7. **Is a PIN required for online transactions?** No, online transactions in India require card details (number, expiry, CVV) and an OTP. The PIN is used for ATMs and retail shop machines.
8. **Do credit cards also use Green PIN?** Yes, the process to set and reset credit card PINs is also paperless.
9. **Can I use my new PIN immediately?** Yes, the PIN works instantly for transactions.
10. **Do banks charge for resetting a PIN?** Resetting via ATM, app, or net banking is free.
11. **Why am I not receiving the PIN reset OTP?** Check if your mobile network is stable and verify if your number is correctly registered in the bank's records.
12. **Can I reset my PIN at another bank's ATM?** No, for security reasons, Green PIN generation must be done only at your own bank's ATMs.
13. **Do minor cards have PINs?** Yes, minor debit cards have their own 4-digit PINs.
14. **What should I do if my card is blocked due to wrong PIN entries?** You can wait 24 hours for it to unblock automatically, or perform a PIN reset online to unblock it instantly.
15. **Can I reset my PIN without a registered mobile number?** No, since OTP verification is mandatory, you must first register your active mobile number at the branch.
16. **How often should I change my card PIN?** It is recommended to change your PIN once every 6 months for optimal security.
17. **What is a PIN mailer?** The old system where banks printed the PIN on paper and sent it in a sealed envelope via post.
18. **Can I set the same PIN for my debit and credit cards?** You can, but it is safer to have different PINs for different cards.
19. **What is shoulder surfing?** The practice of looking over a person's shoulder to steal their PIN or password.
20. **Can I reset my forex card PIN?** Yes, forex card PINs can be reset online through the bank's prepaid card portal.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** Bank managers can see my PIN in the computer. **Fact:** PINs are encrypted and stored securely; no bank employee has access to view your PIN.
2. **Myth:** I must pay to change my PIN at the ATM. **Fact:** Changing the PIN at your bank's ATM is completely free.
3. **Myth:** If I enter my PIN backwards at an ATM, it alerts the police. **Fact:** This is a famous myth; entering the PIN backwards will simply show a "Wrong PIN" error.
4. **Myth:** The PIN is the same as the CVV. **Fact:** The PIN is a 4-digit secret code you create; CVV is a 3-digit code printed on the back of the card.
5. **Myth:** If someone has my card, they can reset the PIN without my phone. **Fact:** A PIN reset requires the OTP sent to your registered mobile number, making it secure.
6. **Myth:** New debit cards come with a default PIN like 1234. **Fact:** New cards have no PIN set; you must generate a new Green PIN before using the card.
7. **Myth:** I can change my PIN at a shop's swipe machine. **Fact:** POS swipe machines cannot be used to reset or change your PIN; use ATMs or official apps.
8. **Myth:** Resetting my PIN deletes my transaction history. **Fact:** Changing the security PIN has no effect on your bank statement or account history.
9. **Myth:** A blocked card must be thrown away. **Fact:** If blocked temporarily due to wrong PIN entries, it can be reactivated by resetting the PIN.
10. **Myth:** Sharing my PIN with my spouse is legally allowed by banks. **Fact:** Bank terms state that cards are non-transferable and PINs must not be shared with anyone, including family.

## Conversation Examples
1. **User:** Sir, main debit card ka PIN bhul gaya hu, ab kya karu?
   **Assistant:** Aap apne bank ke ATM me jaakar 'Pin Generation/Green PIN' option select karein, ya mobile app me 'Instant PIN Generation' par jaakar naya PIN set kar sakte hain. Iske liye aapke registered mobile par ek OTP aayega.
2. **User:** Green PIN kya hota hai?
   **Assistant:** Green PIN paperless PIN generation method hai. Isme bank aapko paper par PIN likh kar nahi bhejta, balki aap khud ATM ya mobile app se OTP verify karke naya PIN bana sakte hain.
3. **User:** Mera card 3 baar wrong PIN daalne par block ho gaya hai, naya card lena padega?
   **Assistant:** Naya card lene ki zaroorat nahi hai. Ye block 24 hours ke liye hota hai. Agar aapko turant activate karna hai, toh aap online bank app se PIN reset kar dein, card instantly chalne lagega.
4. **User:** Kya main kisi bhi dusre bank ke ATM me jaakar PIN badal sakta hu?
   **Assistant:** Security reasons ke kaaran aap Green PIN generation ya PIN change sirf apne hi bank ke ATM par kar sakte hain. Dusre bank ke ATM par ye service nahi chalegi.
5. **User:** Mobile app se PIN reset karne par koi charge lagta hai?
   **Assistant:** Nahi, mobile app ya internet banking ke zariye card ka PIN reset karna bilkul free hai.
6. **User:** OTP nahi aa raha, kya bina OTP ke PIN reset ho sakta hai?
   **Assistant:** Nahi, safety ke liye OTP mandatory hai. Agar OTP nahi aa raha, toh check karein ki network sahi hai aur bank me sahi mobile number linked hai.
7. **User:** Mere pass bank account number nahi hai, kya main ATM me PIN reset kar sakta hu?
   **Assistant:** ATM me Green PIN setup ke waqt bank account number enter karna padta hai. Isliye account number passbook se dekh kar saath le jayein.
8. **User:** credit card ka PIN reset kaise hoga?
   **Assistant:** Credit card ka PIN reset bhi aap bank app ke cards section me jaakar, credit card choose karke 'Instant PIN generation' se kar sakte hain.
9. **User:** Kya bank employee phone karke naya PIN set karne me madad kar sakta hai?
   **Assistant:** Bank employees kabhi bhi call par PIN reset nahi karte aur na hi OTP maangte hain. Aise calls se sawdhan rahein, ye fraud ho sakte hain.
10. **User:** ATM me PIN enter karte waqt kya sawdhani rakhein?
    **Assistant:** PIN enter karte waqt keypad ko apne dusre haath se cover karein taaki koi peeche khada vyakti ya CCTV camera aapka PIN na dekh sake (Shoulder Surfing se bachein).
11. **User:** Mujhe lagta hai kisi ne mera PIN dekh liya hai, kya badlu?
    **Assistant:** Haan, turant mobile banking app kholkar ya kisi bhi ATM me jaakar apna PIN badal dein taaki aapka account surakshit rahe.
12. **User:** IVR se PIN reset kaise hota hai?
    **Assistant:** Aap bank ke helpline number par call karke IVR options me 'Card Services' aur fir 'Generate PIN' select karte hain, fir system aapko automated call par details enter karne ko kehta hai.
13. **User:** Kya main 0000 ya apna birth year PIN set kar sakta hu?
    **Assistant:** Aise predictable PINs rakhna unsafe hai kyuki fraudsters inka asani se andaza laga sakte hain. Koi random 4-digit code hi choose karein.
14. **User:** Naya card aaya hai, purana PIN chalega kya?
    **Assistant:** Nahi, jab bhi replacement ya naya card aata hai, aapko uske liye naya PIN generate karna padta hai. Purana PIN kaam nahi karega.
15. **User:** ATM PIN change karne par koi message aata hai?
    **Assistant:** Haan, PIN badalne par aapke registered mobile number par bank ki taraf se confirmation SMS aata hai ki aapka PIN change ho gaya hai.
