**English:** A disclaimer is a formal statement intended to specify or delimit the scope of rights and obligations that may be exercised and enforced by parties in a legally recognized relationship. In the context of Saarthi AI, this disclaimer serves as a protective legal shield, clarifying that the information provided across banking, legal, medical, and public administration domains is for informational and educational purposes only and does not constitute professional advice.

## Definition

**English:** A disclaimer is a repudiation or denial of responsibility or connection. Legally, it is a statement designed to protect the information provider from liability and prevent the establishment of a duty of care, ensuring the recipient understands the informational limits of the provided data.

**Hindi:** अस्वीकरण जिम्मेदारी या संबंध का खंडन या इनकार है। कानूनी रूप से, यह एक ऐसा बयान है जिसे सूचना प्रदाता को देयता से बचाने और देखभाल के कर्तव्य की स्थापना को रोकने के लिए डिज़ाइन किया गया है, यह सुनिश्चित करते हुए कि प्राप्तकर्ता प्रदान किए गए डेटा की सूचनात्मक सीमाओं को समझता है।

**Hinglish:** Disclaimer responsibility ya connection ka denial hota hai. Legally, ye ek statement hai jo information provider ko liability se bachane aur duty of care establish hone se rokne ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Isse ye ensure hota hai ki user information ki limits ko samjhe.

## Why It Matters

**English:** Disclaimers are crucial because they set user expectations and protect the platform from unwarranted lawsuits or claims. They prevent misunderstandings, especially in high-stakes domains like health (where incorrect actions can be fatal), finance (where losses can be devastating), and legal matters (where misinterpretations can lead to severe penalties).

**Hindi:** अस्वीकरण महत्वपूर्ण हैं क्योंकि वे उपयोगकर्ता की अपेक्षाओं को निर्धारित करते हैं और प्लेटफ़ॉर्म को अनुचित मुकदमों या दावों से बचाते हैं। वे गलतफहमी को रोकते हैं, खासकर स्वास्थ्य (जहां गलत कार्यों के घातक परिणाम हो सकते हैं), वित्त (जहां नुकसान विनाशकारी हो सकते हैं), और कानूनी मामलों (जहां गलत व्याख्याओं से गंभीर दंड हो सकते हैं) जैसे उच्च-जोखिम वाले डोमेन में।

**Hinglish:** Disclaimers isliye zaruri hain kyunki wo user expectations set karte hain aur platform ko unjustified lawsuits se bachate hain. ## Common Myths vs Facts

1. **Myth:** AI is a certified doctor.
   **Fact:** AI is software trained on text; it cannot examine patients or prescribe medicine.
2. **Myth:** If AI says an investment is safe, I won't lose money.
   **Fact:** All investments carry market risks. AI cannot guarantee financial returns.
3. **Myth:** I can use AI as my lawyer in court.
   **Fact:** AI cannot represent you, nor is its advice legally actionable.
4. **Myth:** AI outputs are approved by the government.
   **Fact:** AI provides information from public domain but is not officially endorsed by any government body.
5. **Myth:** The AI is liable if I follow its bad advice.
   **Fact:** The Terms of Service and Disclaimers explicitly waive any liability from the AI creators.
6. **Myth:** AI can approve my credit card application.
   **Fact:** Only banks have the authority to approve financial products.
7. **Myth:** I don't need to read terms and conditions if the AI summarizes them.
   **Fact:** Summaries may miss critical nuances; you must read original legal documents.
8. **Myth:** AI can file my FIR at the police station.
   **Fact:** AI can guide you on the process, but you must physically or through official online portals file an FIR.
9. **Myth:** AI's medical diagnosis is 100% accurate.
   **Fact:** AI can hallucinate or lack clinical context. It is never 100% accurate for medical diagnosis.
10. **Myth:** Disclaimers are just standard text and mean nothing in Indian law.
    **Fact:** Disclaimers form a critical part of the digital contract under the IT Act, 2000, and are legally enforceable.
