# National Emergency Contacts and Assistance Helplines

## Overview
**English:** Emergency contacts are verified, nationally and regionally integrated toll-free numbers established by the Government of India and various regulatory bodies (like RBI for banking, MoHFW for health, and MHA for security) to provide immediate assistance to citizens during medical, legal, financial, or civic crises.
**Hindi Unicode:** आपातकालीन संपर्क वे सत्यापित, राष्ट्रीय और क्षेत्रीय रूप से एकीकृत टोल-फ्री नंबर हैं जो भारत सरकार और विभिन्न नियामक निकायों द्वारा नागरिकों को चिकित्सा, कानूनी, वित्तीय या नागरिक संकट के दौरान तत्काल सहायता प्रदान करने के लिए स्थापित किए गए हैं।
**Hinglish:** Emergency contacts verified, nationally aur regionally integrated toll-free numbers hote hain jo Government of India aur regulatory bodies (jaise RBI, MoHFW, MHA) dwara set up kiye gaye hain taaki citizens ko medical, legal, financial ya civic emergencies mein turant madad mil sake.

## Quick Summary
**English:** A consolidated directory of all critical helplines including 112 (National Emergency), 1930 (Cyber Fraud), 108 (Ambulance), 1091 (Women Helpline), and domain-specific contacts for banking, legal, and public administration emergencies.
**Hindi Unicode:** सभी महत्वपूर्ण हेल्पलाइनों की एक समेकित निर्देशिका जिसमें 112 (राष्ट्रीय आपातकाल), 1930 (साइबर धोखाधड़ी), 108 (एम्बुलेंस), 1091 (महिला हेल्पलाइन), और बैंकिंग, कानूनी और लोक प्रशासन आपात स्थितियों के लिए डोमेन-विशिष्ट संपर्क शामिल हैं।
**Hinglish:** Yeh ek consolidated directory hai jismein saari zaroori helplines jaise 112 (National Emergency), 1930 (Cyber Fraud), 108 (Ambulance), aur domain-specific helplines shamil hain.

## Definition
**English:** An emergency contact helpline is a dedicated telecommunication service, often operating 24/7, designed to act as the first point of contact between a citizen in distress and the respective first responder or resolution authority.
**Hindi Unicode:** एक आपातकालीन संपर्क हेल्पलाइन एक समर्पित दूरसंचार सेवा है, जो अक्सर 24/7 संचालित होती है, जिसे संकट में फंसे नागरिक और संबंधित प्रथम प्रतिक्रियाकर्ता या समाधान प्राधिकरण के बीच संपर्क के पहले बिंदु के रूप में कार्य करने के लिए डिज़ाइन किया गया है।
**Hinglish:** Emergency contact helpline ek dedicated telecommunication service hai jo 24/7 kaam karti hai, jiska main maqsad distress mein phase citizens aur first responders ke beech first point of contact banna hai.

## Why It Matters
**English:** Timely access to the correct emergency contact can save lives, prevent financial ruin, ensure legal protection, and expedite public administration interventions. Memorizing or having immediate access to these numbers is a critical life skill.
**Hindi Unicode:** सही आपातकालीन संपर्क तक समय पर पहुंच जीवन बचा सकती है, वित्तीय बर्बादी को रोक सकती है, कानूनी सुरक्षा सुनिश्चित कर सकती है और लोक प्रशासन के हस्तक्षेप को तेज कर सकती है।

## Applicability
- **Who is covered:** All residents, citizens, and tourists present within the geographical boundaries of India.
- **Who is exempt:** None. Emergency services are fundamental rights available to all without discrimination.
- **Special conditions:** Some numbers (like 100, 101, 102) are legacy numbers being gradually integrated into the unified 112 Emergency Response Support System (ERSS).

## Rights of Citizens
**English:** Citizens have the right to receive prompt, non-discriminatory, and toll-free assistance when calling an official emergency helpline, regardless of their telecom provider or account balance.
**Hindi Unicode:** नागरिकों को किसी भी आधिकारिक आपातकालीन हेल्पलाइन पर कॉल करने पर बिना किसी भेदभाव के और टोल-फ्री सहायता प्राप्त करने का अधिकार है, चाहे उनका टेलीकॉम प्रदाता या अकाउंट बैलेंस कुछ भी हो।
**Hinglish:** Citizens ka haq hai ki unhe bina kisi bhed-bhav ke aur toll-free assistance mile jab wo official emergency helpline par call karein, chahe unka balance zero hi kyun na ho.

## Duties & Responsibilities
**English:** Citizens must use emergency contacts responsibly. Making hoax calls, prank calls, or verbally abusing helpline operators is a punishable offense under the Indian Penal Code and Telecommunications Act.
**Hindi Unicode:** नागरिकों को आपातकालीन संपर्कों का जिम्मेदारी से उपयोग करना चाहिए। झूठी कॉल करना, प्रैंक कॉल करना या हेल्पलाइन ऑपरेटरों को गाली देना भारतीय दंड संहिता और दूरसंचार अधिनियम के तहत एक दंडनीय अपराध है।
**Hinglish:** Citizens ko emergency numbers ka use responsibly karna chahiye. Fake calls ya prank calls karna ek punishable offence hai aur isse zarooratmand logon ki madad mein delay ho sakta hai.

## Step-by-Step Procedure / Usage Guide
**English:**
1. Dial the required emergency number (e.g., 112).
2. State the nature of the emergency calmly and clearly (Medical, Police, Fire).
3. Provide your exact location (Landmarks, Pin code).
4. Follow the dispatcher's instructions while help arrives. Do not hang up unless instructed.
**Hindi Unicode:**
1. आवश्यक आपातकालीन नंबर डायल करें (उदा., 112)।
2. आपातकाल की प्रकृति को स्पष्ट रूप से बताएं।
3. अपना सटीक स्थान (लैंडमार्क, पिन कोड) प्रदान करें।
4. मदद आने तक डिस्पैचर के निर्देशों का पालन करें।
**Hinglish:**
1. Zaroorat ke hisaab se emergency number dial karein (jaise 112).
2. Apni problem ya emergency clearly batayein (jaise Police, Medical, Fire).
3. Apni exact location aur aas-paas ka landmark batayein.
4. Operator ki instructions follow karein aur phone disconnect na karein jab tak bola na jaye.

## Required Documents / Reference Tables
No documents are required to initiate an emergency call. However, for follow-ups (e.g., Cyber fraud on 1930), keep the following ready:
- Bank Account Number / UPI ID
- Transaction ID / UTR Number
- Aadhaar Card / Identity Proof
- Vehicle Registration Number (for highway emergencies)

- **RBI (Reserve Bank of India):** 14448

## Common Errors & Misconceptions
- **Error:** Calling 100 for a banking fraud.
  - *Correction:* Always call 1930 for cyber/banking fraud to ensure immediate freezing of funds.
- **Error:** Assuming helplines don't work without a SIM card.
  - *Correction:* Emergency number 112 can be dialed from any mobile phone, even without an active SIM card or network (using other available towers).

## Safety & Verification Tips
**English:** Never share OTPs, CVVs, or PINs with emergency responders. Genuine government helplines will NEVER ask for your banking passwords to dispatch an ambulance or police unit.
**Hindi Unicode:** आपातकालीन उत्तरदाताओं के साथ कभी भी ओटीपी, सीवीवी या पिन साझा न करें। वास्तविक सरकारी हेल्पलाइन कभी भी एम्बुलेंस या पुलिस यूनिट भेजने के लिए आपके बैंकिंग पासवर्ड नहीं मांगेंगी।
**Hinglish:** Emergency responders ke saath kabhi bhi OTP, CVV, ya PIN share na karein. Genuine helplines kabhi bhi aapse banking passwords nahi mangti madad bhejne ke liye.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** 112 replaces 100 instantly everywhere.
   **Fact:** While 112 is the integrated number, 100 still connects to local police control rooms in many regions.
2. **Myth:** Emergency helplines will ask for your OTP for verification.
   **Fact:** No legitimate emergency helpline will EVER ask for an OTP, PIN, or CVV.
3. **Myth:** 108 ambulance charges a fee.
   **Fact:** 108 is a free government medical service for emergencies.
4. **Myth:** Cybercrime helpline 1930 is only for credit card frauds.
   **Fact:** 1930 covers ALL financial cyber frauds, including UPI, NetBanking, and wallet scams.
5. **Myth:** You cannot dial 112 without a SIM card.
   **Fact:** Emergency numbers bypass regular carrier restrictions; they can often connect without an active SIM.
6. **Myth:** Childline 1098 is only for rescuing orphans.
   **Fact:** 1098 helps any child in distress, including cases of abuse, missing children, or child labour.
7. **Myth:** Helplines only operate in Hindi and English.
   **Fact:** Most national helplines, like 112 and 1930, have regional language support based on the caller's state.
8. **Myth:** Calling 14448 (RBI) will get my money back from a scammer.
   **Fact:** 14448 is for banking grievances; for active scams and money freezing, call 1930.
9. **Myth:** Women helplines are only for domestic violence.
   **Fact:** 1091/181 handles workplace harassment, stalking, cyberbullying, and all forms of distress against women.
10. **Myth:** I will get into legal trouble if I call 112 for someone else.
    **Fact:** Good Samaritans are protected by law; calling for help for others is encouraged and safe.

## Conversation Examples

**Scenario 1: Reporting a Medical Emergency**
- **Citizen:** Meri maa behosh ho gayi hain, unhe turant hospital le jana hai. (My mother has fainted, she needs to be taken to the hospital immediately.)
- **Assistant:** Ghabrayein nahi. Kripya turant 108 (Ambulance) par call karein aur apna exact address batayein. (Don't panic. Please call 108 immediately and state your exact address.)

**Scenario 2: Reporting Cyber Fraud**
- **Citizen:** Mere bank account se abhi 50,000 rupaye gayab ho gaye, maine koi OTP bhi nahi diya!
- **Assistant:** Yeh ek cyber fraud hai. Bina deri kiye 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline) par call karein taaki aapka transaction freeze kiya ja sake. Apna account number aur transaction SMS ready rakhein.

**Scenario 3: Domestic Abuse Reporting**
- **Citizen:** Pados wale ghar se maar-peet ki awaazein aa rahi hain ek aurat ki.
- **Assistant:** Yeh bahut serious hai. Aap turant 1091 (Women Helpline) ya 112 (National Emergency) par call karke anonymous complaint darj kara sakte hain. Police turant dispatch hogi.

**Scenario 4: Child Labour Sighting**
- **Citizen:** Ek chhota bachcha paas ke dhaba mein kaam kar raha hai. Kise bataun?
- **Assistant:** Aapko 1098 (Childline) par call karke inform karna chahiye. Yeh helpline 24/7 bachchon ki suraksha ke liye kaam karti hai.

**Scenario 5: Banking Grievance**
- **Citizen:** Bank pichle ek mahine se mera loan dispute solve nahi kar raha hai.
- **Assistant:** Agar bank se samadhan nahi mil raha hai, toh aap RBI Ombudsman ki toll-free helpline 14448 par call karke apni shikayat darj karwa sakte hain.

**Scenario 6: Highway Breakdown**
- **Citizen:** Meri gaadi highway par kharab ho gayi hai aur raat ka waqt hai.
- **Assistant:** Aap National Highway helpline 1033 par call karein. Wo aapko nearest toll-plaza se towing ya patrol assistance bhejenge.

**Scenario 7: Fire Emergency**
- **Citizen:** Yahan aag lag gayi hai factory mein!
- **Assistant:** Kripya turant 112 ya 101 dial karein aur operator ko apni location batayein. Sabhi ko building se bahar nikalne ko kahein.

**Scenario 8: Mental Health Crisis**
- **Citizen:** Mujhe bahut depression ho raha hai aur negative thoughts aa rahe hain.
- **Assistant:** Aap akele nahi hain. Kripya turant Tele-MANAS helpline 14416 ya Kiran helpline 1800-599-0019 par call karein. Trained counselors aapki madad karenge.

**Scenario 9: Train Travel Issue**
- **Citizen:** Train mein koi mera luggage chori karke bhaag gaya.
- **Assistant:** Kripya turant Railway Helpline 139 par call karein aur security options choose karein. TTE ya RPF aapki coach mein madad ke liye aayenge.

**Scenario 10: Aadhaar Fraud**
- **Citizen:** Mujhe lagta hai kisi ne mere Aadhaar se naya SIM card liya hai.
- **Assistant:** Yeh identity theft ho sakta hai. UIDAI helpline 1947 par call karein aur apne Aadhaar biometrics ko online lock kar dein.

**Scenario 11: Tourist Assistance**
- **Citizen:** Main India ghoomne aayi hoon aur mera passport ghoom gaya hai.
- **Assistant:** Aap Incredible India Tourist Helpline 1363 par call karein. Wo aapko nearest embassy aur police station ki jaankari denge.

**Scenario 12: Senior Citizen Harassment**
- **Citizen:** Mere beta-bahu mujhe ghar se nikalne ki dhamki de rahe hain. Main 70 saal ka hoon.
- **Assistant:** Aap bilkul na ghabrayein. Elderline 14567 par call karein. Wo aapko legal aur emotional assistance dono provide karenge.

**Scenario 13: Consumer Fraud**
- **Citizen:** Maine online fridge mangaya tha par kharab piece aaya hai aur return nahi le rahe.
- **Assistant:** Aap National Consumer Helpline 1915 par call karke is e-commerce company ke khilaf complaint darj kar sakte hain.

**Scenario 14: Disaster Warning**
- **Citizen:** Yahan bahut bhari baarish se paani bhar gaya hai aur naddi ka paani badh raha hai.
- **Assistant:** Kripya NDMA/State Disaster Helpline 1070 ya 1078 par call karein evacuation ki jaankari aur relief boats ke liye.

**Scenario 15: Suspect Activity**
- **Citizen:** Mere pados mein kuch log sandigdh (suspicious) lag rahe hain, unke paas hatiyaar hain.
- **Assistant:** Turant 112 par call karein aur Police ko inform karein. Apni pehchaan gupt rakhne ki request bhi kar sakte hain.
