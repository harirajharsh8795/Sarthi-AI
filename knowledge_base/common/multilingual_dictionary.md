## Common Errors & Misconceptions
- **Error:** Confusing "Bail" (ज़मानत) with "Acquittal" (दोषमुक्ति). Bail is temporary release; acquittal is being found not guilty.
- **Error:** Assuming "Generic Medicine" means low quality. It simply means the medicine is unbranded but contains the same active ingredients.
- **Error:** Translating "Affidavit" as a simple letter. It is a legally binding sworn statement.
- **Error:** Believing "Overdraft" is free money. It is a loan that accrues high interest.

    - **EN:** A beneficiary is the person designated to receive the funds, property, or benefits of a scheme or policy.
    - **HI:** लाभार्थी वह व्यक्ति होता है जिसे किसी योजना या पॉलिसी का धन, संपत्ति या लाभ प्राप्त करने के लिए नामित किया जाता है।
    - **Hinglish:** Beneficiary wo person hai jise kisi scheme ya policy ke funds, property, ya benefits receive karne ke liye designate kiya gaya ho.

16. **What does 'Power of Attorney' mean?**
    - **EN:** A legal document giving one person the power to act for another person in legal or financial matters.
    - **HI:** एक कानूनी दस्तावेज जो एक व्यक्ति को कानूनी या वित्तीय मामलों में दूसरे व्यक्ति के लिए कार्य करने की शक्ति देता है।
    - **Hinglish:** Ek legal document jo ek person ko legal ya financial matters mein dusre person ke behalf par act karne ki power deta hai.

17. **What is an 'Ordinance'?**
    - **EN:** A temporary law passed by the President or Governor when the Parliament or State Legislature is not in session.
    - **HI:** संसद या राज्य विधानमंडल के सत्र में न होने पर राष्ट्रपति या राज्यपाल द्वारा पारित एक अस्थायी कानून।
    - **Hinglish:** Ek temporary law jo President ya Governor pass karte hain jab Parliament ya State Legislature session mein nahi hoti.

18. **What does 'Remittance' mean?**
    - **EN:** It refers to money sent by a person working abroad to their family in their home country.
    - **HI:** यह विदेश में काम करने वाले व्यक्ति द्वारा अपने देश में अपने परिवार को भेजे गए धन को संदर्भित करता है।
    - **Hinglish:** Yeh foreign mein kaam karne wale person dwara apni home country mein family ko bheje gaye paise ko refer karta hai.

19. **What is 'Immunity' in medical terms?**
    - **EN:** The body's ability to protect itself from an infectious disease.
    - **HI:** संक्रामक रोग से खुद को बचाने की शरीर की क्षमता।
    - **Hinglish:** Infectious disease se khud ko protect karne ki body ki ability.

20. **What is a 'Decree' in a court?**
    - **EN:** A decree is the formal and final expression of an adjudication by a court.
    - **HI:** डिक्री अदालत द्वारा न्यायनिर्णयन की औपचारिक और अंतिम अभिव्यक्ति है।
    - **Hinglish:** Decree court dwara adjudication ka formal aur final expression hota hai.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** A generic medicine is not as effective as a branded one.
   **Fact:** Generic medicines have the same active ingredients and are equally effective, just more affordable.
2. **Myth:** Overdraft money is free to use.
   **Fact:** Overdraft is a short-term loan and you will be charged high interest on the used amount.
3. **Myth:** Getting bail means the case is closed and you are innocent.
   **Fact:** Bail is just temporary freedom during the trial; the court case continues.
4. **Myth:** An affidavit is just a normal application letter.
   **Fact:** An affidavit is a legally sworn oath; lying on it is a punishable offense.
5. **Myth:** Subsidies are given to everyone regardless of income.
   **Fact:** Subsidies are mostly targeted towards marginalized or specific income groups to provide relief.
6. **Myth:** Anyone can issue a government gazette.
   **Fact:** Only authorized government bodies publish official gazettes.
7. **Myth:** You must pay to use the OPD in a government hospital.
   **Fact:** Government hospital OPDs are usually heavily subsidized or completely free for citizens.
8. **Myth:** Power of Attorney transfers ownership of the property forever.
   **Fact:** PoA only gives representation rights, not actual legal ownership, and can be revoked.
9. **Myth:** A credit score is permanently ruined if you miss one payment.
   **Fact:** It dips temporarily but can be rebuilt with consistent future payments.
10. **Myth:** English is the only legal language in Indian lower courts.
    **Fact:** Regional languages and Hindi are widely used and accepted in state and district courts.

## Conversation Examples

**Conversation 1: Hospital OPD**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mujhe doctor se milna hai, OPD kahan hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** OPD ka matlab Outpatient Department (बाह्य रोगी विभाग) hai, jahan aap bina admit hue doctor ko dikha sakte hain. Yeh ground floor par hai.
**Citizen (EN):** I need to see a doctor, where is the OPD?
**Assistant (EN):** OPD stands for Outpatient Department. You can consult a doctor there without being admitted. It is on the ground floor.

**Conversation 2: Legal Affidavit**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Lawyer ne mujhse 'Affidavit' maanga hai, yeh kya hota hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Affidavit ek shapath patra (शपथ पत्र) hota hai. Yeh ek legal document hai jisme aap likh kar kasam khate hain ki di gayi jankari sach hai.
**Citizen (HI):** वकील ने मुझसे 'शपथ पत्र' मांगा है, यह क्या होता है?
**Assistant (HI):** शपथ पत्र एक कानूनी दस्तावेज है जिसमें आप लिखकर शपथ लेते हैं कि दी गई जानकारी सत्य है।

**Conversation 3: Banking Fixed Deposit**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Main apne paise FD mein daalna chahta hu.
**Assistant (Hinglish):** FD yani Fixed Deposit (सावधि जमा). Isme aap apne paise ek fixed time ke liye bank mein rakhte hain aur ispar savings account se zyada byaj (interest) milta hai.
**Citizen (EN):** I want to put my money in an FD.
**Assistant (EN):** FD means Fixed Deposit. You keep your money locked for a specific period to earn a higher interest rate than a regular savings account.

**Conversation 4: Overdraft Facility**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Bank ne mujhe Overdraft offer kiya hai, kya yeh free hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Nahi, Overdraft ek short-term loan hota hai. Agar aapke account mein zero balance hai toh aap bank ke paise use kar sakte hain, par ispar interest lagta hai.
**Citizen (HI):** बैंक ने मुझे ओवरड्राफ्ट की पेशकश की है, क्या यह मुफ़्त है?
**Assistant (HI):** नहीं, ओवरड्राफ्ट एक अल्पकालिक ऋण है। यदि आपके खाते में शून्य शेष है तो आप बैंक के पैसे का उपयोग कर सकते हैं, लेकिन इस पर ब्याज लगता है।

**Conversation 5: Government Subsidy**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Cylinder par subsidy aayegi kya?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Subsidy yani aarthik sahayata (आर्थिक सहायता). Sarkar gas cylinder ke price ka kuch hissa aapke bank account mein wapas bhejti hai taaki aapko sasta pade.
**Citizen (EN):** Will I get a subsidy on the cylinder?
**Assistant (EN):** Subsidy means financial assistance. The government refunds a portion of the cost directly to your bank account to make it affordable.

**Conversation 6: Beneficiary**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Form mein Beneficiary ka naam pucha hai, kiska naam likhu?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Beneficiary ka matlab Labharthi (लाभार्थी) hota hai. Jisko is scheme ya policy ka fayda ya paisa milne wala hai, uska naam likhiye.

**Conversation 7: Credit Score**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Loan ke liye bank wale Credit Score kyu check kar rahe hain?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Credit Score aapka financial report card hota hai. Isse bank dekhta hai ki aapne pichle loans time par chukaye hain ya nahi.

**Conversation 8: Generic Medicine**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Doctor ne mujhe generic dawa di hai, kya yeh kharab hoti hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Bilkul nahi. Generic dawa (जेनेरिक दवा) bilkul branded dawa jaisi hi asardaar hoti hai, bas iska koi brand name nahi hota isliye yeh sasti hoti hai.

**Conversation 9: Bail vs Acquittal**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mere bhai ko bail mil gayi, kya case khatam ho gaya?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Nahi, Bail (ज़मानत) sirf temporary rihaai hai. Jab tak court use nirdosh (acquittal) saabit nahi karta, case chalega.

**Conversation 10: Plaintiff and Defendant**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Notice mein Vadi aur Prativadi likha hai, main kya hu?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Jisne case kiya hai wo Vadi (Plaintiff) hai, aur jispar case hua hai wo Prativadi (Defendant) hai.

**Conversation 11: Ordinance**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Sarkar ne naya Adhyadesh pass kiya hai, iska kya matlab hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Adhyadesh yani Ordinance. Yeh ek temporary kanoon hota hai jo emergency mein laya jata hai jab parliament session mein nahi hoti.

**Conversation 12: Collateral**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Bank loan ke liye collateral maang raha hai.
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Collateral ka matlab Girvi rakhi property (संपार्श्विक) hota hai. Aapko apne ghar ya gold ke papers bank ke paas security ke taur par rakhne honge.

**Conversation 13: Subpoena / Samman**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mujhe court se samman aaya hai, mujhe kya karna chahiye?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Samman (Subpoena) ek legal order hai. Iska matlab aapko di gayi tarikh par court mein hazir hona zaruri hai.

**Conversation 14: ICU**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Patient ko ICU kyu bheja gaya hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** ICU yani Intensive Care Unit (गहन चिकित्सा कक्ष). Yahan serious patients ko rakha jata hai taaki doctors unki 24 ghante continuously dekhbhal kar sakein.

**Conversation 15: Gazette**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Name change karne ke liye Gazette mein nikalna padega, yeh kya hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Gazette ek sarkari rajpatra (राजपत्र) hota hai. Yeh sarkar ka official newspaper hai jisme notices aur naam badalne jaisi jaankari chhapwani padti hai.
