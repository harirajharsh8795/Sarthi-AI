## Common Errors & Misconceptions
- **Error:** Confusing NEFT with RTGS. **Correction:** RTGS is for larger amounts (minimum 2 Lakhs) and is real-time; NEFT has no minimum limit but works in batches.
- **Error:** Assuming FIR and NCR are the same. **Correction:** FIR is for cognizable (serious) offenses where police can arrest without a warrant. NCR (Non-Cognizable Report) is for less serious offenses requiring a magistrate's permission to investigate.
- **Error:** Believing generic medicines are less effective than branded ones. **Correction:** Generic medicines have the same active ingredients, strength, and efficacy as branded counterparts but are usually significantly cheaper.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** A bank can close your account anytime without notice.
   **Fact:** Banks must provide adequate notice before closing an account, stating valid reasons unless the account is linked to severe fraudulent activities.
2. **Myth:** FIR must be filed at the police station where the crime happened.
   **Fact:** A Zero FIR can be filed at any police station, irrespective of jurisdiction, and it is later transferred to the correct police station.
3. **Myth:** Generic medicines are of lower quality than branded ones.
   **Fact:** Generic medicines meet the same rigorous quality, safety, and efficacy standards as branded medicines.
4. **Myth:** You cannot change your Aadhaar photo after 18 years of age.
   **Fact:** Biometrics including the photograph can be updated at any Aadhaar Seva Kendra at any age.
5. **Myth:** Writing a cheque with a future date means it cannot be cashed today.
   **Fact:** A post-dated cheque is valid, but the bank cannot honor it before the date written on it.
6. **Myth:** Traffic police can confiscate your original license for minor offenses.
   **Fact:** They can only seize the license under specific provisions of the Motor Vehicles Act; digital copies in DigiLocker are considered valid proof.
7. **Myth:** Government hospitals provide substandard care compared to private ones.
   **Fact:** Many government hospitals, like AIIMS, have world-class facilities, top doctors, and handle the most complex medical cases in the country.
8. **Myth:** Minimum balance charges apply to all bank accounts.
   **Fact:** Basic Savings Bank Deposit Accounts (BSBDA) and Jan Dhan accounts do not have minimum balance requirements.
9. **Myth:** RTI applications require a lawyer to draft.
   **Fact:** RTI applications can be written simply on plain paper by any citizen or filed online easily.
10. **Myth:** Paying someone through UPI is unsafe as money often gets lost.
    **Fact:** UPI is a highly secure, encrypted system. Failed transactions are usually auto-refunded by the bank within 3 to 5 working days.
