# Marriage Law in India (भारत में विवाह कानून)

## Overview
**English:** Marriage in India is governed by personal laws based on religion, as well as secular laws like the Special Marriage Act for interfaith or non-religious marriages. The law regulates the conditions for a valid marriage, registration, and the rights of the spouses.
**Hindi Unicode:** भारत में विवाह धर्म के आधार पर व्यक्तिगत कानूनों और धर्मनिरपेक्ष कानूनों (जैसे विशेष विवाह अधिनियम) द्वारा शासित होता है। कानून वैध विवाह, पंजीकरण और पति-पत्नी के अधिकारों को नियंत्रित करता है।
**Hinglish:** India mein marriage religion ke basis par personal laws aur interfaith marriages ke liye Special Marriage Act ke under aati hai. Kanoon valid marriage, registration aur spouses ke rights ko regulate karta hai.

## Quick Summary
**English:** A legal union between two individuals subject to conditions like age (18 for women, 21 for men), consent, and mental capacity. Registration is mandatory in most states to establish legal validity.
**Hindi Unicode:** दो व्यक्तियों के बीच एक कानूनी संघ, जिसमें उम्र (महिलाओं के लिए 18, पुरुषों के लिए 21), सहमति और मानसिक क्षमता जैसी शर्तें शामिल हैं। कानूनी मान्यता के लिए पंजीकरण अनिवार्य है।
**Hinglish:** Do logon ke beech ek legal union jisme age, consent aur mental capacity dekhi jati hai. Legal validity ke liye zyada tar states mein registration mandatory hai.

## Definition
**English:** Marriage is a legally recognized union between individuals establishing rights and obligations between them, their children, and their in-laws, governed by specific personal or secular statutes in India.
**Hindi Unicode:** विवाह व्यक्तियों के बीच एक कानूनी रूप से मान्यता प्राप्त संघ है जो उनके, उनके बच्चों और उनके ससुराल वालों के बीच अधिकारों और दायित्वों को स्थापित करता है, जिसे भारत में विशिष्ट व्यक्तिगत या धर्मनिरपेक्ष कानूनों द्वारा शासित किया जाता है।
**Hinglish:** Marriage ek legally recognized union hai jo husband, wife, bacchon aur in-laws ke beech rights aur duties establish karti hai, jise India ke specific laws govern karte hain.

## Why It Matters
**English:** It confers legal rights regarding inheritance, maintenance, and child custody. It also ensures social security and legal protection against domestic exploitation.
**Hindi Unicode:** यह विरासत, भरण-पोषण और बच्चे की कस्टडी के संबंध में कानूनी अधिकार प्रदान करता है। यह घरेलू शोषण के खिलाफ सामाजिक सुरक्षा और कानूनी सुरक्षा भी सुनिश्चित करता है।
**Hinglish:** Ye inheritance, maintenance, aur child custody ke legal rights deta hai. Ye domestic abuse ke khilaf social aur legal protection bhi ensure karta hai.

## Legal Background
**History & Purpose:** To regulate family structures, prevent child marriage, and protect women's rights in matrimonial homes.
**Applicable Acts:**
- Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 (HMA)
- Special Marriage Act, 1954 (SMA)
- Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application Act, 1937
- Indian Christian Marriage Act, 1872
- Anand Marriage Act, 1909 (for Sikhs)
- Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006
**Relevant Constitutional Articles:** Article 21 (Right to Life and Personal Liberty - includes the right to marry a person of choice).
**Relevant Sections:** Section 5 HMA (Conditions for a Hindu marriage), Section 8 HMA (Registration), Section 4 SMA (Conditions), Section 16 SMA (Registration of marriage celebrated in other forms).

## Applicability
**Who is covered:** All citizens of India based on their respective religions, or under the Special Marriage Act regardless of religion.
**Who is exempt:** Foreigners can marry in India under the Special Marriage Act or Foreign Marriage Act.
**Special conditions:** Both parties must give free consent, be of legal age, not have a living spouse (except under Muslim law for men, up to four), and not be within prohibited degrees of relationship.

## Rights of Citizens
**English:** Right to reside in the matrimonial home, right to maintenance, right to mutual consortium, right to seek divorce on legal grounds.
**Hindi Unicode:** वैवाहिक घर में रहने का अधिकार, भरण-पोषण का अधिकार, आपसी साहचर्य का अधिकार, कानूनी आधार पर तलाक लेने का अधिकार।
**Hinglish:** Matrimonial ghar mein rehne ka right, maintenance ka right, mutual consortium, aur legal grounds par divorce lene ka right.

## Duties & Responsibilities
**English:** Mutual financial and emotional support, conjugal duties, joint responsibility for children's upbringing, and respect for each other's legal rights.
**Hindi Unicode:** पारस्परिक वित्तीय और भावनात्मक समर्थन, वैवाहिक कर्तव्य, बच्चों की परवरिश के लिए संयुक्त जिम्मेदारी, और एक-दूसरे के कानूनी अधिकारों का सम्मान।
**Hinglish:** Mutual financial aur emotional support, bacchon ki upbringing ki joint responsibility, aur ek-dusre ke legal rights ki respect karna.

## Step-by-Step Legal Procedure
**English:**
1. solemnization of marriage as per religious customs or under the Special Marriage Act.
2. Applying for registration at the Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM) or Marriage Registrar's office.
3. Submitting required documents (age, address proof, photos).
4. Physical presence of both parties and witnesses.
5. Verification and issuance of the Marriage Certificate.
**Hindi Unicode:**
1. धार्मिक रीति-रिवाजों या विशेष विवाह अधिनियम के तहत विवाह का संपन्न होना।
2. उप-विभागीय मजिस्ट्रेट (एसडीएम) या विवाह रजिस्ट्रार के कार्यालय में पंजीकरण के लिए आवेदन करना।
3. आवश्यक दस्तावेज जमा करना (उम्र, पता प्रमाण, तस्वीरें)।
4. दोनों पक्षों और गवाहों की भौतिक उपस्थिति।
5. सत्यापन और विवाह प्रमाण पत्र जारी करना।
**Hinglish:**
1. Religious customs ya Special Marriage Act ke under shadi karna.
2. SDM ya Marriage Registrar office mein registration apply karna.
3. Required documents (age, address proof, photos) submit karna.
4. Dono parties aur witnesses ka present hona.
5. Verification ke baad Marriage Certificate issue hona.

## Required Documents
- Passport-sized photographs of the bride and groom.
- Proof of age (Birth certificate, 10th mark sheet, Passport).
- Proof of residence (Aadhar, Voter ID, Passport).
- Affidavit stating marital status, age, and mutual consent.
- Wedding invitation card (if applicable).
- Photographs of the marriage ceremony.
- ID proofs of 3 witnesses.

## Government Authorities
- Registrar of Marriages / Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM).
- Family Courts (for disputes).
- Lok Adalat (for amicable settlements).
- Women's Cell / Police (for domestic violence or dowry harassment).

## Penalties & Punishments
- Child Marriage: Rigorous imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine up to ₹1 Lakh under the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act.
- Bigamy (marrying again while spouse is living): Imprisonment up to 7 years and fine under Section 82 of Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) [formerly Sec 494 IPC], applicable to non-Muslims.
- Dowry demands: Imprisonment of not less than 5 years and fine under the Dowry Prohibition Act.

## Exceptions
- Customary laws of certain Scheduled Tribes may override statutory laws if specifically exempted.
- Prohibited degrees of relationship may have exceptions if customary laws allow such marriages (e.g., in some Southern Indian communities).

## Important Timelines
- Special Marriage Act Notice: 30 days mandatory public notice period before solemnization.
- Registration Application: Generally within 30 to 60 days of the wedding, varying by state rules (late fees apply thereafter).
- Restitution of Conjugal Rights: Can be filed if a spouse withdraws from society without reasonable excuse.

## Common Legal Mistakes
- Not registering the marriage.
- Ignoring the 30-day notice period under SMA.
- Using fake age documents.
- Marrying while a previous divorce is still pending or not finalized.

## Safety Tips
**English:** Always register your marriage. Verify the legal age of your partner. Do not pay or accept dowry. Keep multiple certified copies of the marriage certificate.
**Hindi Unicode:** हमेशा अपने विवाह का पंजीकरण कराएं। अपने साथी की कानूनी उम्र सत्यापित करें। दहेज न दें और न लें। विवाह प्रमाण पत्र की कई प्रमाणित प्रतियां रखें।
**Hinglish:** Hamesha apni marriage register karayein. Partner ki legal age verify karein. Dowry na dein na lein. Marriage certificate ki multiple copies sambhal kar rakhein.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Q:** Is marriage registration mandatory in India?
**A:** Yes, the Supreme Court of India has made marriage registration mandatory. It helps in preventing child marriages and provides legal protection to women. **Hindi:** हाँ, भारत के सर्वोच्च न्यायालय ने विवाह पंजीकरण अनिवार्य कर दिया है। **Hinglish:** Haan, Supreme Court ne registration mandatory kar diya hai, ye legal protection deta hai.

2. **Q:** What is the legal age for marriage in India?
**A:** The legal age is 18 years for females and 21 years for males. Marrying below this age is considered child marriage. **Hindi:** महिलाओं के लिए 18 वर्ष और पुरुषों के लिए 21 वर्ष है। **Hinglish:** Ladkiyon ke liye 18 saal aur ladkon ke liye 21 saal hai.

3. **Q:** Can an Indian marry a foreigner in India?
**A:** Yes, an Indian can marry a foreigner under the Special Marriage Act, 1954. A 30-day notice is required. **Hindi:** हाँ, विशेष विवाह अधिनियम के तहत एक भारतीय किसी विदेशी से शादी कर सकता है। **Hinglish:** Haan, Special Marriage Act ke under Indian aur foreigner shadi kar sakte hain.

4. **Q:** What is a court marriage?
**A:** A court marriage is solemnized under the Special Marriage Act without religious ceremonies in the presence of a Marriage Officer. **Hindi:** कोर्ट मैरिज विशेष विवाह अधिनियम के तहत एक विवाह अधिकारी की उपस्थिति में संपन्न होती है। **Hinglish:** Court marriage Special Marriage Act ke under Marriage Officer ke samne hoti hai, bina religious ceremony ke.

5. **Q:** How many witnesses are needed for a court marriage?
**A:** Three witnesses are required for a court marriage under the Special Marriage Act. **Hindi:** विशेष विवाह अधिनियम के तहत कोर्ट मैरिज के लिए तीन गवाहों की आवश्यकता होती है। **Hinglish:** Court marriage mein 3 witnesses ki zarurat hoti hai.

6. **Q:** Can Arya Samaj marriages be registered?
**A:** Yes, Arya Samaj marriages are recognized under the Hindu Marriage Act and must be registered with the local registrar. **Hindi:** हाँ, आर्य समाज के विवाह हिंदू विवाह अधिनियम के तहत मान्यता प्राप्त हैं और पंजीकृत होने चाहिए। **Hinglish:** Haan, Arya Samaj marriage Hindu Marriage Act ke under aati hai aur register ho sakti hai.

7. **Q:** What is the 30-day notice period in SMA?
**A:** It is a mandatory public notice period intended to invite objections regarding the legality of the intended marriage. **Hindi:** यह एक अनिवार्य सार्वजनिक नोटिस अवधि है जिसका उद्देश्य विवाह की वैधता के संबंध में आपत्तियां आमंत्रित करना है। **Hinglish:** Ye 30 din ka notice period objections ke liye hota hai ki shadi valid hai ya nahi.

8. **Q:** Is Aadhar card mandatory for marriage registration?
**A:** While Aadhar is widely used as proof of identity and address, other valid ID proofs like Voter ID or Passport are also acceptable. **Hindi:** आधार पहचान के प्रमाण के रूप में व्यापक रूप से उपयोग किया जाता है, लेकिन अन्य वैध आईडी भी स्वीकार्य हैं। **Hinglish:** Aadhar ek main ID hai, par Voter ID ya Passport bhi valid hain.

9. **Q:** What is restitution of conjugal rights?
**A:** It is a legal remedy available to a spouse if the other spouse has withdrawn from their society without a reasonable excuse. **Hindi:** यह एक कानूनी उपाय है यदि दूसरा जीवनसाथी बिना किसी उचित कारण के समाज से अलग हो गया हो। **Hinglish:** Ye ek legal remedy hai jab ek spouse bina kisi valid reason ke alag rehne lage.

10. **Q:** Can cousins marry in India?
**A:** Generally, marrying within prohibited degrees (like first cousins) is illegal under Hindu Law, unless customary law permits it. Muslim law permits cousin marriages. **Hindi:** हिंदू कानून के तहत वर्जित डिग्री के भीतर शादी करना अवैध है, जब तक कि प्रथागत कानून इसकी अनुमति न दे। **Hinglish:** Hindu law mein generally cousins ki shadi illegal hai unless custom allow kare, par Muslim law mein allowed hai.

11. **Q:** What happens if I don't register my marriage?
**A:** Non-registration can result in a fine. More importantly, you will face difficulties in obtaining visas, claiming life insurance, or establishing legal rights. **Hindi:** पंजीकरण न करने पर जुर्माना हो सकता है और वीजा आदि प्राप्त करने में कठिनाई हो सकती है। **Hinglish:** Registration na hone par fine lag sakta hai aur visa ya insurance claims mein problem aati hai.

12. **Q:** How much does marriage registration cost?
**A:** Government fees are nominal, usually between ₹100 to ₹500, though lawyer fees or agent fees will vary. **Hindi:** सरकारी शुल्क मामूली है, आमतौर पर ₹100 से ₹500 के बीच। **Hinglish:** Government fees bahut kam hoti hai, ₹100 se ₹500 tak.

13. **Q:** Do I need a lawyer to register a marriage?
**A:** No, you can apply directly at the registrar's office. However, a lawyer can assist with drafting affidavits and ensuring paperwork is correct. **Hindi:** नहीं, आप सीधे रजिस्ट्रार कार्यालय में आवेदन कर सकते हैं। **Hinglish:** Lawyer mandatory nahi hai, aap direct bhi apply kar sakte hain, par lawyer help kar sakta hai.

14. **Q:** Can a runaway couple get police protection?
**A:** Yes, adults marrying of their own free will can seek police protection from the High Court if they face threats from their families. **Hindi:** हाँ, अपनी मर्जी से शादी करने वाले वयस्क उच्च न्यायालय से पुलिस सुरक्षा मांग सकते हैं। **Hinglish:** Haan, adults jo apni marzi se shadi karte hain, wo High Court se police protection le sakte hain.

15. **Q:** Is bigamy a crime in India?
**A:** Yes, for Hindus, Christians, and Parsis, marrying again while the first spouse is alive and the marriage is not dissolved is a crime under BNS Sec 82. **Hindi:** हाँ, पहली पत्नी के जीवित रहते हुए और विवाह भंग हुए बिना दूसरी शादी करना अपराध है। **Hinglish:** Haan, pehli shadi ke hote hue dusri shadi karna crime hai (except for Muslim men).

16. **Q:** What is the Anand Marriage Act?
**A:** The Anand Marriage Act regulates and registers the marriages of Sikhs. It was amended to allow Sikhs to register under their own act instead of the Hindu Marriage Act. **Hindi:** आनंद विवाह अधिनियम सिखों के विवाह को नियंत्रित और पंजीकृत करता है। **Hinglish:** Ye act Sikhs ki marriages ko regulate aur register karne ke liye hai.

17. **Q:** Can a marriage be registered online?
**A:** Many states offer an online application process, but both parties must physically visit the registrar's office to sign the register and be verified. **Hindi:** कई राज्य ऑनलाइन आवेदन प्रक्रिया प्रदान करते हैं, लेकिन हस्ताक्षर के लिए कार्यालय जाना आवश्यक है। **Hinglish:** Online apply kar sakte hain, par sign karne ke liye office jana padta hai.

18. **Q:** Are dowry demands illegal?
**A:** Yes, demanding, giving, or taking dowry is a criminal offense under the Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961. **Hindi:** हाँ, दहेज मांगना, देना या लेना दहेज निषेध अधिनियम, 1961 के तहत एक आपराधिक अपराध है। **Hinglish:** Haan, dowry maangna, dena ya lena strict crime hai Dowry Prohibition Act ke under.

19. **Q:** What if the groom is 20 and bride is 18?
**A:** It is illegal. The groom must be 21 years old. A marriage where the boy is under 21 is a child marriage and is punishable by law. **Hindi:** यह अवैध है। दूल्हे की उम्र 21 साल होनी चाहिए। **Hinglish:** Ye illegal hai. Ladke ki age 21 saal honi zaruri hai.

20. **Q:** Can I change my name after marriage?
**A:** Yes, a woman or a man can change their name after marriage. The marriage certificate acts as valid proof for updating names on Aadhar, Passport, and PAN card. **Hindi:** हाँ, विवाह के बाद नाम बदला जा सकता है। विवाह प्रमाण पत्र इसके लिए वैध प्रमाण है। **Hinglish:** Haan, shadi ke baad name change kar sakte hain, marriage certificate valid proof hota hai.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** Registration is not required if the marriage is performed in a temple.
**Fact:** Temple marriages must still be registered with the local registrar to be legally recognized by state authorities for official purposes.
2. **Myth:** Court marriage takes only one day.
**Fact:** Under SMA, a 30-day notice period is mandatory before the marriage can be solemnized.
3. **Myth:** Parents' consent is required for court marriage.
**Fact:** If both partners are of legal age (18 for girls, 21 for boys), they do not need parental consent.
4. **Myth:** Only the husband can claim maintenance.
**Fact:** Under HMA Section 24, both husband and wife can claim interim maintenance depending on their financial capabilities.
5. **Myth:** A Hindu man can have two wives if the first wife agrees.
**Fact:** No, bigamy is illegal for Hindus regardless of the first wife's consent. The second marriage is void.
6. **Myth:** Changing surname is legally mandatory for wives.
**Fact:** There is no legal obligation for a woman to change her surname after marriage.
7. **Myth:** Marriages cannot be registered after 1 year.
**Fact:** You can register a marriage anytime, but late registration might incur nominal late fees.
8. **Myth:** Live-in relationships are illegal in India.
**Fact:** Live-in relationships are legal in India, and partners/children have certain rights under the Domestic Violence Act.
9. **Myth:** You cannot marry someone outside your caste.
**Fact:** Caste does not restrict marriage. Inter-caste marriages are perfectly legal and even promoted by some government schemes.
10. **Myth:** Dowry gifts are legal if given "willingly."
**Fact:** The Dowry Prohibition Act bans any property or valuable security given directly or indirectly in connection with marriage.

**Assistant:** You will need age proof (like birth certificate or 10th marksheet), address proof (Aadhar/Voter ID), passport size photos, and 3 witnesses with their IDs.

**Assistant:** Nahi, Bharat me ladke ki legal marriage age 21 saal hai aur ladki ki 18 saal. Aapko boyfriend ki age 21 hone tak wait karna hoga.

4. **Citizen:** How long does it take to register a marriage under the Special Marriage Act?
**Assistant:** Under the Special Marriage Act, a mandatory 30-day public notice period is required. So, it takes at least 30 days from the date of application.

5. **Citizen:** Kya shadi ke baad surname change karna zaruri hai?
**Assistant:** Nahi, kanoon ke mutabiq shadi ke baad surname change karna mandatory nahi hai. Ye poori tarah se aapki choice hai.

6. **Citizen:** Humara inter-faith marriage hai, kya hum Hindu Marriage Act ke under shadi kar sakte hain?
**Assistant:** Nahi, agar dono parties Hindu, Buddhist, Jain ya Sikh nahi hain, toh aapko Special Marriage Act ke under shadi register karni hogi.

7. **Citizen:** Can I apply for a marriage certificate online?
**Assistant:** Yes, many state governments offer online applications on their e-District portals. However, you will still need to visit the registrar's office once for verification and signatures.

8. **Citizen:** Mere husband ne bina divorce diye dusri shadi kar li hai. Mujhe kya karna chahiye?
**Assistant:** Ye kanoonan jurm hai (Bigamy). Aap Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) ke Section 82 ke tahat police me complain darj kara sakti hain aur Family Court me case file kar sakti hain.

9. **Citizen:** We ran away to get married and our family is threatening us.
**Assistant:** If you both are of legal age, you can approach the High Court through a lawyer to seek police protection. Your life and liberty are protected under Article 21 of the Constitution.

10. **Citizen:** Is an Arya Samaj certificate enough for a visa?
**Assistant:** No, an Arya Samaj certificate is not sufficient for international travel or visa purposes. You must register the marriage with the local municipal or government registrar to get a valid government Marriage Certificate.

11. **Citizen:** Mujhe shaadi ka certificate nikalna hai, fees kitni lagegi?
**Assistant:** Government fees aam taur par ₹100 se ₹500 ke beech hoti hai, state ke rules ke hisaab se. Late registration par extra fine lag sakta hai.

12. **Citizen:** Kya court marriage me lawyer zaruri hai?
**Assistant:** Kanoonan lawyer mandatory nahi hai. Aap khud documents SDM office me de sakte hain. Par forms aur affidavits theek se bharne ke liye log lawyer ki madad lete hain.

13. **Citizen:** What happens if someone objects during the 30-day notice period?
**Assistant:** The Marriage Officer will investigate the objection within 30 days. If the objection is legally valid (e.g., underage, existing spouse), the marriage will not proceed.

14. **Citizen:** Kya NRI se shadi karne par koi alag rule hai?
**Assistant:** Haan, NRI se shadi karne par aapko unka passport, visa, aur marital status ka affidavit (single status certificate) chahiye hota hai. Registration zaroor karwayein for safety.

15. **Citizen:** My in-laws are demanding a car as a "gift". Is this legal?
