# Rent and Tenancy Laws in India: Complete Guide

## Overview
**English:** Rent and tenancy laws in India govern the relationship between landlords (property owners) and tenants (renters). These laws aim to balance the rights of property owners to receive fair returns and repossess their properties, with the rights of tenants against arbitrary eviction and unfair rent hikes. The legal framework relies heavily on state-specific Rent Control Acts, the Transfer of Property Act, and recently, the Model Tenancy Act, 2021.
**Hindi Unicode:** भारत में किराया और किरायेदारी कानून मकान मालिकों (संपत्ति के मालिकों) और किरायेदारों (किराये पर रहने वालों) के बीच संबंधों को नियंत्रित करते हैं। इन कानूनों का उद्देश्य संपत्ति के मालिकों को उचित रिटर्न प्राप्त करने और अपनी संपत्तियों पर फिर से कब्ज़ा करने के अधिकारों के साथ-साथ मनमाने निष्कासन और अनुचित किराया वृद्धि के खिलाफ किरायेदारों के अधिकारों को संतुलित करना है।
**Hinglish:** India mein rent aur tenancy laws landlords aur tenants ke beech ke relation ko govern karte hain. In laws ka main aim hai landlord ke fair returns aur property repossession ke rights ko protect karna, aur saath hi tenant ko arbitrary eviction aur unfair rent hike se bachana. Ye state-specific Rent Control Acts aur Model Tenancy Act, 2021 par based hai.

## Quick Summary
**English:** A rent agreement is a legally binding contract defining terms of tenancy, including rent amount, duration, and maintenance responsibilities. Registration is mandatory for agreements exceeding 11 months. The Model Tenancy Act establishes Rent Authorities to quickly resolve disputes.
**Hindi Unicode:** रेंट एग्रीमेंट एक कानूनी रूप से बाध्यकारी अनुबंध है जो किरायेदारी की शर्तों को परिभाषित करता है, जिसमें किराये की राशि, अवधि और रखरखाव की जिम्मेदारियां शामिल हैं। 11 महीने से अधिक के समझौतों के लिए पंजीकरण अनिवार्य है।
**Hinglish:** Rent agreement ek legally binding contract hai jo rent amount, duration aur maintenance responsibilities define karta hai. 11 mahine se zyada ke agreements ka registration mandatory hai. Model Tenancy Act rent disputes ko jaldi resolve karne ke liye Rent Authorities banata hai.

## Definition
**English:** Rent is the periodic payment made by a tenant to a landlord for the right to use and occupy a premises. Tenancy is the legal state of holding this property under a lease or rent agreement.
**Hindi Unicode:** किराया एक किरायेदार द्वारा किसी परिसर का उपयोग करने और उस पर कब्जा करने के अधिकार के लिए मकान मालिक को किया जाने वाला आवधिक भुगतान है। किरायेदारी एक पट्टे या किराये के समझौते के तहत इस संपत्ति को धारण करने की कानूनी स्थिति है।
**Hinglish:** Rent wo regular payment hai jo tenant landlord ko property use karne ke liye deta hai. Tenancy us property ko lease ya rent agreement ke under hold karne ka legal status hai.

## Why It Matters
**English:** Clear tenancy laws prevent property disputes, protect tenants from sudden homelessness, and assure landlords that they can safely rent out their properties without fear of illegal squatting. It formalizes the real estate rental market, ensuring economic stability.
**Hindi Unicode:** स्पष्ट किरायेदारी कानून संपत्ति विवादों को रोकते हैं, किरायेदारों को अचानक बेघर होने से बचाते हैं, और मकान मालिकों को आश्वस्त करते हैं कि वे अवैध कब्जे के डर के बिना अपनी संपत्तियों को सुरक्षित रूप से किराए पर दे सकते हैं। यह रियल एस्टेट किराये के बाजार को औपचारिक बनाता है।
**Hinglish:** Clear tenancy laws property disputes ko rokte hain, tenants ko achanak beghar hone se bachate hain, aur landlords ko assurance dete hain ki wo bina illegal occupation ke darr ke property rent par de sakte hain. Ye rental market ko formalize karta hai.

## Legal Background
**History & Purpose:** Historically, Rent Control Acts were enacted post-World War II to prevent exploitation of tenants when housing was scarce. However, these laws became archaic, freezing rents at abnormally low levels and discouraging landlords from renting out properties. The Model Tenancy Act 2021 was introduced to overhaul this system and create a transparent, market-driven rental ecosystem.
**Applicable Acts:** 
- Transfer of Property Act, 1882 (TPA)
- Indian Contract Act, 1872
- Registration Act, 1908
- The Model Tenancy Act, 2021 (adopted by various states)
- State-specific Rent Control Acts (e.g., Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958; Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999)
**Relevant Constitutional Articles:** Article 21 (Right to Life and Personal Liberty - interpreted to include the right to shelter). Housing is a State subject under the State List of the Seventh Schedule.
**Relevant Sections:**
- Section 105 to 111 of TPA: Defines lease, rights and liabilities of lessor and lessee, and determination of lease.
- Section 17 of Registration Act: Makes registration of lease agreements exceeding one year mandatory.
- Model Tenancy Act Sections: Sec 4 (Tenancy Agreement), Sec 11 (Security Deposit - capped at 2 months for residential), Sec 21 & 22 (Eviction conditions).

## Applicability
**Who is covered:** All residential and commercial tenants and landlords operating under a written agreement.
**Who is exempt:** Government-owned properties, premises owned by religious or charitable institutions (in most states), properties rented out to industrial units (under specific state exemptions), and hotels/lodging houses.
**Special Conditions:** The Model Tenancy Act operates prospectively; it does not retrospectively alter existing rent agreements unless they are renewed under the new Act.

## Rights of Citizens
**English:** Tenants have the right to a written agreement, peaceful possession, rent receipts, essential services (water/electricity cannot be cut off), and protection from arbitrary eviction. Landlords have the right to receive timely rent, periodic inspection with prior notice, and eviction for valid reasons (e.g., non-payment, subletting without consent).
**Hindi Unicode:** किरायेदारों को एक लिखित समझौते, शांतिपूर्ण कब्जे, किराए की रसीदें, आवश्यक सेवाओं (पानी / बिजली काटी नहीं जा सकती है), और मनमाने ढंग से बेदखली से सुरक्षा का अधिकार है। मकान मालिकों को समय पर किराया प्राप्त करने, पूर्व सूचना के साथ आवधिक निरीक्षण, और वैध कारणों से बेदखली का अधिकार है।
**Hinglish:** Tenants ke paas written agreement, peaceful possession, rent receipts, essential services (water/light nahi kaat sakte), aur arbitrary eviction se protection ka right hai. Landlords ke paas timely rent, prior notice ke saath inspection, aur valid reasons par eviction ka right hai.

## Duties & Responsibilities
**English:** Tenants must pay rent/utility bills on time, maintain the property reasonably, and avoid illegal activities or unapproved structural changes. Landlords must hand over the property in good condition, undertake major structural repairs, and refund the security deposit upon vacating.
**Hindi Unicode:** किरायेदारों को समय पर किराए/उपयोगिता बिलों का भुगतान करना चाहिए, संपत्ति को उचित रूप से बनाए रखना चाहिए, और अवैध गतिविधियों या अस्वीकृत संरचनात्मक परिवर्तनों से बचना चाहिए। मकान मालिकों को संपत्ति अच्छी स्थिति में सौंपनी चाहिए, प्रमुख संरचनात्मक मरम्मत करनी चाहिए, और खाली करने पर सुरक्षा जमा वापस करनी चाहिए।
**Hinglish:** Tenants ko rent/utility bills time par pay karna chahiye, property maintain karni chahiye, aur illegal activities ya bina permission structural changes nahi karne chahiye. Landlords ko property good condition mein deni chahiye, major repairs karwane chahiye, aur vacate karne par security deposit refund karna chahiye.

## Step-by-Step Legal Procedure
**English:** 
1. Negotiation: Landlord and tenant agree on rent, tenure, and deposit.
2. Drafting: A rental agreement is drafted on e-stamp paper covering all clauses (rent, notice period, maintenance).
3. Notarization/Registration: Agreements up to 11 months can be notarized. Above 11 months must be registered at the Sub-Registrar's office.
4. Intimation to Rent Authority: Under MTA, both parties must jointly inform the Rent Authority within 2 months of signing.
5. Eviction: If terms are violated, landlord issues a legal notice. If the tenant doesn't vacate, landlord files a case with the Rent Court.
**Hindi Unicode:** 
1. बातचीत: मकान मालिक और किरायेदार किराए, कार्यकाल और जमा राशि पर सहमत होते हैं।
2. प्रारूपण: सभी खंडों (किराया, नोटिस अवधि, रखरखाव) को कवर करते हुए ई-स्टाम्प पेपर पर एक रेंटल एग्रीमेंट तैयार किया जाता है।
3. नोटरीकरण/पंजीकरण: 11 महीने तक के समझौतों को नोटरीकृत किया जा सकता है। 11 महीने से ऊपर उप-पंजीयक कार्यालय में पंजीकृत होना चाहिए।
4. किराया प्राधिकरण को सूचना: एमटीए के तहत, दोनों पक्षों को हस्ताक्षर करने के 2 महीने के भीतर संयुक्त रूप से किराया प्राधिकरण को सूचित करना होगा।
5. बेदखली: यदि शर्तों का उल्लंघन किया जाता है, तो मकान मालिक कानूनी नोटिस जारी करता है।
**Hinglish:** 
1. Negotiation: Landlord aur tenant rent, tenure, aur deposit par agree karte hain.
2. Drafting: E-stamp paper par rental agreement draft hota hai jisme sabhi clauses hote hain.
3. Notarization/Registration: 11 months tak notarize karwa sakte hain. 11 months se upar Sub-Registrar office mein register karna mandatory hai.
4. Intimation: MTA ke under, 2 months mein Rent Authority ko inform karna hota hai.
5. Eviction: Rules tootne par legal notice issue hota hai, phir Rent Court mein case file hota hai.

## Required Documents
- Proof of Identity of Landlord and Tenant (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport).
- Proof of Ownership of the Landlord (Sale Deed, Utility Bills, Tax Receipt).
- Passport size photographs of both parties and two witnesses.
- E-Stamp paper of appropriate value (varies by state).
- Police Verification form (mandatory in cities like Delhi, Mumbai).

## Government Authorities
- Rent Authority (Appointed under Model Tenancy Act / State Acts).
- Rent Court & Rent Tribunal (For dedicated dispute resolution and appeals).
- Sub-Registrar of Assurances (For agreement registration).
- Local Police Station (For tenant verification).

## Penalties & Punishments
- Overstaying Tenant (Under MTA): Liable to pay twice the monthly rent for the first two months, and four times the monthly rent thereafter.
- Withholding Essential Services: If a landlord cuts water/electricity, the Rent Authority can impose a penalty and order immediate restoration.
- Unregistered 11+ month Agreement: Cannot be presented as evidence in a court of law; heavily penalized with stamp duty arrears up to 10 times the original duty.
- Refusal to refund Security Deposit: Landlord can be ordered by Rent Court to refund the amount with interest.

## Exceptions
- Leave and License Agreements (common in Maharashtra) operate under the Indian Easements Act rather than Rent Control Acts, giving landlords greater leverage to evict.
- Properties leased to banks, multinational companies, or foreign missions are usually exempt from standard rent control protections and are governed purely by the signed contract.

## Important Timelines
- Notice Period for Eviction: Generally 30 to 60 days, as specified in the agreement.
- Security Deposit Refund: Within 1 month of vacating the premises (under MTA).
- Appeal to Rent Tribunal: Must be filed within 30 days of the Rent Court's order.
- Agreement Registration: Within 4 months from the date of execution.

## Common Legal Mistakes
- Relying entirely on verbal agreements without any written documentation.
- Using 11-month agreements continuously for years without registering them.
- Landlords taking the law into their own hands (e.g., throwing out tenant's belongings, cutting power) instead of following due legal eviction processes.
- Tenants withholding rent completely because the landlord refused to do a minor repair.
- Skipping mandatory Police Verification, leading to potential criminal liability for the landlord.

## Safety Tips
**English:** Always sign a written agreement. Never pay in cash without taking a receipt. Landlords must always conduct police verification of tenants. Tenants should thoroughly inspect the property and document existing damages before moving in.
**Hindi Unicode:** हमेशा एक लिखित समझौते पर हस्ताक्षर करें। रसीद लिए बिना कभी भी नकद में भुगतान न करें। मकान मालिकों को हमेशा किरायेदारों का पुलिस सत्यापन कराना चाहिए।
**Hinglish:** Hamesha written agreement sign karein. Bina receipt ke cash payment kabhi na karein. Landlords ko hamesha tenant ki police verification karwani chahiye. Tenants move in karne se pehle property inspect karein aur existing damages document karein.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Can a landlord increase the rent suddenly during the 11-month tenure?**
**English:** No, the landlord cannot increase the rent midway unless there is a specific clause in the rent agreement allowing it. The rent is fixed for the tenure of the agreement. Any hike must be mutually agreed upon or done at the time of renewal.
**Hindi Unicode:** नहीं, मकान मालिक बीच में किराया नहीं बढ़ा सकता जब तक कि रेंट एग्रीमेंट में इसकी विशेष अनुमति देने वाला कोई खंड न हो। समझौते के कार्यकाल के लिए किराया तय किया जाता है।
**Hinglish:** Nahi, landlord beech mein rent nahi badha sakta jab tak agreement mein specific clause na ho. Rent tenure ke liye fixed hota hai aur hike renewal par hi hota hai.

2. **Is it mandatory to make a rent agreement for exactly 11 months?**
**English:** No, it is not mandatory to make it for 11 months. The 11-month duration is commonly used to avoid the strict registration requirements and stamp duty associated with leases of 12 months or more under the Registration Act.
**Hindi Unicode:** नहीं, इसे 11 महीने का बनाना अनिवार्य नहीं है। पंजीकरण अधिनियम के तहत 12 महीने या उससे अधिक के पट्टों से जुड़ी सख्त पंजीकरण आवश्यकताओं और स्टाम्प ड्यूटी से बचने के लिए 11 महीने की अवधि का उपयोग किया जाता है।
**Hinglish:** Nahi, 11 months ka banana mandatory nahi hai. Ye duration isliye use hota hai taaki Registration Act ke under 12 months ya usse zyada ke leases par lagne wali stamp duty aur strict registration rules se bacha ja sake.

3. **What happens if a tenant refuses to vacate after the agreement expires?**
**English:** If a tenant overstays, the landlord must issue a legal notice. Under the Model Tenancy Act, an overstaying tenant is liable to pay double the rent for the first two months and four times thereafter. The landlord can file an eviction suit in the Rent Court.
**Hindi Unicode:** यदि कोई किरायेदार अधिक समय तक रहता है, तो मकान मालिक को कानूनी नोटिस जारी करना होगा। मॉडल टीनेंसी एक्ट के तहत, ओवरस्टे करने वाला किरायेदार पहले दो महीनों के लिए दोगुना और उसके बाद चार गुना किराया देने के लिए उत्तरदायी है।
**Hinglish:** Agar tenant vacate nahi karta, toh landlord ko legal notice dena hoga. MTA ke under overstaying tenant ko pehle do mahine double rent aur phir char guna rent dena padta hai. Landlord Rent Court mein case file kar sakta hai.

4. **Can my landlord cut my electricity and water if I default on rent?**
**English:** No, essential services like water and electricity cannot be disconnected by the landlord, even if you default on rent. This is a basic human right. If disconnected, you can immediately approach the Rent Authority or police to restore them.
**Hindi Unicode:** नहीं, मकान मालिक पानी और बिजली जैसी आवश्यक सेवाएं नहीं काट सकता है, भले ही आप किराए का भुगतान न करें। यह एक बुनियादी मानवाधिकार है।
**Hinglish:** Nahi, rent default karne par bhi landlord water aur electricity jaisi essential services cut nahi kar sakta. Ye basic human right hai. Aisa hone par aap Rent Authority ya police ke paas ja sakte hain.

5. **Is police verification mandatory for tenants?**
**English:** Yes, in most major cities and states (like Delhi, UP, Maharashtra), it is mandatory under Section 188 of the IPC to submit tenant details to the local police. Failure to do so can result in imprisonment or a fine for the landlord.
**Hindi Unicode:** हां, अधिकांश प्रमुख शहरों और राज्यों में किरायेदारों का विवरण स्थानीय पुलिस को सौंपना अनिवार्य है। ऐसा न करने पर मकान मालिक को जेल या जुर्माना हो सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Haan, most major cities mein tenant details local police ko dena mandatory hai. Aisa na karne par landlord ko IPC Sec 188 ke under fine ya jail ho sakti hai.

6. **How much security deposit can a landlord legally demand?**
**English:** Under the Model Tenancy Act, a landlord can demand a maximum of two months' rent as a security deposit for residential properties, and a maximum of six months' rent for non-residential (commercial) properties.
**Hindi Unicode:** मॉडल किरायेदारी अधिनियम के तहत, एक मकान मालिक आवासीय संपत्तियों के लिए अधिकतम दो महीने के किराए और गैर-आवासीय संपत्तियों के लिए छह महीने के किराए की मांग कर सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Model Tenancy Act ke according, landlord residential property ke liye maximum 2 months ka rent, aur commercial property ke liye maximum 6 months ka rent as security deposit demand kar sakta hai.

7. **Can a tenant sublet the property to someone else?**
**English:** A tenant cannot sublet the property or a part of it without the prior written consent of the landlord. Subletting without permission is a valid and strong ground for immediate eviction under tenancy laws.
**Hindi Unicode:** किरायेदार मकान मालिक की पूर्व लिखित सहमति के बिना संपत्ति को किराए पर नहीं दे सकता है। बिना अनुमति के सबलेटिंग तत्काल बेदखली के लिए एक वैध आधार है।
**Hinglish:** Tenant bina landlord ki written permission ke property ya uska hissa sublet (aage rent par) nahi kar sakta. Bina permission sublet karna eviction ka strong ground hai.

8. **Who is responsible for repairs in a rented house?**
**English:** Major structural repairs are the responsibility of the landlord. Day-to-day maintenance, changing bulbs, or minor plumbing fixes are usually the tenant's responsibility. The exact division should be clearly stated in the rent agreement.
**Hindi Unicode:** प्रमुख संरचनात्मक मरम्मत मकान मालिक की जिम्मेदारी है। दिन-प्रतिदिन का रखरखाव आमतौर पर किरायेदार की जिम्मेदारी है। सटीक विभाजन रेंट एग्रीमेंट में स्पष्ट रूप से बताया जाना चाहिए।
**Hinglish:** Major structural repairs landlord ki responsibility hoti hai. Day-to-day maintenance ya minor repairs tenant ko khud karne hote hain. Exact details agreement mein likhi honi chahiye.

9. **What is a Leave and License agreement?**
**English:** Mostly used in Maharashtra, a Leave and License agreement allows a person to occupy a property for a specific period without creating any tenancy rights or property interest. It is easier for landlords to evict under this agreement.
**Hindi Unicode:** मुख्य रूप से महाराष्ट्र में उपयोग किया जाने वाला एक लीव एंड लाइसेंस एग्रीमेंट किसी व्यक्ति को किरायेदारी के अधिकार बनाए बिना एक विशिष्ट अवधि के लिए संपत्ति पर कब्जा करने की अनुमति देता है।
**Hinglish:** Leave and License agreement mainly Maharashtra mein use hota hai. Ye tenant ko property use karne ka right deta hai bina tenancy rights create kiye, jisse landlord ke liye eviction easy ho jata hai.

10. **Can I use my residential rented property for a commercial business?**
**English:** No, residential properties cannot be used for commercial purposes without the landlord's consent and local municipal approvals. Doing so can lead to eviction and municipal penalties for illegal land use.
**Hindi Unicode:** नहीं, मकान मालिक की सहमति और स्थानीय नगरपालिका की मंजूरी के बिना आवासीय संपत्तियों का उपयोग व्यावसायिक उद्देश्यों के लिए नहीं किया जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Nahi, residential property ko commercial purpose ke liye bina landlord aur municipal approval ke use nahi kiya ja sakta. Aisa karne par eviction aur fines ho sakte hain.

11. **How can a landlord legally evict a tenant?**
**English:** A landlord must send a formal legal notice stating the valid reason (non-payment, expiry of term, subletting). If the tenant refuses to leave, the landlord must file a petition in the Rent Court. Forceful eviction is illegal.
**Hindi Unicode:** मकान मालिक को वैध कारण बताते हुए एक औपचारिक कानूनी नोटिस भेजना होगा। यदि किरायेदार जाने से इंकार कर देता है, तो मकान मालिक को रेंट कोर्ट में याचिका दायर करनी होगी। बलपूर्वक बेदखली अवैध है।
**Hinglish:** Landlord ko valid reason ke saath formal legal notice bhejna hota hai. Agar tenant nahi jaata, toh Rent Court mein petition file karni padti hai. Forceful eviction illegal hai.

12. **Does the death of a tenant end the rent agreement?**
**English:** Not necessarily. Tenancy rights are often heritable. If a tenant passes away, the right of tenancy can pass to their legal heirs (spouse, children) who were living with the tenant at the time of death, subject to state laws.
**Hindi Unicode:** जरूरी नहीं। किरायेदारी के अधिकार अक्सर विरासत में मिलते हैं। यदि किसी किरायेदार की मृत्यु हो जाती है, तो किरायेदारी का अधिकार उनके कानूनी उत्तराधिकारियों को दिया जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Zaroori nahi. Tenancy rights heritable hote hain. Tenant ki death par, unke legal heirs (spouse, children) jo waha reh rahe the, unhe tenancy rights mil sakte hain, state laws ke anusar.

13. **Is an unregistered rent agreement valid in court?**
**English:** An unregistered rent agreement for a period of 12 months or more is not admissible as primary evidence in court. For agreements of 11 months, notarization is sufficient and generally accepted as evidence of the contract.
**Hindi Unicode:** 12 महीने या उससे अधिक अवधि के लिए एक अपंजीकृत रेंट एग्रीमेंट अदालत में प्राथमिक साक्ष्य के रूप में स्वीकार्य नहीं है। 11 महीने के समझौतों के लिए, नोटरीकरण पर्याप्त है।
**Hinglish:** 12 months ya usse zyada ka unregistered rent agreement court mein primary evidence nahi maana jata. 11 months ke liye, notarization sufficient hai aur evidence ki tarah accept hota hai.

14. **What is the Pagdi system?**
**English:** The Pagdi system is an old tenancy model in cities like Mumbai where the tenant pays a lump sum 'premium' to the landlord and pays a nominal monthly rent. Tenants become part-owners and can even sub-let or sell the tenancy rights with the landlord's consent.
**Hindi Unicode:** पगड़ी प्रणाली मुंबई जैसे शहरों में एक पुराना किरायेदारी मॉडल है जहां किरायेदार मकान मालिक को एकमुश्त 'प्रीमियम' का भुगतान करता है और मामूली मासिक किराए का भुगतान करता है।
**Hinglish:** Pagdi system Mumbai jaise shehron mein ek old tenancy model hai jisme tenant ek lump sum amount deta hai aur nominal monthly rent pay karta hai. Tenant part-owner ban jata hai.

15. **Can my landlord visit the property anytime without asking?**
**English:** No, a landlord cannot drop in unannounced. They must provide reasonable prior notice (usually 24 hours) and visit only during daylight hours for inspections or repairs. The tenant has a right to privacy.
**Hindi Unicode:** नहीं, मकान मालिक बिना बताए नहीं आ सकता। उन्हें पूर्व सूचना (आमतौर पर 24 घंटे) देनी चाहिए और निरीक्षण या मरम्मत के लिए दिन के उजाले के दौरान ही जाना चाहिए। किरायेदार को निजता का अधिकार है।
**Hinglish:** Nahi, landlord bina bataye nahi aa sakta. Unhe reasonable prior notice (mostly 24 hours) dena hota hai aur sirf daytime mein inspection ke liye aa sakte hain. Tenant ke paas right to privacy hai.

16. **What if the landlord refuses to refund the security deposit?**
**English:** If a landlord wrongfully holds the deposit after the premises are vacated and handed over, the tenant can issue a legal notice. If unresolved, the tenant can approach the Rent Court to recover the amount with interest.
**Hindi Unicode:** यदि मकान मालिक परिसर खाली करने के बाद जमा राशि को गलत तरीके से अपने पास रखता है, तो किरायेदार कानूनी नोटिस जारी कर सकता है। अनसुलझे रहने पर, किरायेदार ब्याज के साथ राशि वसूलने के लिए रेंट कोर्ट जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Agar landlord vacate karne ke baad deposit hold karta hai, toh tenant legal notice bhej sakta hai. Resolve na hone par, tenant interest ke saath amount recover karne Rent Court ja sakta hai.

17. **Can a tenant deduct repair costs from the rent?**
**English:** Yes, if the landlord refuses to carry out major structural repairs despite written notice, the tenant can undertake the repairs and deduct the cost from the rent, provided the deduction does not exceed a certain percentage of the rent (varies by state).
**Hindi Unicode:** हां, यदि मकान मालिक लिखित नोटिस के बावजूद प्रमुख मरम्मत करने से इनकार करता है, तो किरायेदार मरम्मत कर सकता है और किराए से लागत में कटौती कर सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Haan, agar landlord written notice ke baad bhi major repairs nahi karta, toh tenant repairs karwa ke cost rent se deduct kar sakta hai, ek specific limit tak.

18. **Is verbal rent agreement legal in India?**
**English:** While verbal agreements hold some legal validity under the Indian Contract Act, they are exceedingly difficult to prove in court. Under the Model Tenancy Act, written and registered agreements are mandatory for any legal dispute resolution.
**Hindi Unicode:** जबकि भारतीय अनुबंध अधिनियम के तहत मौखिक समझौतों की कुछ कानूनी वैधता होती है, अदालत में उन्हें साबित करना बेहद मुश्किल होता है।
**Hinglish:** Verbal agreements ki Indian Contract Act mein kuch validity hai, par court mein prove karna bohot mushkil hota hai. MTA ke under written aur registered agreements mandatory hain.

19. **Can the landlord keep the keys of the rented house?**
**English:** A landlord may keep a spare key, but they cannot use it to enter the premises without the tenant's permission, except in extreme emergencies (like fire). Unauthorized entry is a violation of the tenant's privacy.
**Hindi Unicode:** एक मकान मालिक अतिरिक्त चाबी रख सकता है, लेकिन वे चरम आपात स्थिति (जैसे आग) को छोड़कर, किरायेदार की अनुमति के बिना परिसर में प्रवेश करने के लिए इसका उपयोग नहीं कर सकते हैं।
**Hinglish:** Landlord spare key rakh sakta hai, par tenant ki permission ke bina use enter karne ke liye use nahi kar sakta, except emergency (jaise aag lagna). Ye privacy ka violation hai.

20. **What is a lock-in period in a rent agreement?**
**English:** A lock-in period is a stipulated duration during which neither the landlord nor the tenant can terminate the agreement. If a party terminates it during this period, they are usually liable to pay a penalty, such as the rent for the remaining lock-in period.
**Hindi Unicode:** लॉक-इन अवधि एक निर्धारित अवधि है जिसके दौरान न तो मकान मालिक और न ही किरायेदार समझौते को समाप्त कर सकता है। इस अवधि के दौरान इसे समाप्त करने पर जुर्माना देना पड़ सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Lock-in period wo duration hai jiske dauran koi bhi party agreement terminate nahi kar sakti. Agar koi is period mein terminate karta hai, toh unhe penalty ya bache hue time ka rent dena padta hai.

## Common Myths vs Facts
1. **Myth:** A landlord can throw out your belongings if you don't pay rent.
   **Fact:** This is absolutely illegal. The landlord must follow the legal eviction process through the court. Taking the law into their own hands is a punishable offense.
2. **Myth:** Police can evict a tenant immediately.
   **Fact:** Police do not have the authority to evict tenants in civil rent disputes. Only a Rent Court or civil court can pass an eviction order.
3. **Myth:** A 11-month rent agreement gives the tenant ownership rights after 12 years.
   **Fact:** Tenancy never converts to ownership merely by duration (Adverse possession rules are highly complex and paying rent completely negates adverse possession).
4. **Myth:** The landlord can increase the rent every year by 10% automatically.
   **Fact:** Rent increase must be explicitly written in the agreement. There is no automatic statutory 10% increase rule.
5. **Myth:** Tenants are completely responsible for all damages in the house.
   **Fact:** Tenants are only responsible for intentional damages or minor day-to-day wear and tear. Structural damages are the landlord's responsibility.
6. **Myth:** You don't need a written agreement if renting to a relative.
   **Fact:** A written agreement is critical even with relatives to prevent bitter family disputes and establish clear terms.
7. **Myth:** Leave and License is exactly the same as a Lease Agreement.
   **Fact:** They are different. Leave and license does not create an interest in the property, making eviction much easier for landlords.
8. **Myth:** Landlords can demand 1 year's rent as a security deposit.
   **Fact:** Under the Model Tenancy Act, the cap is strictly 2 months for residential and 6 months for commercial properties.
9. **Myth:** If the agreement expires, the tenant becomes illegal instantly.
   **Fact:** If the landlord continues to accept rent, the tenant becomes a "tenant holding over," and the tenancy continues on a month-to-month basis.
10. **Myth:** NRI landlords don't have to pay taxes on rent received in India.
    **Fact:** Rental income in India is fully taxable for NRIs under the Income Tax Act, and tenants must deduct TDS at 30% before paying rent to an NRI landlord.

*Assistant:* (English) You should first send a formal legal notice demanding the refund. A broken tile is a minor repair and does not justify withholding the entire amount. If he ignores the notice, you can file a complaint with the Rent Authority.
(Hinglish) Aap sabse pehle ek legal notice bhejiye refund demand karte hue. Ek tooti tile ke liye poora deposit rokna galat hai. Agar wo notice ignore karein, toh aap Rent Authority mein complaint file kar sakte hain.

**Scenario 2: Unannounced Visits**
*Citizen:* Mera landlord jab chahe mere flat mein ghus aata hai bina puche. Kya ye legal hai?
*Assistant:* (Hindi) बिल्कुल नहीं। रेंट एग्रीमेंट के तहत, आपके पास निजता का अधिकार है। मकान मालिक को किसी भी निरीक्षण या मरम्मत के लिए आने से पहले आपको कम से कम 24 घंटे का नोटिस देना होगा। आप उन्हें स्पष्ट रूप से मना कर सकते हैं।
(Hinglish) Bilkul nahi. Tenant hone ke naate aapke paas privacy ka right hai. Landlord ko aane se pehle kam se kam 24 ghante ka notice dena hota hai. Aap unhe bina permission aane se mana kar sakte hain.

**Scenario 3: Eviction Notice**
*Citizen:* The landlord gave me only 5 days to vacate the house. Is this allowed?
*Assistant:* (English) No, an eviction notice must provide a reasonable period, usually 30 to 60 days as specified in your rent agreement. A 5-day notice is generally not legally valid.
(Hinglish) Nahi, eviction notice mein reasonable time dena zaroori hai, mostly 30 se 60 din jo agreement mein likha ho. 5 din ka notice legally valid nahi maana jata.

**Scenario 4: Cutting Electricity**
*Citizen:* Maine ek mahine ka rent nahi diya toh landlord ne meri light kaat di. Main kya karu?
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Essential services jaise light aur paani kaatna illegal hai, chahe rent default hua ho. Aap turant local police station mein complain kar sakte hain ya Rent Authority ko approach kar sakte hain immediate restoration ke liye.

**Scenario 5: Rent Increase**
*Citizen:* Can my landlord increase rent by 20% in the middle of my 11-month contract?
*Assistant:* (English) No. Unless there is a specific clause in your signed agreement permitting a mid-term increase, the rent is locked for the 11-month duration. Any increase can only happen at the time of renewal with mutual consent.

**Scenario 6: Missing Rent Agreement**
*Citizen:* Humne koi written agreement nahi banaya tha, aur ab landlord mujhe kal nikalna chahta hai.
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Written agreement na hone par bhi, aap ek "month-to-month" tenant maane jaate hain. Unhe aapko kam se kam 15-30 din ka legal notice dena hoga (Transfer of Property Act ke tahat). Wo aapko achanak kal nahi nikal sakte.

**Scenario 7: Paying in Cash**
*Citizen:* My landlord insists on cash payments and doesn't give receipts.
*Assistant:* (English) It is highly risky. You must insist on rent receipts or pay via bank transfer/UPI to maintain a digital trail. Without proof of payment, the landlord could falsely accuse you of defaulting on rent.

**Scenario 8: Subletting Issue**
*Citizen:* Mere dost ko room chahiye, kya main apna ek room usko rent par de sakta hu?
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Nahi, jab tak aapke landlord ne aapko written permission nahi di hai, aap property sublet nahi kar sakte. Agar aap aisa karte hain, toh ye legally aapke eviction ka strong ground ban jayega.

**Scenario 9: Unregistered 3-Year Lease**
*Citizen:* I have a 3-year lease but we never registered it at the Sub-Registrar office. Is it valid?
*Assistant:* (English) An unregistered lease exceeding 11 months cannot be presented as primary evidence in a court of law. It makes enforcing the terms extremely difficult. You should get it registered immediately by paying the required stamp duty and penalty.

**Scenario 10: Fixing Water Pump**
*Citizen:* Paani ki motor kharab ho gayi hai. Nayi motor lagwane ka paisa kaun dega?
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Motor replace karna major structural/equipment repair mein aata hai, isliye iska kharcha landlord ko uthana chahiye. Lekin agar motor aapki negligence se kharab hui hai, toh kharcha aapko dena pad sakta hai. Agreement check karein.

**Scenario 11: Police Verification**
*Citizen:* Is it my job as a tenant to do the police verification?
*Assistant:* (English) The primary legal responsibility lies with the landlord to submit the tenant verification form to the police. However, you must cooperate by providing your ID proofs, photographs, and permanent address details.

**Scenario 12: Locking the Gate**
*Citizen:* Landlord raat 10 baje main gate lock kar deta hai aur mujhe bahar rehna padta hai.
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Ye aapke right to peaceful possession ka violation hai. Agar aap rent de rahe hain, toh aapke paas 24x7 entry/exit ka access hona chahiye. Aap unhe legal notice bhej sakte hain agar wo baar-baar aisa karte hain.

**Scenario 13: Commercial Use of Residential Property**
*Citizen:* Can I run a small cloud kitchen from my rented apartment?
*Assistant:* (English) Only if you have explicit written permission from your landlord in the rent agreement and the local municipal society/RWA allows it. Without this, running a commercial business in a residential rented property can lead to immediate eviction.

**Scenario 14: TDS on Rent**
*Citizen:* Mera rent 60,000 rupees mahina hai. Kya mujhe ispar TDS kaatna chahiye?
*Assistant:* (Hinglish) Haan, agar aap ek individual hain aur mahine ka rent 50,000 rupees se zyada hai, toh Income Tax rules ke hisaab se aapko rent pay karne se pehle 5% TDS kaatna hoga.

**Scenario 15: Selling Property**
*Citizen:* My landlord sold the house. Does the new owner have the right to throw me out instantly?
*Assistant:* (English) No. The new owner steps into the shoes of the old landlord and is bound by the existing rent agreement until its expiry. They cannot throw you out instantly and must honor the existing terms or give proper legal notice as per the contract.
