# Warranty and Guarantee under Indian Consumer Law

## Overview 

**English:** A warranty is a legally binding assurance provided by a manufacturer or seller to the buyer, promising to repair or replace a product if it develops defects within a specified period. A guarantee is a broader commitment, often promising a complete replacement or refund if the product fails to meet the promised standards. Under Indian law, particularly the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, and the Sale of Goods Act, 1930, consumers are highly protected against arbitrary warranty denials, unfair trade practices, and defective goods. 

**Hindi (हिंदी):** वारंटी एक कानूनी रूप से बाध्यकारी आश्वासन है जो किसी निर्माता या विक्रेता द्वारा खरीदार को दिया जाता है। इसमें वादा किया जाता है कि यदि निर्दिष्ट अवधि के भीतर उत्पाद में कोई खराबी आती है, तो उसे ठीक किया जाएगा या बदला जाएगा। गारंटी एक व्यापक प्रतिबद्धता है, जिसमें अक्सर उत्पाद के वादे के अनुसार काम न करने पर पूर्ण प्रतिस्थापन (रिप्लेसमेंट) या रिफंड का वादा किया जाता है। भारतीय कानून (उपभोक्ता संरक्षण अधिनियम, 2019) के तहत, उपभोक्ताओं को वारंटी देने से मना करने और दोषपूर्ण सामान के खिलाफ मजबूत सुरक्षा प्राप्त है।

**Hinglish:** Warranty ek legal promise hota hai jo company ya seller aapko deta hai. Agar ek fix time ke andar product kharab ho jaye, toh unhe free mein repair ya replace karke dena padta hai. Guarantee thoda bada promise hota hai jisme usually naya product ya refund diya jata hai agar purana theek se kaam na kare. India ke Consumer Protection Act, 2019 ke under, agar koi company warranty claim reject karti hai bina valid reason ke, toh consumer Consumer Court mein case file karke compensation maang sakta hai.

## Quick Summary

**English:** Consumers have the fundamental right to demand free repairs, replacements, or refunds for defective products during the warranty period. Denial of a valid warranty claim is considered an 'Unfair Trade Practice' under Section 2(47) of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, and makes the manufacturer liable under 'Product Liability' (Section 84).
**Hindi (हिंदी):** उपभोक्ताओं को वारंटी अवधि के दौरान दोषपूर्ण उत्पादों के लिए मुफ्त मरम्मत, प्रतिस्थापन या रिफंड की मांग करने का मौलिक अधिकार है। वैध वारंटी दावे को अस्वीकार करना 'अनुचित व्यापार व्यवहार' माना जाता है।
**Hinglish:** Warranty period mein defective product ki free repair, replacement ya refund maangna aapka right hai. Valid warranty deny karna 'Unfair Trade Practice' hai, aur aap NCH (National Consumer Helpline) par complaint kar sakte hain.

## Definition

**English:** 
- **Warranty:** A written guarantee, issued to the purchaser of an article by its manufacturer, promising to repair or replace it if necessary within a specified period of time. Legal framework treats it as a stipulation collateral to the main purpose of the contract.
- **Guarantee:** A formal pledge or assurance (usually in writing) that certain conditions will be fulfilled, especially that a product will be of a specified quality and durability.

**Hindi (हिंदी):** 
- **वारंटी (Warranty):** यह निर्माता द्वारा दिया गया एक लिखित वादा है कि यदि कोई वस्तु एक निश्चित समय में खराब होती है, तो उसे मुफ्त में सुधारा जाएगा।
- **गारंटी (Guarantee):** यह इस बात का आश्वासन है कि वस्तु एक निश्चित गुणवत्ता की होगी। यदि वह उन मानकों को पूरा नहीं करती है, तो पूरा उत्पाद बदला जा सकता है या पैसे वापस किए जा सकते हैं।

**Hinglish:** 
- **Warranty:** Ek written promise manufacturer se ki agar specified time mein product kharab hua toh wo use repair karenge.
- **Guarantee:** Ek strong promise ki product top quality ka hoga, aur agar nahi hua toh replacement ya refund milega.

## Legal Background

### History and Purpose
Historically, the rule of "Caveat Emptor" (Let the buyer beware) dominated. However, the Sale of Goods Act, 1930, introduced implied conditions and warranties, shifting some burden to the seller. The Consumer Protection Act of 1986, and later the massively updated Consumer Protection Act, 2019, introduced strict "Product Liability" and "Unfair Trade Practices," shifting the paradigm to "Caveat Venditor" (Let the seller beware).

### Applicable Acts
1. **The Consumer Protection Act, 2019 (CPA, 2019)**
2. **The Sale of Goods Act, 1930 (SGA, 1930)**
3. **The Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016**
4. **The Legal Metrology Act, 2009** (For packaging and declarations)

### Relevant Sections
- **Section 12 of SGA, 1930:** Defines Condition and Warranty. A warranty is collateral to the main purpose; its breach gives rise to a claim for damages but not the right to reject goods.
- **Section 2(47) of CPA, 2019:** Defines "Unfair Trade Practice." Falsely representing that goods are of a particular standard, quality, or have specific warranty/guarantee when they do not, falls here.
- **Section 2(9) of CPA, 2019:** Consumer Rights, including the right to be protected against the marketing of goods which are hazardous to life and property, and the right to seek redressal.
- **Section 84 of CPA, 2019:** Defines Product Liability action. A manufacturer is liable if the product contains a manufacturing defect or deviates from the express warranty.
- **Section 85 of CPA, 2019:** Liability of product service providers if they fail to provide adequate instructions or warnings, or if their service is deficient.

## Applicability

**Who is Covered?**
- Any individual or entity that buys goods for personal use (not for commercial resale).
- The user of such goods, provided they use it with the approval of the original buyer (e.g., someone who receives a gifted phone can claim the warranty).
- Online buyers (E-commerce consumers).

**Who is Exempt / Not Covered?**
- Buyers purchasing goods for commercial purposes (e.g., a factory buying 50 ACs for its plant; though certain exceptions exist if it's for self-employment).
- Products damaged due to customer misuse, physical damage, water damage (unless IP68 certified and marketed as such), or unauthorized third-party repairs.
- Products where the warranty period has strictly expired.

## Rights of Citizens

**English:**
1. **Right to Free Repair:** If a defect occurs during the warranty period (not caused by the user), the company must repair it free of charge, including parts and labor.
2. **Right to Replacement:** If a product has a fundamental manufacturing defect that cannot be repaired after multiple attempts, the consumer has the right to demand a brand-new replacement.
3. **Right to Refund:** Under extreme circumstances where neither repair nor replacement is possible or viable, the consumer can seek a refund with interest.
4. **Right to Implied Warranty:** Even if there is no explicit written warranty, the Sale of Goods Act guarantees an "implied warranty of fitness and merchantable quality."

**Hindi (हिंदी):**
1. **मुफ्त मरम्मत का अधिकार:** यदि वारंटी अवधि में कोई खराबी आती है, तो कंपनी को बिना किसी शुल्क (पार्ट्स और लेबर) के उसे ठीक करना होगा।
2. **प्रतिस्थापन (Replacement) का अधिकार:** यदि उत्पाद में गंभीर निर्माण दोष है और बार-बार मरम्मत के बाद भी ठीक नहीं हो रहा है, तो नया उत्पाद मांगने का अधिकार है।
3. **धन वापसी (Refund) का अधिकार:** यदि कंपनी उत्पाद को ठीक करने या बदलने में असमर्थ है, तो आप पैसे वापस मांग सकते हैं।
4. **निहित वारंटी (Implied Warranty):** यदि लिखित वारंटी नहीं भी है, तो भी कानून के तहत सामान को उसके मुख्य उद्देश्य के लिए ठीक और गुणवत्तापूर्ण होना चाहिए।

**Hinglish:**
1. **Free Repair ka Right:** Warranty ke andar, company ko labor aur parts ke liye charge kiye bina product theek karke dena hoga.
2. **Replacement ka Right:** Agar product baar-baar kharab ho raha hai aur theek nahi ho pa raha, toh aap naya piece maang sakte hain.
3. **Refund maangne ka haq:** Agar repair ya replacement dono possible nahi hai, toh company ko aapke paise wapas karne padenge.
4. **Implied Warranty:** Bina written card ke bhi, kanoon kehta hai ki agar aapne paise diye hain toh product sahi chalna chahiye.

## Step-by-Step Legal Procedure

**English:**
**Step 1: Contact the Authorized Service Center:** Present the product along with the invoice and warranty card. Ensure you take a written job sheet/receipt when handing over the product.
**Step 2: Written Complaint to the Company:** If the service center denies the warranty or delays the repair unreasonably, email the company's customer grievance officer. Keep a trail of all emails.
**Step 3: Register Grievance on National Consumer Helpline (NCH):** If the company ignores your emails, register a complaint via the INGRAM portal (consumerhelpline.gov.in) or call 1915.
**Step 4: Issue a Legal Notice:** If NCH mediation fails, send a formal legal notice to the manufacturer and the seller demanding repair/replacement and compensation for mental agony.
**Step 5: File a Case in Consumer Commission:** File a formal complaint online via the e-Daakhil portal (edaakhil.nic.in). 
- District Commission: Claims up to ₹50 Lakhs.
- State Commission: Claims between ₹50 Lakhs and ₹2 Crores.
- National Commission: Claims above ₹2 Crores.

**Hindi (हिंदी):**
**चरण 1: अधिकृत सर्विस सेंटर से संपर्क करें:** अपना बिल और वारंटी कार्ड लेकर जाएं। उत्पाद जमा करते समय 'जॉब शीट' या रसीद अवश्य लें।
**चरण 2: कंपनी को लिखित शिकायत:** यदि सर्विस सेंटर वारंटी देने से मना करे या बहुत समय ले, तो कंपनी के नोडल अधिकारी को ईमेल करें।
**चरण 3: राष्ट्रीय उपभोक्ता हेल्पलाइन (NCH) पर शिकायत:** यदि कंपनी जवाब न दे, तो 1915 पर कॉल करें या INGRAM पोर्टल पर शिकायत दर्ज करें।
**चरण 4: कानूनी नोटिस भेजें:** यदि बात न बने, तो वकील के माध्यम से कंपनी को कानूनी नोटिस (Legal Notice) भिजवाएं।
**चरण 5: उपभोक्ता अदालत में केस दर्ज करें:** e-Daakhil पोर्टल के माध्यम से ऑनलाइन शिकायत दर्ज करें (जिला फोरम में 50 लाख रुपये तक के दावों के लिए)।

**Hinglish:**
**Step 1:** Sabse pehle authorized service center jao, bill aur product lekar. Job sheet zaroor lena jab product submit karo.

**Step 3:** Agar email ka fayda na ho, toh 1915 (National Consumer Helpline) par call karke complaint register karao.
**Step 4:** Phir bhi solution na mile, toh ek lawyer ke through Legal Notice send karo.
**Step 5:** Aakhiri rasta hai Consumer Court. e-Daakhil website par jakar aap khud online case file kar sakte ho, jisme lawyer ki bhi zaroorat nahi hoti.

2. Original Warranty/Guarantee Card (Preferably stamped by the retailer)
3. Job sheet or service center rejection letter (Crucial for proof of denial)
4. Email correspondence with customer support
5. Photographs or videos of the defect
6. Copy of the Legal Notice sent and its postal delivery receipt

## Government Authorities
1. **National Consumer Helpline (NCH):** First point of contact for mediation.
2. **Central Consumer Protection Authority (CCPA):** Can take suo-moto action against class-wide warranty frauds.
3. **District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (DCDRC):** Lowest tier of consumer courts.
4. **State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (SCDRC):** Appellate authority over District courts.
5. **National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC):** Apex consumer court in New Delhi.

---

## Penalties & Punishments
- **Compensation & Damages:** Consumer courts regularly order companies to refund the entire product cost along with 9% to 12% interest.
- **Mental Agony Costs:** Companies are often ordered to pay additional sums (e.g., ₹5,000 to ₹50,000+) for the mental harassment caused to the consumer.
- **Litigation Costs:** The losing company is usually ordered to reimburse the legal expenses incurred by the consumer.
- **CCPA Penalties:** Under Section 21 of the CPA 2019, the CCPA can impose fines up to ₹10 Lakhs (extendable to ₹50 Lakhs for repeat offenses) for misleading advertisements regarding warranties.

---

## Exceptions
- **Physical Damage:** Drops, dents, screen cracks are not covered under standard warranty (requires accidental damage insurance).
- **Liquid Damage:** Unless the product is explicitly sold as waterproof (and even then, strict IP rating limits apply).
- **Consumables:** Batteries, printer ink, filters, etc., often have a shorter warranty (e.g., 6 months) compared to the main device (1 year).
- **Unauthorized Repair:** Opening the device outside the official service network automatically voids the warranty.

---

## Important Timelines
- **Defect Reporting:** Must ideally be done within the warranty period (e.g., within 365 days of purchase).
- **Limitation Period for Consumer Court:** A case must be filed within **2 years** from the date the cause of action arose (i.e., the date the service center formally denied the warranty).
- **Appeal Timeline:** Appeals against a District Commission order must be filed in the State Commission within **45 days**.

---

## Common Legal Mistakes
- **Losing the Original Invoice:** A bank statement is not enough; the actual tax invoice with the product serial number is legally required.
- **Relying on Verbal Promises:** Shopkeepers often say "hum baithe hain, kuch hoga toh le aana" (we are here, bring it if anything goes wrong). This holds no legal weight. Get it in writing.
- **Delaying Action:** Waiting for months after the warranty expires to report a defect that started *during* the warranty.
- **Not getting a Job Sheet:** Giving the product to the service center without a written receipt. If they lose your product, you have no proof.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**1. Can a company deny warranty if I bought the product online?**
**English:** No, a company cannot deny a warranty merely because the product was purchased from an e-commerce platform like Amazon or Flipkart, provided the seller is authorized.
**Hindi:** नहीं, यदि आपने किसी अधिकृत ई-कॉमर्स साइट से उत्पाद खरीदा है, तो कंपनी वारंटी देने से मना नहीं कर सकती।
**Hinglish:** Bilkul nahi. Agar aapne online valid platform se kharida hai aur original bill hai, toh company ko warranty deni padegi.

**2. What if I lose my warranty card but have the bill?**
**English:** The original tax invoice (bill) is the primary document of purchase. Even without a physical warranty card, the bill is sufficient to claim a warranty.
**Hindi:** मूल जीएसटी बिल ही सबसे महत्वपूर्ण है। वारंटी कार्ड खो जाने पर भी बिल के आधार पर वारंटी का दावा किया जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Bill sabse main document hota hai. Agar warranty card gum ho gaya hai toh koi baat nahi, original bill se kaam ho jayega.

**3. Does warranty cover physical damage like a broken screen?**
**English:** Standard warranties strictly cover manufacturing defects only. Accidental physical damage or liquid ingress is not covered unless you purchased special accidental damage protection.
**Hindi:** सामान्य वारंटी में केवल निर्माण संबंधी खराबियां शामिल होती हैं। टूटना-फूटना या पानी जाना इसमें कवर नहीं होता है।
**Hinglish:** Normal warranty mein physical damage (jaise screen tootna) cover nahi hota. Uske liye aapko alag se accidental insurance lena padta hai.

**4. The service center is taking 3 months to repair my TV. Is this legal?**
**English:** No. Unreasonable delays in repairing a product under warranty amount to "deficiency in service." You can demand a replacement or approach the consumer court.
**Hindi:** नहीं, इतनी देरी को 'सेवा में कमी' माना जाता है। आप नया टीवी या रिफंड मांगने के लिए उपभोक्ता अदालत जा सकते हैं।
**Hinglish:** 3 mahine bahut zyada hain. Kanoon ke hisaab se ye 'deficiency in service' hai aur aap court mein case kar sakte hain naye TV ke liye.

**5. Are parts and labor both free during the warranty period?**
**English:** Yes. A comprehensive warranty mandates that the company cannot charge you for the spare parts or the service/labor charges required to fix the defect.
**Hindi:** हाँ। वारंटी अवधि के दौरान कंपनी आपसे पार्ट्स या लेबर के लिए कोई शुल्क नहीं मांग सकती।
**Hinglish:** Haan, warranty period mein company na hi parts ka paisa le sakti hai aur na hi service charge. Sab kuch free hona chahiye.

**6. I bought a second-hand car. Does the original warranty still apply?**
**English:** Usually, warranties are tied to the product and the specific timeframe, not the owner. However, you must check the company's specific terms; some require formal transfer of ownership in their records.
**Hindi:** वारंटी आमतौर पर वाहन से जुड़ी होती है, मालिक से नहीं। लेकिन आपको कंपनी के रिकॉर्ड में नाम बदलवाना पड़ सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Warranty gadi ki hoti hai, owner ki nahi. Lekin RC transfer ke baad company ke system mein bhi naam update karana zaroori hota hai.

**7. Do display units or demo models have a warranty?**
**English:** Unless explicitly stated otherwise in writing on the invoice ("sold as is"), products sold at a discount as display units carry the standard warranty from the date of your purchase.
**Hindi:** जब तक बिल पर स्पष्ट रूप से न लिखा हो, डिस्प्ले वाले उत्पादों पर भी खरीद की तारीख से सामान्य वारंटी लागू होती है।
**Hinglish:** Agar bill par "No Warranty" nahi likha hai, toh display models par bhi normal fresh product wali warranty milti hai.

**8. Can they replace a defective part with a refurbished one?**
**English:** During warranty repairs, authorized service centers are required to use new, original equipment manufacturer (OEM) spare parts, not refurbished ones, unless the terms explicitly state otherwise.
**Hindi:** वारंटी के दौरान कंपनी को नए और मूल (OEM) पार्ट्स का ही इस्तेमाल करना होता है, पुरानी या रिफर्बिश्ड चीजों का नहीं।
**Hinglish:** Company ko hamesha naye aur original parts lagane hote hain warranty repairs mein, refurbished (purane repair kiye hue) nahi.

**9. What is an extended warranty? Is it legally binding?**
**English:** An extended warranty is an additional service contract purchased separately. It is a legally binding contract, but carefully read whether it is provided by the manufacturer or a third-party insurance firm.
**Hindi:** एक्सटेंडेड वारंटी एक अतिरिक्त अनुबंध है जिसके लिए आप पैसे देते हैं। यह कानूनी रूप से बाध्यकारी है, लेकिन इसकी शर्तें अलग हो सकती हैं।
**Hinglish:** Extended warranty ek extra protection plan hota hai jo aap kharidte ho. Ye legally valid hota hai, par check karein ki ye company khud de rahi hai ya koi third-party.

**10. What if the company goes bankrupt or shuts down?**
**English:** If a company liquidates, claiming a warranty becomes extremely difficult. You may have to file a claim as an unsecured creditor in the corporate insolvency resolution process, which rarely yields results for end consumers.
**Hindi:** अगर कंपनी दिवालिया हो जाती है, तो वारंटी का दावा करना लगभग असंभव हो जाता है।
**Hinglish:** Agar company hi band ho jaye, toh warranty claim karna bahut mushkil hai. Aise cases mein consumer ka nuksan hi hota hai.

**11. The showroom says I must contact the manufacturer for the warranty. Is this true?**
**English:** Yes and no. The primary liability of a warranty lies with the manufacturer. However, under the CPA 2019, if the manufacturer is inaccessible, the seller can also be held liable under certain conditions.
**Hindi:** वारंटी की मुख्य जिम्मेदारी निर्माता (कंपनी) की होती है। लेकिन अगर कंपनी जवाब नहीं देती है, तो विक्रेता (दुकानदार) को भी जिम्मेदार ठहराया जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Mostly warranty company ki hoti hai, service center unhi ka hota hai. Par agar company bhag jaye, toh kanoon kehta hai ki dukaandar bhi liability se bach nahi sakta.

**12. Is the warranty valid if the product was gifted to me?**
**English:** Yes. The Consumer Protection Act defines a consumer as anyone who buys the goods OR uses them with the approval of the buyer. The person possessing the gifted item with the original bill can claim the warranty.
**Hindi:** हाँ। यदि आपके पास मूल बिल है, तो उपहार में मिले सामान पर भी वारंटी का दावा किया जा सकता है।
**Hinglish:** Haan, agar aapke paas original bill hai, toh gift kiye hue product ki warranty bhi bilkul valid hoti hai.

**13. Does rooting a phone or jailbreaking void the warranty?**
**English:** Yes. Almost all smartphone manufacturers explicitly state in their terms that unauthorized software modifications, rooting, or unlocking the bootloader will void the hardware warranty.
**Hindi:** हाँ, फोन को रूट करना या अनाधिकृत सॉफ्टवेयर डालना वारंटी को समाप्त कर देता है।
**Hinglish:** Haan, agar aap apne phone ko root karte hain ya custom ROM dalte hain, toh company turant warranty void kar deti hai.

**14. What is a global warranty?**
**English:** A global warranty means the manufacturer promises to honor the warranty in any country where they have official operations, regardless of where the product was originally purchased.
**Hindi:** ग्लोबल वारंटी का मतलब है कि आप उत्पाद दुनिया के किसी भी देश में खरीदें, कंपनी उसे उन सभी देशों में ठीक करेगी जहां उसके सर्विस सेंटर हैं।
**Hinglish:** Global warranty mein aap laptop America se kharid kar India mein free mein repair kara sakte ho, basharte India mein unka service center ho.

**15. Can the service center charge a 'checking fee' during warranty?**
**English:** No. If the product is under a valid warranty, the authorized service center cannot charge a diagnosis, inspection, or checking fee.
**Hindi:** नहीं। वारंटी के दौरान अधिकृत सर्विस सेंटर जांच या निरीक्षण के लिए कोई शुल्क नहीं ले सकता।
**Hinglish:** Bilkul nahi. Warranty period mein checking ya inspection ke naam par koi charge nahi liya ja sakta.

**16. What if the required spare part is out of stock?**
**English:** If a spare part is unavailable for an extended period, rendering the product useless, you have the right to demand a replacement of the entire product or a prorated refund.
**Hindi:** यदि कोई पुर्जा लंबे समय तक उपलब्ध नहीं है, तो आप पूरे उत्पाद को बदलने या पैसे वापस करने की मांग कर सकते हैं।
**Hinglish:** Agar part mil hi nahi raha aur time waste ho raha hai, toh company ko aapko naya product ya refund dena chahiye.

**17. Do clothes or shoes have a warranty?**
**English:** Yes, though usually shorter (e.g., 30 to 90 days). If a shoe sole comes apart within a week of normal usage, it is a manufacturing defect, and the brand is liable to replace it.
**Hindi:** हाँ, कपड़े और जूतों पर भी वारंटी होती है, भले ही वह कम समय के लिए हो। यदि जूता एक हफ्ते में फट जाए, तो कंपनी को उसे बदलना होगा।
**Hinglish:** Haan, branded shoes aur clothes ki bhi guarantee/warranty hoti hai. Agar jaldi kharab ho jaye (manufacturing defect), toh replace karana aapka haq hai.

**18. What does "Pro-rata Warranty" on car batteries mean?**
**English:** A pro-rata warranty means if the battery fails after the full-replacement period but within the extended period, you will only get a percentage discount on the MRP of a new battery, not a free replacement.
**Hindi:** प्रो-राटा वारंटी का मतलब है कि एक निश्चित समय के बाद आपको नई बैटरी पूरी तरह मुफ्त नहीं मिलेगी, बल्कि उसकी कीमत पर कुछ छूट मिलेगी।
**Hinglish:** Pro-rata ka matlab hai aadhi-adhuri warranty. Shuru ke 1-2 saal free replacement hota hai, uske baad kharab hone par naye battery par sirf kuch discount milta hai.

**19. My warranty expired yesterday. Today the product broke. Will it be covered?**
**English:** Legally, the warranty ends precisely on the expiry date. However, as a goodwill gesture, some brands may entertain a claim if it is just a few days out of warranty, though they are not legally obligated.
**Hindi:** कानूनी रूप से वारंटी समाप्त होने के बाद कंपनी मुफ्त मरम्मत के लिए बाध्य नहीं है।
**Hinglish:** Kanoon ke hisaab se toh warranty khatam, toh free service nahi milegi. Par request karne par kuch companies goodwill mein theek kar deti hain (par ye unki marzi hai).

**20. Can I claim mental harassment for a denied warranty?**
**English:** Yes. If a valid warranty claim was wrongfully denied, forcing you to run around, you can claim compensation for mental agony along with the cost of the product in a Consumer Court.
**Hindi:** हाँ। यदि आपके वैध वारंटी दावे को खारिज किया गया जिससे आपको परेशानी हुई, तो आप उपभोक्ता अदालत में मानसिक उत्पीड़न के लिए मुआवजे की मांग कर सकते हैं।
**Hinglish:** Haan bilkul. Agar unhone bina baat warranty deny ki aur aapko pareshan kiya, toh Consumer court product cost ke sath-sath mental harassment ka paisa bhi dilwati hai.

## Common Myths vs Facts

**Myth 1:** You need the original box/packaging to claim a warranty.
**Fact:** The original box is NOT required. Only the product with its intact serial number/IMEI and the original tax invoice are required.

**Myth 2:** If you lose the physical warranty card, the warranty is void.
**Fact:** The original GST invoice is the absolute proof of purchase. The warranty card is secondary.

**Myth 3:** Companies can dictate that disputes can only be filed in their home city's courts.
**Fact:** Under the CPA 2019, consumers can file a case in the District Commission where the *consumer* resides or works, regardless of what the warranty card terms say.

**Myth 4:** "Goods once sold will not be taken back" printed on the bill protects the seller.
**Fact:** Such a clause is legally invalid and constitutes an unfair trade practice if the goods are found to have a manufacturing defect.

**Myth 5:** Service centers can hold your product indefinitely until a part arrives.
**Fact:** Unreasonable delay (usually beyond 30-45 days) is a "deficiency in service" for which a consumer can demand a full replacement or refund.

**Myth 6:** Warranty covers all types of damages, including dropping a phone.
**Fact:** Standard warranty only covers internal manufacturing or software defects, never physical or liquid damage caused by the user.

**Myth 7:** Removing the brand sticker/label doesn't matter.
**Fact:** Removing or tampering with the serial number sticker or the tamper-evident warranty seal will instantly void the warranty.

**Myth 8:** You have to hire a lawyer to fight a warranty case.
**Fact:** In Consumer Courts, consumers are encouraged to argue their own cases. The e-Daakhil portal allows online filing without any legal representation.

**Myth 9:** Extended warranties offered by third-party websites are identical to brand warranties.
**Fact:** Third-party warranties are insurance contracts. Their terms, deductibles, and repair qualities often differ significantly from original manufacturer warranties.

**Myth 10:** The seller (retailer) has zero responsibility once the product leaves the shop.
**Fact:** While the manufacturer provides the warranty, the seller is the first point of contact and can be held liable for selling defective goods under the Sale of Goods Act.

## Conversation Examples

**Scenario 1: Missing Warranty Card**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mera TV kharab ho gaya hai, par mujhe warranty card nahi mil raha. Kya free repair milega?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Agar aapke paas original shop ka bill hai jisme date aur TV ka serial number likha hai, toh warranty card ki zaroorat nahi hai. Aap bill dikha kar free repair le sakte hain.
**Assistant (English):** If you possess the original retail invoice bearing the date of purchase and the product's serial number, a physical warranty card is not mandatory for claiming free service.

**Scenario 2: Water Damage Denial**
**Citizen (Hindi):** सर्विस सेंटर वाले कह रहे हैं कि मेरे फोन में पानी गया है, इसलिए वारंटी नहीं मिलेगी। पर मैंने तो फोन पानी में नहीं गिराया।
**Assistant (Hindi):** सर्विस सेंटर को फोन के अंदर 'लिक्विड डैमेज इंडिकेटर' (LDI) लाल हुआ दिखाना होगा। यदि वे सबूत नहीं दे पाते और सिर्फ बहाने बना रहे हैं, तो आप नेशनल कंज्यूमर हेल्पलाइन (1915) पर शिकायत कर सकते हैं।
**Assistant (English):** The service center must demonstrate that the internal Liquid Damage Indicator (LDI) has turned pink/red. If they cannot prove water ingress, their denial is invalid, and you should escalate to the National Consumer Helpline.

**Scenario 3: Unreasonable Repair Delay**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Laptop 2 mahine se service center mein hai. Har baar bolte hain motherboard aane wala hai. Main kya karun?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Ye 'deficiency in service' hai. Company ki nodal officer ko email likhein ki agar 7 din mein laptop theek nahi hua, toh aap Consumer Court mein replacement aur mental harassment ka case file karenge.
**Assistant (English):** This constitutes a deficiency in service. Email the company's grievance officer stating that if the repair is not completed within 7 days, you will approach the Consumer Commission seeking a replacement and compensation.

**Scenario 4: Refund for Unrepairable Product**
**Citizen (English):** They have changed the display of my smartwatch three times, and it still goes blank. I don't want a repair anymore. I want my money back.
**Assistant (English):** You have a strong case under the Consumer Protection Act for a refund. Repeated failures of a critical component prove a fundamental manufacturing defect. Send a formal legal notice demanding a full refund.
**Assistant (Hindi):** उपभोक्ता संरक्षण अधिनियम के तहत, बार-बार की खराबी यह साबित करती है कि यह एक 'मैन्युफैक्चरिंग डिफेक्ट' है। आप कानूनी नोटिस भेजकर अपने पूरे पैसे वापस मांग सकते हैं।

**Scenario 5: Third-party repair voided warranty**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mere AC ka fan local mechanic se theek karwaya tha. Ab compressor kharab hai aur company warranty dene se mana kar rahi hai.
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Company sahi hai. Agar aap kisi unauthorized person se product khulwate hain, toh terms and conditions ke hisaab se warranty turant void (khatam) ho jati hai. Ab aapko paise dene padenge.

**Scenario 6: Display Piece Purchase**
**Citizen (Hindi):** मैंने सेल में डिस्प्ले वाला फ्रिज खरीदा था। अब वह ठंडा नहीं कर रहा, पर कंपनी कह रही है कि डिस्प्ले पीस पर वारंटी नहीं है।
**Assistant (Hindi):** जब तक आपके बिल पर साफ अक्षरों में "No Warranty" नहीं लिखा है, तब तक डिस्प्ले पीस पर भी आपके खरीदने के दिन से पूरी वारंटी लागू होती है। आप कंपनी के खिलाफ NCH पर शिकायत करें।

**Scenario 7: E-commerce Warranty Dispute**
**Citizen (English):** I bought headphones online, but the manufacturer is denying warranty saying the seller was unauthorized.
**Assistant (English):** E-commerce platforms must display the seller's details. If the platform allowed an unauthorized seller without a clear warning, the platform (Amazon/Flipkart) and the seller can be held jointly liable. File a grievance against the platform.

**Scenario 8: Expired Warranty Goodwill**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Mera warranty kal khatam hua aur aaj phone on nahi ho raha. Kya main kuch kar sakta hu?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Legally, company bound nahi hai free service dene ke liye. Par aap unke customer care executive ya manager se politely request kar sakte hain "goodwill exception" ke liye. Kabhi kabhi wo maan jate hain.

**Scenario 9: Forced to Travel to Another City**
**Citizen (Hindi):** मेरे शहर में कंपनी का सर्विस सेंटर नहीं है। वे कह रहे हैं कि मुझे 200 किलोमीटर दूर खुद लेकर आना होगा।
**Assistant (Hindi):** यदि कंपनी ने उत्पाद आपके शहर में बेचा है, तो उसे ठीक करने की जिम्मेदारी (या तो पिकअप या कूरियर खर्च) भी उनकी ही है। आप उपभोक्ता फोरम में सेवा में कमी का केस कर सकते हैं।

**Scenario 10: "Goods once sold not taken back"**
**Citizen (English):** The shopkeeper sold me a defective juicer. The bill says "Goods once sold will not be taken back." He is refusing a replacement.
**Assistant (English):** That clause has been struck down by Indian courts and holds absolutely no legal validity against defective goods. The retailer is obligated to replace a defective product sold by them.

**Scenario 11: Replacing whole vs replacing part**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Bike ka engine kharab hai pehle mahine mein. Main chahta hu puri bike replace ho, par wo sirf engine change kar rahe hain.
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Warranty terms ke mutabiq, company ki primary responsibility defective part (engine) ko naye part se replace karne ki hoti hai. Jab tak wo repair impossible na ho, poori bike replace karna kanoonan zaroori nahi hai.

**Scenario 12: No Bill, Only Online Transaction Proof**
**Citizen (Hindi):** मेरे पास फ्रिज का बिल नहीं है, पर क्रेडिट कार्ड का स्टेटमेंट है जिससे मैंने उसे खरीदा था। क्या वारंटी मिलेगी?
**Assistant (Hindi):** केवल कार्ड स्टेटमेंट पर्याप्त नहीं है क्योंकि उसमें फ्रिज का सीरियल नंबर नहीं होता। आपको उस दुकान से संपर्क करके डुप्लिकेट इनवॉइस (बिल) निकलवाना होगा, तभी वारंटी मिलेगी।

**Scenario 13: Free Labor, but Charging for Gas in AC**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** AC warranty mein hai. Cooling coil change ki hai free mein, par gas bharne ke ₹2000 maang rahe hain.
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Agar defect company ki wajah se tha aur gas leak hui, toh gas refilling ka charge bhi unhi ko bear karna hoga. Warranty ka matlab zero cost repair hota hai.

**Scenario 14: Accidental Damage Cover vs Warranty**
**Citizen (English):** My laptop fell and the screen shattered. I have a 1-year warranty. Will it be free?
**Assistant (English):** No. Standard warranties only cover internal hardware/software failures. Physical breakage is only covered if you explicitly purchased an 'Accidental Damage Protection' (ADP) plan.

**Scenario 15: Compensation for Delay**
**Citizen (Hinglish):** Company ne 4 mahine baad phone replace karke diya. Mera bahut loss hua. Kya mujhe aur compensation mil sakta hai?
**Assistant (Hinglish):** Haan. Aap Consumer Court mein case file kar sakte hain 'deficiency in service' aur 'mental harassment' ke liye. Court company ko fine lagakar wo paisa aapko dilwa sakti hai.

## Government Services
**English:**
1. **National Consumer Helpline (NCH):** Dial 1915 or visit `consumerhelpline.gov.in`. It provides pre-litigation mediation and forces companies to resolve issues without court intervention.
2. **e-Daakhil:** `edaakhil.nic.in` is the official portal for filing online consumer complaints in the District/State/National Commissions from the comfort of your home.
3. **CCPA (Central Consumer Protection Authority):** Investigates massive consumer rights violations and false warranty claims at a national level.

**Hinglish:**
1. **NCH (1915):** Sabse pehle yahan call karke complaint likhao. 80% cases yahin solve ho jate hain kyu ki NCH seedha company ke head office se baat karta hai.
2. **e-Daakhil:** Agar NCH se problem solve na ho, toh e-Daakhil website par jakar aap court case file kar sakte hain.
