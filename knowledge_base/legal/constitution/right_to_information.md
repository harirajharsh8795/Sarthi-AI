# Right to Information (RTI) Act 2005 - Complete Guide / सूचना का अधिकार नियम

## Overview / अवलोकन / Quick Summary
- **English**: The Right to Information (RTI) Act, 2005 empowers Indian citizens to request information from public authorities (government bodies, departments, public sector units). Public authorities are required to reply within 30 days.
- **Hindi**: सूचना का अधिकार (RTI) अधिनियम, 2005 भारतीय नागरिकों को सरकारी विभागों और सार्वजनिक प्राधिकरणों से जानकारी माँगने का कानूनी अधिकार देता है। सरकारी अधिकारी को 30 दिनों के भीतर जवाब देना अनिवार्य है।
- **Hinglish**: Right to Information (RTI) Act 2005 Indian citizens ko govt offices aur public authorities se information mangne ka right deta hai. Public Information Officer (PIO) ko 30 din me reply dena hota hai.

---

## Key Provisions & Legal Rights / मुख्य प्रावधान एवं अधिकार

### 1. Who can file an RTI? / RTI कौन फाइल कर सकता है?
- **English**: Any citizen of India can file an RTI application.
- **Hindi**: भारत का कोई भी नागरिक RTI आवेदन दायर कर सकता है।
- **Hinglish**: India ka koi bhi citizen RTI application file kar sakta hai.

### 2. Time Limit for Response / जवाब की समय सीमा
- **Standard Request**: 30 days from the date of receipt.
- **Life & Liberty Request**: 48 hours if the information concerns the life or liberty of a person.
- **Third Party Information**: 40 days.

## Step-by-Step Procedure to File an RTI / RTI कैसे फाइल करें

### Step 1: Identify the Department / विभाग की पहचान करें
- **English**: Determine which central or state government ministry or department holds the required information.
- **Hindi**: उस सरकारी विभाग या मंत्रालय की पहचान करें जिसके पास जानकारी है।
- **Hinglish**: Us government department ya ministry ko find karein jiske paas aapki required file ya information hai.

### Step 2: Draft the Application / आवेदन लिखें
- **English**: Write your application on plain paper or online via `rtionline.gov.in`. Specify clear and precise questions.
- **Hindi**: plain paper पर या online rtionline.gov.in पर आवेदन लिखें। अपने प्रश्न स्पष्ट और सटीक पूछें।
- **Hinglish**: Plain paper par application likhein ya online `rtionline.gov.in` portal par submit karein. Clear aur specific questions pochein.

### Step 3: Pay the Application Fee / शुल्क का भुगतान करें
- **Fee**: ₹10 for Central Government offices. BPL (Below Poverty Line) cardholders are exempt from paying fees.
- **Payment Mode**: Postal Order, Demand Draft, Cash, or Net Banking/UPI for online RTI.

### Step 4: Submit to PIO / PIO को जमा करें
- **English**: Address the application to the Public Information Officer (PIO) / Assistant Public Information Officer (APIO) of the respective department.
- **Hindi**: आवेदन को संबंधित विभाग के जन सूचना अधिकारी (PIO) के पते पर भेजें।
- **Hinglish**: Application ko department ke Public Information Officer (PIO) ya Speed Post dwara submit karein.

## Appeals & Remedies / अपील और उपाय

### First Appeal / प्रथम अपील
- **Condition**: If no response is received within 30 days, or if the information provided is incomplete/incorrect.
- **Timeframe**: File within 30 days from the expiry of the 30-day response period.
- **Authority**: First Appellate Authority (FAA) within the same department.

### Second Appeal / द्वितीय अपील
- **Condition**: If the First Appellate Authority fails to provide relief.
- **Timeframe**: File within 90 days of the FAA decision.
- **Authority**: Central Information Commission (CIC) or State Information Commission (SIC).

---

## Important Rules & Tips / महत्वपूर्ण नियम
1. RTI cannot be used to ask for opinions or advice; ask for recorded facts, documents, orders, files, and circulars.
2. Exempted Information (Section 8): National security, sovereignty, trade secrets, and personal privacy without public interest.
